1. We need to ______ a plan for the school trip. Any ideas?

(A) think over (B) think up (C) talk over

2. Please ______ the jacket before you buy it. The size might be wrong.

(A) try out (B) try on (C) wear out

3. ______ for the wet floor — someone just mopped it.

(A) Warm up (B) Wake up (C) Watch out

4. He ______ me ______ going to the concert, even though I had homework.

(A) talked ... into (B) talked ... over (C) split ... into

5. Can we ______ the project details before the meeting tomorrow?

(A) talk over (B) talk into (C) think up

6. I ______ all night to finish my report. Now I can't stop yawning.

(A) stayed out (B) stayed up (C) woke up

7. Mom said, "______ before dinner — your hands are dirty!"

(A) Warm up (B) Tidy up (C) Wash up

8. The class will ______ three groups for the science experiment.

(A) split up (B) split into (C) switch off

9. Please ______ the light. It's too dark in here.

(A) switch off (B) switch on (C) throw away

10. Let me ______ it ______ for a few days before I give you an answer.

(A) think ... up (B) think ... over (C) talk ... into

11. Don't ______ these old books — we can donate them.

(A) use up (B) throw away (C) tidy up

12. If you have a question, please ______ so the teacher can hear you.

(A) speak for (B) slow down (C) speak up

13. ______ me ______ at six tomorrow. I have an early bus.

(A) Wake ... up (B) Warm ... up (C) Wash ... up

14. We ______ all the paper. Can someone bring more from the office?

(A) wore out (B) threw away (C) used up

15. ______ your room before your grandma comes, please.

(A) Throw away (B) Tidy up (C) Try on

16. ______, you're driving too fast! The speed limit is 50.

(A) Slow down (B) Stay out (C) Switch off

17. No matter what happens, I'll ______ you. That's what friends do.

(A) stick to (B) stick with (C) split up

18. I ran so much that my new shoes are already ______.

(A) used up (B) thrown away (C) worn out

19. Please ______ your phones during class. No distractions.

(A) switch on (B) switch off (C) throw away

20. Let's do some stretches and ______ before the race starts.

(A) warm up (B) wake up (C) wash up
