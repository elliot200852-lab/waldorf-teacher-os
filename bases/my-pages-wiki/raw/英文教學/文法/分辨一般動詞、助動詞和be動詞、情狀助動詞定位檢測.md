分辨一般動詞、助動詞和be動詞
第一部分：填空題
請在每個空格填入正確的動詞（有些是 be 動詞，有些是助動詞或一般動詞）。
1. She ______________ a student. 
2. My brother ______________ pizza very much. (like)
3. ______________ you ______________ basketball yesterday? (play)
4. They ______________ not  at home last night.
5. He ______________ English every day. (study)
6. ______________ you ______________ your homework this morning? (do)


7. I ______________  not sure about the answer.
第二部分：填充題
請在空格內填入適當的助動詞（do, does, did）或 be動詞（am, is, are, was, were），使句子完整正確。一個限填一字。
1. ______________ you like apples?
2. He ______________  not go to school yesterday.
3. She ______________ reading a book now.
4. ______________  you at the library this afternoon?
5. We ______________  watching TV when it started to rain.

助動詞分為有意義/無意義，但在文法上都有一樣的功能
情態助動詞 （must, will, might, can, may, could)
1. She ____________  speak English fluently. （能力）
2. You ______________  not open the door. （禁止）
3.______________  I help you with that? （請求）
4. He______________  play the piano when he was young. （過去能力）
5. We ______________  finish this project tomorrow. （未來計畫）
6.______________  you please be quiet? （請求）
7. She ______________ _ be at home now. （可能性）
8. He______________  run very fast. （能力）
9. I ______________  see you later. （未來可能性）
10. They ____________  not have enough money. （可能性）
