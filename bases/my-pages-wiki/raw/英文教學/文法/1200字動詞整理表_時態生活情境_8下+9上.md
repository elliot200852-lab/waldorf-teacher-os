200 個最常用動詞片語（依核心動詞分組）
COME
1. come across = 偶然發現／遇見
A: I came across our old class photo today.
B: No way—does it bring back memories?
2. come along = 一起來／進展
A: We’re going to the night market. Want to come along?
B: Sure, give me five minutes.
3. come back = 回來
A: When will you come back from Taipei?
B: Probably Sunday evening.
4. come by = 順道拜訪／得到
A: Can I come by your office after lunch?
B: Yes, I’ll be there till three.
5. come down with = 染上（病）
A: You look tired. Are you okay?
B: I think I’m coming down with a cold.
GET
6. get along (with) = 相處融洽
A: Do you get along with your new roommates?
B: Yeah, we cook together on weekends.
7. get around = 到處走動／避開
A: How do you get around the city?
B: Mostly by bike and MRT.
8. get away = 逃走／度假
A: Any plans for the long weekend?
B: We’re getting away to Hualien.
9. get back = 回來／拿回
A: When did you get back from the trip?
B: Late last night.
10. get by = 勉強過活／湊合
A: Is your part-time job enough?
B: It’s tight, but I can get by.
11. get in = 到達／進入
A: What time did your flight get in?
B: Around midnight.
12. get into = 對…感興趣／進入
A: How did you get into photography?
B: My dad gave me his old camera.
TAKE
13. take after = 長得像；性格像
A: Your son really takes after you.
B: Same smile, right?
14. take apart = 拆開
A: Why is the fan on the floor?
B: I took it apart to clean the dust.
15. take back = 收回（話）／退回
A: Do you still want that comment on record?
B: No, I take it back.
16. take down = 寫下；拆掉
A: Could you take down the meeting notes?
B: Sure, I’ll share them later.

17. take in = 吸收／理解；改小（衣服）

B: Let’s review it slowly.
18. take off = 起飛；脫掉；迅速成功

B: At 7:30 p.m.
19. take on = 承擔；雇用；挑戰

B: Yes, if we set clear deadlines.
