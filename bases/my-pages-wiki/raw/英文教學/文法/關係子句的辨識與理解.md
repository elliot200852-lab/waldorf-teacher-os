請用螢光筆標示出關係子句並試著理解情境：

The dog I have is the yellow one.

The bear the man killed was really large.

The cat she wants to have doesn’t like fish.

The cows the man keep in the farm don’t have milk.

The tigers we see in the zoo should run free in the forest. 

I just saw a pig that she played with.

I killed a wolf that the old man lived with together.

I am riding a horse that she owns.

I am worried about the lions which people keep in the zoo. 

請先確認句子是否有錯誤再寫下正確的句子，並試著理解情境：

The elephants run in the forest are very big.
