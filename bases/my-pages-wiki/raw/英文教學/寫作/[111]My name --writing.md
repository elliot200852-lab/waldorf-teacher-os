My Name

In Chinese my name 信宏 means faith and being great. For me it means too many brothers. It means my family putting too much on me. It means a chain around my neck. I think doing myself is very important. It is also important to live on my own. I don’t think it is a good thing to take money or too much support from my parents. I think it is very beautiful in the human world for everyone to live their own life without asking too much help.
