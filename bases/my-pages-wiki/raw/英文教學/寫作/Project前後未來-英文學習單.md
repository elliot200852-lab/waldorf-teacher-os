🔄 Start-up: Think About Your Journey 
    1、I used to be afraid of… / I didn’t know how to…
    2、But now I understand… / I feel better about…
    3、And I will try to… / I will be more…
    4、And now I have really learned a big lesson.
              “ The more I speak, the more confident I feel.”
               “The more I listen, the more I understand.”
【Part 1: Practice Dialogue in Pairs】
與同學兩人一組，請根據上面完成的內容，改成對話方式練習，可以加入自己的角色或情境：

Dialogue Example
A: Hey! How did you feel about your project?
B: Well, I used to feel really nervous about speaking in front of others.
A: Really? But you looked confident!
B: Thanks! Now I can speak more clearly, and I don’t shake anymore.
A: That’s great! What will you do next time?
B: I will prepare earlier and help my friends more.
A: Me too. I think the older we get, the more we learn.
B: Yes!   I have learned a big lesson, too. The more we practice, the more we grow.

【 🎓 Part 2: Fill in the blanks — Your Story】
請你試著根據以下句型，完成你的故事。你可以自由選擇你的經驗，例如：做報告、與同學合作、口頭報告的準備、挑戰與成長等。

1. Past – I used to…
What were you like before the project?

I used to ____________________________.
(□feel nervous / □not know how to start / □wait for others / □dislike teamwork…)

2. Present – But now I…
How have you changed or grown?

But now I ____________________________.
(□feel more confident /□can speak clearly/ □ enjoy working together…)

3. Future – And I will…
What will you do next time?

And I will ____________________________.
(□try harder / □help others / □be more organized（更有組織力的）…)

4. Reflection – The older I get, the more I learn…
Try to write your own sentence using: The more…, the more…

The older I get, the more I learn that ____________________________.
(Hint: the more I try, the more I grow / the more I listen, the better I understand…

【 🎓 Part 3:寫作靈感參考表】--Project Reflection Examples
1. Afraid to speak in public—（＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿）
	•I used to shake and forget my words when I spoke in front of the class.
	•But now I can say my main ideas clearly.
	•And I will practice more and believe in myself next time.
2. Didn’t know how to organize—（＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿）
	•I used to just copy and paste from the internet.
	•But now I can organize my notes and choose the key points.
	•And I will make a plan before I search next time.
3. Poor time management—（＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿）
	•I used to wait until the last day to do everything.
	•But now I try to finish things step by step.
	•And I will use a schedule and check my progress.

4.  Wanted to avoid hard things—（＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿）
	•I used to say “I don’t want to do this” when it felt too hard.
	•But now I try to face the problem and ask for help.
	•And I will push myself to try before giving up.

5.  Just wanted to finish quickly—（＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿）
	•I used to just do the report to pass.
	•But now I care about doing it well.
	•And I will put more heart into my next work.

6.  No passion, just homework—（＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿）
	•I used to think this was just another boring task.
	•But now I see that this project is about learning something real.
	•And I will choose a topic I love next time.

7. 比較句型結語 — Growing with experience
	•The older I get, the more I learn that effort brings meaning.
	•The more I try, the more I grow.
	•The more I wait, the harder things become.
	•The more I care, the better I become.
🔄 Start-up: Think About Your Journey
✍️ Use the sentence patterns below to describe your personal change through the project. You can talk about teamwork, presentations, time management, or other personal growth.
過去 – I used to... (Before the project, what was difficult for you?) I used to _____________________________________. (e.g., feel nervous, not know how to start, avoid problems) 
現在– But now I... (What is different about you now?) But now I _____________________________________. (e.g., feel more confident, organize better, speak clearly) 
未來– And I will... (What will you try next time?) And I will _____________________________________. (e.g., plan early, help more, try harder) 
現在完成– I have learned a lesson. The older I get, the more I learn... (Use "the more..., the more..." to describe your growth.) The older I get, the more I learn that _____________________________________.
