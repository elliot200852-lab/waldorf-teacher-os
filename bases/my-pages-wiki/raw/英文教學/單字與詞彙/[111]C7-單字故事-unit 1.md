                                         A Story of Prince Tom
Once in a small village, there was a kind boy named Tom. He found a baby bird that couldn't fly and cared for it. Suddenly, an angel appeared and told Tom that the king and queen wanted to meet him.
    Excited, Tom went to the king’s castle and became friends with the prince and princess. One day, a big storm hit the kingdom. Tom gathered the villagers and together they helped everyone.
    The king and queen were thankful and made Tom a lovely prince. Prince Tom continued to show kindness, helping others. Even a young boy can make a big difference by helping and being kind to the people around him.
