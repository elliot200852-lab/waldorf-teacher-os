The Story of Rip Van Winkle
Rip Van Winkle lived in a village near the mountains. He was known for being lazy and often tried to escape chores and his wife's complaints. One day, he went into the mountains with his dog, Wolf.
He met strange men playing a game. They gave him a drink which made him fall asleep. When he woke up, everything was different. His gun was old, his dog was gone, and he had a long beard.
Rip went back to his village but didn't recognize it because he had slept for twenty years. He had missed a big war called the American Revolution. His wife was gone, and his son was all grown up. At first, he was confused, but he soon got used to his new life. He told stories about the past and enjoyed a quiet, simple life. His strange sleep showed the villagers how fast time flies.
