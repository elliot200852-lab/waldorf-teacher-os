The Monks of The Monastery

They take three holy vows, 
Obedience, Poverty, and Chastity. 
Shaving his face and his head
Showing he was free of vanity. 
Life in the monastery is only 
Pray and work. 
Monks learned to write down the wisdom
Of the Bible and of ancient Rome and Greek.
A monastery was like a little island of culture.
