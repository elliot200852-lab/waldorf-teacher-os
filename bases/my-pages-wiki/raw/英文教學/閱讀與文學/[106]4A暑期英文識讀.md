Morning has come. 
Night is away. 
Rise with the sun. 
Welcome the day.

I found a house. 
I can’t open the door. 
I climbed the window.
I jumped on the floor.
I ate the chicken on the table.
I drank the wine on the chair. 
Then I went to bed. 

There are two eggs in the nest. 
There are four nests in the garden.
How many eggs are there in this farm?
The farmer has six horses, three cows, five rabbits, and seven bears. 
How many animals does the farmer have?

My baby can sleep. 
My brother can walk.
My sister can run. 
My mother can jump.
My father can fly.

Elephants are big. 
Ants are small.
Winter is cold.
Summer is hot.
My bike is new.
His bike is old.

Every morning, we go to school.
At school, we open the door.
We wash the window.
We clean the floor.
And then, we start the class.
