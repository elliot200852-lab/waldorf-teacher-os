有著金星閃耀的天堂，
天使從那兒帶著我，
來到了地球之上，
降生在我可愛的家。
歡迎，歡迎這金光閃耀的一天，
陽光明媚，鮮花綻放，鳥兒歡唱，
為我帶來善良、仁慈和堅強。
In heaven shines a golden star
An angel brought me from afar
From heaven high unto the earth
And brought me to my house of birth
Welcome, welcome, oh golden day
With sunshine bright and flowers gay
Where painted birds do sing their song
And make me good and kind and strong.
