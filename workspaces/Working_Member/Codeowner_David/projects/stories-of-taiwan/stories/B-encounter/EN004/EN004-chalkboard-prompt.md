# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN004 高砂國的傳說——日本人眼中的臺灣

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a deep black background, rendered in soft chalk pastels.

In the foreground, on the left side: a single folded letter or scroll, partially open, lying on a dark wooden surface. The paper has a warm egg-yolk yellow tone, with faint brushstroke characters visible. A bright red-gold seal stamp glows in the lower right corner of the letter.

Behind the letter, the scene opens into a vast dark sea — the "Black Water Channel" between Japan and Taiwan. Turbulent indigo and dark teal waves roll across the middle ground.

On the far right horizon, barely visible through the mist: the silhouette of a mountainous island — Taiwan — rising from the water. Its peaks catch a faint golden light from above, as if emerging from clouds.

The overall mood is one of longing, distance, and unreachable mystery. No people are visible. The letter waits. The island exists beyond reach.

Color palette: deep black background, warm egg-yolk yellow for the letter, deep indigo and teal for the sea, pale golden white for the distant mountains, soft red-gold for the seal. Chalk texture throughout, slightly smudged at the edges.

Style: Waldorf blackboard drawing, impressionistic, no hard outlines, colors blend softly into each other.

## 中文翻譯

華德福黑板畫風格，深黑色背景，以柔和粉筆色調繪製。

前景左側：一封半開的信或卷軸，靜靜平躺在深色木桌上。紙張呈現溫暖的蛋黃色調，隱約可見筆觸字跡。信的右下角有一個發光的紅金印章。

信的背後，場景開展成廣闊的黑暗海洋——日本與臺灣之間的「黑水溝」。深靛藍和深青色的波浪翻滾於畫面中段。

最右方的地平線上，在薄霧中隱約可見：一座多山島嶼的輪廓——臺灣——從水中升起。山峰在上方的微弱金光中若隱若現，彷彿從雲霧中現身。

整體氛圍是渴望、距離與難以觸及的神祕感。畫中沒有人物。那封信靜靜等待。島嶼遠在彼端。

色彩：深黑色底、蛋黃色信紙、深靛藍與深青色海洋、淡金白色遠山、柔和紅金印章。全幅保留粉筆質感，邊緣略為模糊。
