# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN006 一萬張鹿皮——漢人為什麼要來臺灣

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create a Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a solid black background, rendered as if drawn with soft chalk pastels. The scene depicts a single Formosan sika deer standing in a vast grassland plain at golden hour. The deer faces slightly to the right, head raised alertly, with its distinctive white spots (like plum blossom petals) clearly visible on its warm brown coat. The grassland stretches to the horizon with soft, flowing grass rendered in muted greens and golden yellows. In the far background, gentle hills suggest the western Taiwan plains. A warm sunset glow in amber and deep orange illuminates the scene from behind, creating a soft halo around the deer. The overall mood is one of quiet dignity mixed with gentle melancholy — this could be the last deer on the plain, or the first one to return. The chalk technique should show visible strokes and soft blending, with the black background showing through in places, creating depth and atmosphere. No text, no borders.

## 中文翻譯

在純黑色背景上創作一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，以柔和的粉筆蠟筆效果呈現。畫面描繪一隻臺灣梅花鹿獨自站在廣闊的草原平原上，時間是黃金時刻。鹿面向右側微偏，抬頭警覺地望向遠方，溫暖的棕色皮毛上清晰可見其標誌性的白色斑點（如梅花花瓣）。草原延伸至地平線，以柔和流動的草叢呈現，色調為低調的綠色和金黃色。遠處背景中，和緩的丘陵暗示臺灣西部平原。暖色調的夕陽光芒（琥珀色與深橘色）從後方照亮場景，在鹿的周圍形成柔和的光暈。整體氛圍是安靜的尊嚴中帶著淡淡的憂傷——這可能是平原上最後一隻鹿，也可能是第一隻回來的鹿。粉筆技法應呈現可見的筆觸與柔和的暈染，黑色背景在某些地方透出，營造深度與氛圍。無文字、無邊框。
