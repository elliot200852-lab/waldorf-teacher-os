# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN002 海盜、商人與幽靈之島

## English Prompt

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration on a deep black background. In the center, a large wooden sailing junk with dark sails is silhouetted against a twilight sea, the sail slightly torn and weathered. Faint mist rises from the water surface. In the far background, a mysterious island coastline is barely visible through the fog — shadowy cliffs and dense forest. Above, a few scattered stars glow softly in deep indigo. The sea surface has gentle waves reflecting faint starlight. The overall mood is mysterious and tense, yet beautiful — the feeling of secret voyages across East Asian waters at night. Drawn in chalk-like white, pale blue, and soft amber on black, with loose gestural strokes. No text.

## 中文翻譯

以深黑色背景呈現的華德福風格黑板畫。畫面中央，一艘大型木造帆船的剪影映在暮色海面上，帆略顯破損，充滿歷史感。海面升起薄霧，遠方背景中，神秘的島嶼海岸線若隱若現——隱約的峭壁與茂密的叢林。天空深靛藍色，幾顆星星微微發光。海面泛起粼光，映照星輝。整體氛圍神秘而緊繃，卻又帶著美感——夜晚秘密航行於東亞海域的感受。使用粉筆般的白色、淡藍色、柔和琥珀色，在黑色底上以自由筆觸繪製。無文字。
