# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN001 羊皮紙上的美麗之島

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered in soft chalk strokes with warm amber and cream tones in the foreground and cool ocean blues on the right.

Scene: A cartographer's candlelit workshop at night. In the foreground, two hands lean over a large aged parchment map, holding a white quill pen. The parchment shows faint, rough outlines of continents — Asia, Japan's islands. Near the lower right of the visible parchment, the words "I. Fremosa" are written in small, careful chalk letters.

In the upper right corner of the composition, fading into cool blue distance: a tall three-masted Portuguese sailing ship (carrack) riding ocean swells. Behind the ship, barely visible, a mountain silhouette rises — suggesting a large island with a high ridge.

The candlelight casts a warm circle around the cartographer's hands, making the parchment glow amber. The drawing style is gentle, impressionistic — soft edges, no harsh lines. The feeling is intimate and mysterious, as if we are witnessing a secret moment: someone naming a place they have never visited.

## 中文翻譯

黑色背景上的華德福風格粉筆畫，前景使用溫暖的琥珀色和米色，右側轉為冷藍色調。

場景：夜晚的製圖師工坊，燭光昏黃。前景是兩隻手趴在一張巨大的羊皮紙地圖上，握著白色鵝毛筆。羊皮紙上有模糊的大陸輪廓——亞洲、日本群島。在可見羊皮紙的右下方，用小小的謹慎粉筆字寫著「I. Fremosa」。

右上角漸漸轉為冷藍：一艘三桅葡萄牙帆船在海浪中航行。船後方，幾乎看不清楚地，有一道山脈輪廓浮現——暗示一座有高山脊梁的大島。

燭光在製圖師的雙手周圍投下溫暖的光圈，讓羊皮紙發出琥珀色光芒。畫風溫柔、印象派——邊緣柔和，沒有硬線條。整體氛圍是親密而神秘的，像是我們見證了一個秘密時刻：一個人，為一個從未去過的地方，寫下了名字。
