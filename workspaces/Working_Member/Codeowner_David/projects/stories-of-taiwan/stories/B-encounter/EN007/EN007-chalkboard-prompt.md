# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN007 白銀的航線——馬尼拉大帆船與臺灣

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered entirely in soft chalk pastels. At the center of the composition, a large luminous silver coin glows with warm white and pale gold chalk strokes, its surface showing a faintly etched royal profile on one side. From the coin, sweeping curved lines radiate outward like ocean currents — these are trade routes drawn in soft blue and teal chalk. The routes arc across a stylized Pacific Ocean, connecting three glowing points: to the left, a small harbor town with terracotta roofs representing Acapulco; at the bottom, a tropical port with palm trees and a church tower representing Manila; to the upper right, a crescent-shaped harbor with Chinese junks and lanterns representing Yuegang (Moon Harbor). In the very center where all routes converge, a small island shape — Taiwan — is highlighted with a warm golden-yellow glow, like a beacon at a crossroads. A single tall-masted galleon with billowing sails traverses the ocean between Manila and Taiwan, its hull rendered in earthy brown and cream chalk. The overall mood is one of wonder and connection — the world drawn together by silver light. Stars are scattered across the dark sky above. The style is dreamlike, organic, and hand-drawn, with visible chalk texture throughout.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，在純黑背景上，以柔和的粉筆色彩繪製。畫面中央是一枚發光的銀幣，以溫暖的白色和淡金色粉筆筆觸繪製，表面隱約刻著國王側面像。從銀幣向外延伸出像洋流般的弧線——這些是以柔和藍色和青色粉筆畫出的貿易航線。航線橫跨風格化的太平洋，連接三個發光的節點：左方是一座有赤陶屋頂的小港鎮，代表阿卡普爾科；下方是一座有棕櫚樹和教堂塔樓的熱帶港口，代表馬尼拉；右上方是一座月牙形港灣，停著中式帆船並掛著燈籠，代表月港。在所有航線交匯的正中央，一個小島的輪廓——臺灣——被溫暖的金黃色光芒照亮，如同十字路口上的燈塔。一艘高桅大帆船鼓著帆在馬尼拉與臺灣之間航行，船身以土棕色和奶白色粉筆繪製。整體氛圍充滿驚奇與連結——世界被銀色的光線牽引在一起。暗色天空中散布著星星。風格如夢似幻、有機且手繪，全圖可見粉筆質感。
