# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN005 地圖上的謎之島

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered entirely in soft chalk pastels. The scene depicts a 16th-century cartographer's workshop viewed from above the work table. At the center, a large piece of parchment is spread out, showing a hand-drawn map of an island — but the island is split into three separate pieces, with gaps between them suggesting sea straits. The parchment glows in warm golden-ochre tones against the black background. A quill pen rests beside the map, and a small inkwell sits nearby. In the upper portion of the composition, faintly sketched in blue-grey chalk, the true outline of Taiwan as a single complete island emerges like a ghostly correction hovering above the flawed map below. Soft candlelight from the left casts warm amber highlights on the parchment edges. The overall mood is contemplative and mysterious — the tension between imagination and reality. Use only chalk-like textures: soft edges, visible grain, gentle color blending. No hard lines, no digital sharpness. Colors: warm gold and amber for the parchment and candlelight, blue-grey and soft white for the ghost island above, deep earth tones for the quill and inkwell.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，純黑背景，全部以柔和粉筆色調繪製。場景從上方俯瞰 16 世紀製圖師的工作桌。畫面中央，一張大羊皮紙攤開，上面畫著一座島嶼的手繪地圖——但這座島被分成三個分開的部分，間隔處暗示著海峽。羊皮紙在黑色背景上散發溫暖的金赭色光芒。鵝毛筆擱在地圖旁邊，小墨水瓶在一側。畫面上方，以淡淡的藍灰色粉筆隱約勾勒出臺灣真實的完整輪廓，如幽靈般的修正浮現在下方有缺陷的地圖之上。左側的燭光在羊皮紙邊緣投下溫暖的琥珀色高光。整體氛圍沉思而神秘——想像與現實之間的張力。只使用粉筆般的質感：柔和的邊緣、可見的紋理、溫柔的色彩融合。沒有硬線條，沒有數位銳利感。色彩：羊皮紙和燭光用暖金色與琥珀色，上方的幽靈島嶼用藍灰色和柔白色，鵝毛筆和墨水瓶用深沉的大地色調。
