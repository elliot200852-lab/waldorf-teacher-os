# 黑板畫 Prompt：EN003 順風而來——渡過黑水溝的漁民

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered in soft chalk pastels. The scene depicts a small wooden fishing boat crossing a dark, ink-black channel of water — the "Black Water Ditch" of the Taiwan Strait. The boat is simple, with a single patched sail catching the northeast monsoon wind. One lone fisherman stands at the bow, gazing forward into the mist. The sea beneath the boat is rendered in deep indigo and black chalk strokes, swirling with invisible currents. In the far distance, barely visible through sea mist, the faint silhouette of an island chain (Penghu) emerges — a few low, flat shapes on the horizon. Above, a pale winter moon hangs in the dark sky. A school of dark mullet fish (烏魚) can be seen swimming beneath the surface, leading the way forward like guides. The overall mood is quiet, determined, and slightly melancholic — a single person following a faithful fish across the most dangerous strait in the world. Warm ochre and amber tones for the boat and fisherman contrast with the cold blue-black of the sea. Chalk texture should be visible throughout.
