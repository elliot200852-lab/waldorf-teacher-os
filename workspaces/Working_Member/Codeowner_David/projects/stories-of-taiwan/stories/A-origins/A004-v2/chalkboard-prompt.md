# 黑板畫 Prompt：A004-v2 石頭裡的秘密——卑南遺址

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create a Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, drawn with soft chalk pastels. The central image shows a pair of small human figures standing side by side with hands on their hips, with a crouching animal perched on top of their heads — this is the iconic "human-beast jade ornament" from the Beinan archaeological site in Taiwan. The figures and animal are rendered in translucent jade green chalk with subtle darker speckles, glowing softly against the black as if lit from within. Around this central jade carving, faint concentric circles radiate outward like ripples on water, suggesting the ancient maritime trade routes that carried Taiwan jade across the South China Sea. In the lower portion of the image, gentle chalk strokes suggest ocean waves in deep blue and teal. In the upper corners, tiny star-like dots in pale yellow hint at the night sky that ancient seafarers navigated by. The overall mood is one of quiet wonder and deep time — a small precious object that connected an island to the wider world three thousand years ago. The style should be soft, luminous, and dreamlike, typical of Waldorf education chalkboard art.

## 中文翻譯

在純黑色背景上，以柔和粉筆粉彩繪製一幅華德福風格的黑板畫。中央圖像是一對並肩站立的小人形，雙手叉腰，頭頂上蹲著一隻動物——這是臺灣卑南遺址出土的標誌性「人獸形玉玦」。人形和動物以半透明的玉綠色粉筆繪製，帶有細微的深色斑點，在黑色背景上柔和地發光，彷彿從內部被照亮。環繞這件中央玉雕，淡淡的同心圓向外擴散，如水面上的漣漪，暗示著將臺灣玉帶往南海的古老海上貿易路線。畫面下方，輕柔的粉筆筆觸勾勒出深藍色和藍綠色的海浪。畫面上方的角落，淡黃色的小星點暗示著古代航海者賴以導航的夜空。整體氛圍是寧靜的驚奇與深邃的時間感——一件小小的珍貴物品，在三千年前將一座島嶼與更廣闊的世界連結在一起。風格應柔和、明亮、如夢似幻，符合華德福教育黑板畫的典型特徵。
