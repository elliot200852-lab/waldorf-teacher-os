# 黑板畫 Prompt：A011 血脈的地圖

## English Prompt

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a deep black background, rendered as if drawn with soft colored chalk. The central image is a majestic tree with deep, spreading roots that stretch downward into the earth — the roots are labeled "Taiwan" in delicate chalk script. From the trunk, multiple thick branches reach outward in all directions across the composition. Each major branch ends at a distant landmass or island, indicated by small glowing dots: one branch reaches west toward a large island (Madagascar), one reaches north to Hawaii, one south to New Zealand's coastline, one east to a tiny speck (Easter Island). Along each branch, small white chalk words appear: "lima", "mata", "aloha", "Hawaiki" — words that float like seeds carried by the wind. The color palette is warm: amber-gold for the tree trunk, soft blue-green for distant oceans, delicate ivory-white for the chalk text, with the deep Pacific sky rendered in dark indigo. The composition feels expansive, as if the viewer is looking at a map of the world seen through the roots of a single great tree. The style is serene, dreamlike, and slightly luminous — characteristic of Rudolf Steiner's chalkboard drawings, with visible chalk texture throughout.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，以深黑色為背景，如用柔和的彩色粉筆繪製。畫面中心是一棵壯麗的大樹，根系向下深深延伸——根部用細緻的粉筆字標注「臺灣」。樹幹向上，多條粗壯的樹枝朝四方伸展。每條主要樹枝末端到達遠方的島嶼，以小光點標示：一條伸向西邊的大島（馬達加斯加），一條向北延伸至夏威夷，一條向南抵達紐西蘭海岸線，一條向東到達小小的一點（復活島）。沿著每條樹枝，漂浮著小白字：「lima」「mata」「aloha」「Hawaiki」——這些詞如同種子隨風飄散。色調溫暖：樹幹為琥珀金，遠方海洋為柔和藍綠，字跡為象牙白，太平洋天空為深靛藍。整體畫面開闊，如同從一棵大樹的根部向外看見整個世界地圖。風格寧靜、夢幻、略帶光澤——具有魯道夫・斯坦納黑板畫的特色，全幅可見粉筆質感。
