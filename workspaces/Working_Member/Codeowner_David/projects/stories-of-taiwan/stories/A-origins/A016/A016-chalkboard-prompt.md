# 黑板畫 Prompt：A016 追風的人

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration on a deep black background, drawn with colored chalk. Center composition: a traditional double-hulled Polynesian voyaging canoe sailing rightward on open ocean, its triangular brown sail full of wind. The sky above is deep navy blue, with a single bright white star in the upper right (the North Star) surrounded by smaller stars arranged in a arc — the Hawaiian star compass — subtle but visible. Lower left: a seabird (white tern) in mid-flight, wings spread in a simple M-shape, flying toward the horizon. Foreground: gentle ocean waves rendered as flowing curved lines in turquoise and white chalk, rhythmic and calm. Atmosphere: vast, ancient, brave. The entire image feels like it was drawn by a teacher's hand on a blackboard — slightly rough texture, glowing against the dark background. No text. Soft smudged edges for the sky color gradients.

## 中文翻譯

華德福黑板畫風格，深黑色背景，彩色粉筆繪製。畫面中央：一艘傳統雙船體波利尼西亞航海帆船，向右航行於大海上，三角形棕色帆充滿風意。天空深藍，右上角有一顆最亮的白色星（北極星），周圍環繞更小的星點，排列成弧形（星羅盤的輪廓）。左下角：一隻白燕鷗展翅飛翔，翅膀呈簡單 M 形，朝向地平線飛去。前景：海浪以流暢弧線描繪，藍綠色與白色粉筆，節奏平穩。整體氛圍：廣闊、古老、勇敢。手繪感，粉筆筆觸清晰可見。無文字。天空色彩邊緣稍有暈染。
