## English Prompt (Final Version — Attempt 3, accepted)

Waldorf chalkboard drawing. Medium: colored chalk on pure black board, layered stippling and soft blending, visible chalk grain texture. Composition: landscape format, divided into three horizontal zones. Lower zone: two massive tectonic plates rendered as broad, flowing geological forms — the left plate in deep indigo and cobalt tones, the right plate in teal and deep green — converging toward the center with gentle curved motion lines suggesting slow immense pressure. The plate edges tilt upward where they meet. Middle zone: at the collision point, layered rock strata in warm ochre, sienna, and russet are being pushed upward through the ocean surface — the ocean rendered as flowing blue-green veils on either side. The rising forms are organic and rounded, not angular or diagrammatic. Upper zone: the emerged landmass becomes a softly luminous mountain ridge, with pale gold and warm amber light radiating from behind the peaks through layered cloud forms. A thin blue-white stream descends the slope. Key aesthetic requirements: all forms flow organically with dissolved edges, colors blend into one another and into the black ground which remains visible as breathing space throughout. The geological structure (two plates, compression, uplift) must be clearly readable, but expressed through painterly warmth rather than scientific diagram. No text, no labels, no arrows. The overall feeling: slow emergence, ancient power, quiet wonder. Suitable as a teacher reference for hand-drawing on a real classroom chalkboard.

## 中文 Prompt（翻譯對照）

華德福黑板畫。媒材：彩色粉筆畫在純黑色黑板上，層疊點描與柔和暈染，可見粉筆顆粒質感。構圖：橫幅格式，分為三個水平區帶。下方區帶：兩塊巨大的板塊以寬闊、流動的地質形態呈現——左側板塊為深靛藍與鈷藍色調，右側板塊為深青與深綠色——朝中央匯聚，以柔和的曲線運動線暗示緩慢而巨大的壓力。板塊邊緣在相遇處向上傾斜。中間區帶：在碰撞點，暖赭、赭褐與紅褐色的層狀岩層正被向上推擠穿過海面——海洋以兩側流動的藍綠色薄紗呈現。上升的形態是有機的、圓潤的，不是稜角分明或圖示化的。上方區帶：浮出的陸塊化為柔和發光的山脊，淡金與暖琥珀色的光芒從山峰後方透過層疊的雲層形態放射。一條細細的藍白色溪流沿山坡而下。關鍵美學要求：所有形態以溶解邊緣有機地流動，色彩相互融合並融入黑色底色中，黑色底色作為呼吸空間始終可見。地質結構（兩塊板塊、擠壓、隆起）必須清晰可讀，但以繪畫的溫暖而非科學圖示來表現。無文字、無標籤、無箭頭。整體感覺：緩慢浮現、遠古力量、靜默的驚嘆。適合作為教師在真實教室黑板上手繪的參考。
