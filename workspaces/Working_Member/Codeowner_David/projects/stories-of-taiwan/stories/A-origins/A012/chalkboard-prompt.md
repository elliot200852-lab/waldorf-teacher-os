# 黑板畫 Prompt：A012 飛魚的守護者

## English Prompt

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a deep black background, rendered in soft chalk lines with warm luminosity. In the center of the composition, a traditional Tao (Dawu) outrigger fishing boat floats on a calm midnight sea. The boat's hull is decorated with intricate red, black, and white geometric patterns — human figures, fish shapes, and wave curves carved into the wood. Above the ocean surface, three flying fish leap through the air, their wing-like fins spread wide, catching the light of a large full moon positioned in the upper right corner. The moon casts a path of silver-white light across the dark water. In the distant background, a small island silhouette rises — Orchid Island (Lanyu), with low hills and a single tall mountain. The overall palette is deep indigo and black for water, warm chalk-white for the moon and foam, burnt orange and vermillion for the boat's decorations, and electric yellow-green for the flying fish. The mood is sacred and intimate, as if the sea itself is listening to an ancient promise.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格的黑板畫，深黑色背景，柔和的粉筆線條帶著溫暖光暈。畫面中央，一艘達悟族傳統拼板舟漂浮在平靜的深夜海面上，船身刻有精細的紅、黑、白幾何圖案——人形紋、魚形紋和波浪曲線。海面上方，三條飛魚躍起，展開扇形翅鰭迎風飛翔，右上角有一輪滿月灑下銀白色的月光，在深色海面上拉出一條光路。遠景中，蘭嶼的島嶼剪影隱現，低矮的丘陵和一座較高的山峰。整體色調以深靛藍與黑色為水面，粉筆白色為月光和泡沫，燒橘與朱砂紅為船身裝飾，電光黃綠為飛魚。氣氛神聖而親密，如同大海正在傾聽一個千年的承諾。
