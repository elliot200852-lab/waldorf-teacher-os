# 黑板畫 Prompt：A008 浪的節奏——阿美族的海與豐年祭

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration drawn with chalk on a dark slate background. The scene depicts a lone Indigenous Taiwanese man standing on a rocky coastal shore, viewed from behind, gazing out toward the vast Pacific Ocean. He holds a bamboo pole or traditional paddle at his side. The ocean stretches wide before him, with gentle waves catching pale chalk-white highlights. The horizon is soft and glowing, suggesting early morning light. Coastal mountains rise faintly in the distance behind him to the left. A few seabirds trace arcs across the upper sky. In the foreground, large dark rocks and sea grass are sketched in rough strokes. The color palette is cool — deep ocean blues, sea greens, and white chalk highlights on waves and sky — with warm amber tones only at the horizon. The overall mood is quiet reverence: a person facing the immensity of the sea, carrying the memory of ancestors who once crossed it.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，在深色石板背景上以粉筆繪製。場景描繪一位臺灣原住民男子背身站在岩岸上，凝望廣闊的太平洋。他身側握著一根竹竿或傳統槳。海洋向前延伸，溫和的海浪帶著白色粉筆高光。地平線柔和而發光，暗示清晨的光芒。遠方左側，海岸山脈在霧氣中隱約可見。幾隻海鳥在天空上方劃出弧線。前景是以粗獷筆觸勾勒的深色礁石與海草。色調以冷色為主——深海藍、海洋綠、海浪與天空的白色粉筆高光——只有地平線處帶有溫暖的琥珀色調。整體氛圍是靜謐的敬畏：一個人面對無垠大海，心中攜帶著祖先渡海而來的記憶。
