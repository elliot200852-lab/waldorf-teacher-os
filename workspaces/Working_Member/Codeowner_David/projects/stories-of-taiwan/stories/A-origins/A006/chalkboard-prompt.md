# 黑板畫 Prompt：A006 獨木舟上的民族——南島語族與臺灣

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a solid black background, drawn with colored chalk. The central image is a traditional Tao (Yami) tatala boat — a plank-built canoe with a distinctively high-curved prow and stern, decorated with red and white geometric patterns. The boat rests on gentle ocean waves drawn in deep blue and teal chalk strokes. Inside the boat, two or three silhouetted figures paddle with rhythmic energy. Above the boat, a night sky filled with soft white chalk stars, with one bright guiding star slightly larger than the rest. In the background, a subtle suggestion of scattered Pacific islands fading into the distance — small green and brown landmasses connected by faint dotted lines suggesting migration routes. Flying fish leap from the water near the boat in graceful arcs, rendered in silver-white chalk. The overall composition suggests departure, courage, and the vastness of the ocean journey. Chalk texture visible throughout, luminous and soft in the characteristic Waldorf blackboard style.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格黑板畫，黑色背景上用彩色粉筆繪製。畫面中央是一艘傳統達悟族拼板舟（Tatala）——船首與船尾高高翹起，船身裝飾著紅白相間的幾何圖紋。船停在以深藍色與青綠色粉筆畫出的柔和海浪上。船上有兩三個人的剪影正有節奏地划槳。船的上方是星空，以柔和的白色粉筆畫出滿天星斗，其中一顆引路星略大於其他星星。背景中隱約可見散落的太平洋島嶼逐漸消失在遠方——小小的綠色與棕色陸塊之間以淡淡的虛線連接，暗示遷徙路線。飛魚從船旁的水面躍出，以銀白色粉筆畫出優美弧線。整體構圖傳達出啟程、勇氣與海洋旅途的壯闊。全畫保留粉筆質感，展現華德福黑板畫特有的柔和發光品質。
