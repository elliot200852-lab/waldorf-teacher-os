# 黑板畫 Prompt：A014 泥土裡的六千年——大坌坑人的秘密

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A hand-drawn Waldorf chalkboard illustration. This is NOT a photograph. This is NOT a realistic image. This is a DRAWING made with chalk on a blackboard — the kind a teacher would draw by hand in a Waldorf school classroom. Pure black background. All marks are visible chalk strokes — rough, textured, imprecise edges as real chalk on a blackboard looks.

At the center: a large clay pot drawn in simple, bold chalk lines. The pot has a round belly, a low rim, and horizontal wavy rope-pattern lines drawn around its surface — these lines are DRAWN in chalk, not photographic texture. The pot is sketched in warm white and ochre chalk strokes, not shaded realistically.

To the left: a few simple chalk lines suggesting waves or a river. To the right: a gentle mountain outline drawn in a single curved chalk line.

At the bottom, small chalk handwriting: "六千年前"

The entire image must look like a CHALK DRAWING on a blackboard — loose, expressive, hand-made. Think Rudolf Steiner blackboard drawings. No photorealism. No 3D rendering. No studio lighting. Visible chalk grain on black surface.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格黑板畫，純黑背景，軟粉筆質感。畫面中央是一個大型古代陶罐，圓底、低矮的口緣，佔據畫面主體。陶罐表面布滿橫向繩紋——密集、略帶波浪的線條螺旋環繞腹部，彷彿是用纏著繩子的棒棒拍打濕陶留下的印痕。陶罐顏色是暖紅褐色、赭色和土赤色，用柔和的粉筆筆觸呈現。陶罐左側，藍灰色柔和水波暗示河流流向大海。右側，隱約的深色山形輪廓（觀音山）從黑色背景中浮現。畫面底部有極小的細白粉筆字「六千年前」。整體氛圍寧靜、古老、莊重——彷彿我們正穿越時間，凝視那雙製作這個陶罐的手。邊緣柔和漸入黑暗，無銳利線條，全以粉筆的柔軟與溫暖呈現。
