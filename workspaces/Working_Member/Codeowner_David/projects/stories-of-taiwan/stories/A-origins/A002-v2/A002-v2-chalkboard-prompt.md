# 黑板畫 Prompt：A002 水的雕刻刀——立霧溪與太魯閣（第二版）

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered in soft chalk pastels. The composition shows a dramatic vertical cross-section of Taroko Gorge: two towering cliff walls of layered grey and white marble rise from the bottom, nearly touching at the top, with only a thin sliver of deep blue sky visible between them. At the base of the gorge, a narrow turquoise river flows between the cliffs, catching faint light. The marble walls show delicate horizontal striations — the fingerprints of millions of years of water erosion. On the upper left, a single silver raindrop falls from the sky toward the mountain peak, beginning its long journey downward. The right side of the image transitions to a gentle golden-green alluvial plain spreading toward a calm sea at the horizon. The mood is one of immense patience and quiet power — water shaping stone over eons. No text, no labels.

## 中文翻譯

請你生成一張圖片

華德福風格黑板畫，純黑背景，柔和粉筆色調。構圖呈現太魯閣峽谷的垂直剖面：兩面高聳的灰白色大理岩壁從畫面底部升起，頂端幾乎相觸，中間只露出一道深藍色天空的細縫。峽谷底部，一條細窄的碧綠色河流在岩壁之間流過，捕捉微弱的光線。大理岩壁上有細膩的水平紋理——那是百萬年水流侵蝕留下的指紋。左上方，一滴銀色的雨滴從天空落向山頂，開始它漫長的向下旅程。畫面右側過渡為平緩的金綠色沖積平原，向遠方平靜的海面延展。整體氛圍傳達無比的耐心與安靜的力量——水用億萬年的時間雕塑石頭。無文字、無標籤。
