# 黑板畫 Prompt：A003 火的禮物——大屯火山與泥火山

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered in soft chalk pastels. The composition shows a cross-section of volcanic earth: at the center bottom, a deep warm orange-red magma chamber glows beneath layers of brown and grey rock. From the chamber, thin veins of heat rise upward through cracks in the earth. On the left side, these veins reach the surface where a gentle volcanic mountain stands, with wisps of white steam and pale yellow sulfur crystals emerging from fumaroles at its peak. On the right side, a small grey mud cone sits on the surface, with a tiny, delicate blue flame hovering just above its tip — quiet and still, like a candle in windless air. The overall palette transitions from warm reds and oranges below to cool blues and whites above. The mood is one of hidden warmth and quiet power beneath a calm surface.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格黑板畫，純黑背景，柔和粉筆色調。構圖呈現火山地底的剖面：畫面中下方，深層岩漿庫以暖橘紅色光暈呈現，上方覆蓋棕灰色岩層。從岩漿庫延伸出細細的熱脈，沿裂縫向上攀升。左側，熱脈抵達地表，一座溫和的火山山體矗立，山頂噴氣孔冒出白色蒸氣與淡黃色硫磺結晶。右側，地表上有一座小小的灰色泥火山錐，頂端懸浮著一簇纖細的藍色火焰——安靜、靜止，像無風中的燭火。整體色調由下方的暖紅橘過渡到上方的冷藍白。氛圍傳達隱藏在平靜地表下的溫暖與安靜的力量。
