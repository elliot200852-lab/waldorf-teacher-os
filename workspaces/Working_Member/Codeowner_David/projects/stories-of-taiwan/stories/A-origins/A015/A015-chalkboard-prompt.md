# 黑板畫 Prompt：A015 一座小山丘裡的七層時光——芝山岩的秘密

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create an image in Waldorf chalkboard drawing style: a chalk illustration on a solid black background, drawn entirely with soft, hand-applied chalk pastels — no digital effects, no glow, no outlines, only the natural texture of chalk rubbed and layered on a dark surface.

The composition shows a cross-section of a small hill, cut open like a layered cake, revealing seven distinct horizontal strata of earth. The hill sits upon a dark landscape. At the very bottom layer, rough brown tones with wavy scratched textures suggest ancient pottery shards. The second layer is a warm reddish-brown with smooth horizontal strokes. The third layer — the most vivid — shows golden-yellow grains of rice scattered among dark wooden shapes, with a warm amber glow suggesting organic preservation. The fourth layer has white curved shell shapes on grey-green earth. The fifth layer shows geometric impressed patterns in cool grey. The sixth layer, near the top, has a small blue-toned temple silhouette. The topmost surface is lush green with simple tree shapes.

On the left side of the hill, pale blue water stretches to the horizon, evoking the ancient Taipei lake. A few distant mountain silhouettes in deep blue frame the background. The overall mood is contemplative and layered — time made visible through earth. The chalk strokes are loose, textured, and organic, with colors blending softly into the black ground.

## 中文翻譯

以華德福黑板畫風格創作一幅圖：純黑色背景上的粉筆插畫，完全使用柔軟粉筆手工繪製——無數位效果、無光暈、無輪廓線，只有粉筆在暗色表面上反覆塗抹與疊加的自然質感。

構圖呈現一座小山丘的剖面，像切開的分層蛋糕，露出七層清晰的水平地層。最底層用深棕色波浪刮痕表現古老陶片。第二層是溫暖的紅棕色光滑橫線。第三層最為鮮明——金黃色的米粒散落在深色木器形狀之間，帶有溫暖琥珀光澤暗示有機物的保存。第四層有白色弧形貝殼。第五層是冷灰色幾何印紋。第六層靠近山頂處有藍色調的小廟剪影。最上方是翠綠的地表與簡約樹形。

山丘左側有淡藍色水面延伸至地平線，喚起臺北古湖泊的意象。遠處深藍色山脈輪廓構成背景。整體氛圍沉思而層次分明——時間透過泥土變得可見。粉筆筆觸鬆散、有質感、有機，色彩在黑色底面上柔和融合。
