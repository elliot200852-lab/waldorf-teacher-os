# 黑板畫 Prompt：A010 百步蛇的子民

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a deep black background, rendered in soft colored chalks. The image depicts a cross-section view of a traditional Paiwan slate-stone house (Taiwan indigenous architecture) nestled on a mountain slope. The composition is centered and slightly elevated in perspective.

The house structure shows: thick gray slate walls stacked in interlocking layers (each layer offset from the one below), a heavy slate roof pressing down with visible weight and texture, and flat slate flooring. The main entrance has a carved wooden lintel featuring a stylized hundred-pace viper (Deinagkistrodon) motif — diamond-shaped scales rendered as a symmetrical geometric pattern in ochre and rust tones.

To the left of the house, a river valley suggests where the slate was collected. Above the roofline, a vast starlit mountain landscape with the silhouette of Mount Dawu (Kavulungan) in the distance. In the foreground, a small arrangement of objects: a string of glowing glass beads (blue, amber, green) and a ceremonial pottery vessel with serpent markings.

Color palette: deep charcoal background, warm gray for slate stones, ochre and burnt sienna for the door carving, soft teal and amber for the glass beads, deep rust for the pottery. Style is lyrical and impressionistic — not architectural blueprint, but felt sense of weight, warmth, and deep time. Suitable for a fifth-grade Waldorf classroom story about Taiwan's Paiwan indigenous culture.

## 中文翻譯

一幅深黑底色上的華德福風格黑板畫，以柔和粉筆色彩描繪。畫面呈現臺灣排灣族傳統石板屋的剖面圖，嵌在山坡上。

建築結構包含：交錯疊砌的厚重石板牆（每層石板不與上下層對齊）、向下壓重的石板屋頂（可見重量感與材質紋理），以及石板地板。正面入口有木雕門楣，刻著百步蛇圖紋——菱形鱗片對稱幾何圖案，用橙赭色和鐵鏽色表現。

房屋左側暗示著溪谷河床（石板的採集地）。屋頂上方是廣闊山景，遠處有大武山（Kavulungan）的輪廓。前景置有：一串閃爍的琉璃珠（藍、琥珀、黃綠）和一個刻有蛇紋的古陶壺。

色彩：深炭色底、暖灰色石板、橙赭色門楣木雕、柔藍和琥珀色珠串、深鏽色陶壺。風格抒情、印象派——不是建築藍圖，而是重量感、溫暖感和深時間感的表達。適合五年級華德福課堂使用。
