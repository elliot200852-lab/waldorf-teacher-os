# 黑板畫 Prompt：A009 鐵砂與煙霧

## English Prompt

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a deep black background, rendered in soft chalk colors.

The central image is a cylindrical clay furnace glowing intensely from within — the glow transitions from deep red at the base through orange to a brilliant white-yellow at the top opening, where smoke spirals upward in hazy white-gray wisps. Beside the furnace, a crouching figure (seen in silhouette or simple chalk outline) operates a leather bellows, pumping air rhythmically into the base of the furnace. Nearby, a second smaller figure — a young person — holds a stone bowl filled with dark iron sand that glitters faintly.

In the foreground, scattered on the dark ground, are chunks of dark iron slag (rough black shapes), suggesting the remnants of previous smelting. In the far background, barely suggested in soft blue-white chalk, is the shape of a river mouth opening to the sea — the Tamsui River estuary.

The overall mood is warm, elemental, and slightly mysterious — fire meeting earth at the water's edge. The color palette is: black background, terracotta and amber for the clay furnace, glowing orange-white for the fire core, smoky gray-white for the smoke, soft blue for the distant water horizon, and earthy brown for the figures. The lines are loose and expressive, as if drawn by a teacher's hand on a schoolroom chalkboard.

## 中文翻譯

一幅以深黑色為底的華德福風格黑板畫，用柔和的粉筆色彩描繪。

畫面中央是一個圓柱形黏土爐，爐心熾烈發光——從爐底的深紅，往上漸層到橙色，再到爐口的白黃色，煙霧呈灰白色往上旋繞。爐子旁邊，一個蹲著的人影（以粉筆輪廓素描）正在操作皮囊風箱，有節律地往爐底送風。附近，一個較小的人影（年輕人）捧著一個石碗，碗裡裝滿隱隱閃光的黑色鐵砂。

前景散落著幾塊深色的鐵渣（粗糙的黑色形狀），暗示著多次冶鐵留下的痕跡。遠景用柔和的藍白粉筆淡淡勾勒出河口入海的形狀——淡水河出海口。

整體氛圍溫暖、原始、帶一點神祕感——火在水邊接觸土地。色盤：黑色底、赤陶色與琥珀色的爐壁、橙白發光的爐心、灰白的煙霧、柔藍的遠方水平線、大地棕的人物輪廓。線條鬆散而有表現力，彷彿一位老師用手在黑板上即興描繪。
