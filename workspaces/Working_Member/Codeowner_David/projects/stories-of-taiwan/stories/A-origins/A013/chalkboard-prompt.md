# 黑板畫 Prompt：A013 星星告訴我們何時狩獵

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration on a deep black background, drawn with chalk. In the foreground, a Bunun indigenous boy (around 10 years old) and his father sit beside a small fire inside a low-ceiling stone house. The father holds a narrow wooden board — about 80 cm long — covered in carved symbols resembling fish bones, crescent moons, and small dots. The boy leans in, studying the symbols with wide eyes. The firelight casts warm amber and orange glows on their faces and hands. Through the low doorway in the background, a dark mountain silhouette is visible against a deep indigo night sky. Above the mountains, a thin crescent moon and a tight cluster of seven stars (the Pleiades) glow in white chalk. The style is gentle, detailed, and luminous — soft chalk strokes, no harsh outlines. The mood is quiet wonder and intergenerational intimacy.

## 中文翻譯

在深黑色背景上，以粉筆繪製的華德福風格黑板畫。前景是一個布農族少年（約10歲）和他的父親，坐在一間低矮石板屋內的小火堆旁。父親手持一塊窄長的木板——約80公分長——上面刻滿了魚骨狀、彎月狀、小點狀的符號。男孩靠近木板，用充滿好奇的眼睛仔細端詳。火光將暖橘色的光投在他們的臉和手上。透過低矮的門口，背景可以看到深藍色夜空下的山脈剪影。山脈上方，一彎新月和七顆緊聚的星星（昴宿星群）以白色粉筆發光。風格溫柔、細緻而明亮——柔和的粉筆筆觸，無強烈輪廓。整體氛圍是靜謐的驚嘆與跨代的親密。
