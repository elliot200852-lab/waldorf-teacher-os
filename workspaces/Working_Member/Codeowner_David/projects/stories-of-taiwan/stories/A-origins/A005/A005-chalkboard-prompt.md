# 黑板畫 Prompt：A005 海的搖籃——黑潮與臺灣

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a solid black background, drawn with colored chalk. The scene depicts the island of Taiwan viewed from the east, as if floating in a vast ocean. A powerful, sweeping current of deep indigo-blue chalk flows from the lower right (south) along the eastern coast of Taiwan, curving northward — this is the Kuroshio Current, rendered as a living river within the sea. The current is depicted with flowing, rhythmic lines suggesting movement and warmth, with hints of turquoise at its edges where it meets the shore. Beneath the surface on the lower left, a cluster of vibrant coral reef forms in warm oranges, pinks, and soft greens emerge from the ocean floor. Small fish silhouettes swim among the coral. The island itself is rendered in earthy greens and browns, with a suggestion of mountain ridges. Above, a crescent moon and a few stars in soft white chalk. The overall mood is one of gentle power and abundance — the ocean embracing and nourishing the island. Chalk texture visible throughout, with the characteristic soft, luminous quality of Waldorf blackboard art.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格黑板畫，黑色背景上用彩色粉筆繪製。畫面描繪從東方望去的臺灣島，如同漂浮在廣闊的大海中。一道深靛藍色的強勁洋流從右下方（南方）沿著臺灣東海岸向北流動——這是黑潮，被描繪為一條海中的活河。洋流以流動的、有節奏的線條表現，暗示著運動與溫暖，邊緣帶有青綠色。在左下方水面之下，一叢鮮豔的珊瑚礁以暖橘色、粉紅色和柔和的綠色從海底生長出來。小魚的剪影在珊瑚間游動。島嶼本身以土綠色和棕色呈現，隱約可見山脊的輪廓。上方有一彎新月和幾顆星星。整體氛圍是溫柔的力量與豐盈——海洋擁抱並滋養著這座島嶼。全畫保留粉筆質感，展現華德福黑板畫特有的柔和發光品質。
