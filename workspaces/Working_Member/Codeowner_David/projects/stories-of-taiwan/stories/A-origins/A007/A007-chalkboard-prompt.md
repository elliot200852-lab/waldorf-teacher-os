# 黑板畫 Prompt：A007 山是我們的家——泰雅族與他們的獵場

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, rendered entirely in soft chalk pastels. The scene depicts a misty mountain landscape of central Taiwan at dawn. In the foreground, a solitary Atayal hunter stands on a narrow mountain trail, seen from behind, carrying a traditional hunting bow across his shoulders. He is small against the vast mountains, wearing simple traditional clothing. The trail winds through dense subtropical forest with tall camphor trees and ferns. In the middle ground, layers of mountain ridges recede into the distance, each layer progressively lighter in tone — dark green, sage green, pale blue-green, misty white. At the very back, the distinctive barrel-shaped silhouette of Mount Dabajian (Papakwaqa) rises above the clouds, its flat top catching the first golden light. Wisps of cloud drift between the mountain ridges. A small stream glints silver in the valley below. The overall palette uses deep forest greens, cool blue-greys, warm ochre for the dawn light, and pure white for the mist. The mood is reverent, quiet, and vast — a single human being at home in an immense mountain world. The chalk texture should be soft and slightly grainy, with visible strokes that suggest rather than define, in the characteristic style of Waldorf blackboard illustration.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，純黑背景，全部以柔和的粉筆色彩繪製。場景描繪臺灣中部山區黎明時分的霧氣山景。前景中，一位泰雅族獵人獨自站在狹窄的山徑上，背對觀者，肩上斜背傳統獵弓。他的身影在巨大的群山面前顯得渺小，穿著簡樸的傳統服飾。山徑蜿蜒穿過茂密的亞熱帶森林，高大的樟樹與蕨類植物環繞。中景是層層退遠的山稜線，每一層色調漸淡——深綠、灰綠、淡藍綠、霧白。最遠處，大霸尖山（Papakwaqa）獨特的酒桶形剪影高聳入雲，平坦的山頂捕捉到第一道金色晨光。雲霧絲絲縷縷地飄浮在山脊之間。山谷下方一條小溪閃著銀光。整體色調使用深森林綠、冷藍灰、暖赭色的晨光、以及純白的霧氣。氛圍是崇敬的、安靜的、遼闊的——一個渺小的人類，在無邊的山林世界中安然自在。粉筆質感柔和且略帶顆粒感，筆觸以暗示而非精確描繪的方式呈現，符合華德福黑板畫的典型風格。
