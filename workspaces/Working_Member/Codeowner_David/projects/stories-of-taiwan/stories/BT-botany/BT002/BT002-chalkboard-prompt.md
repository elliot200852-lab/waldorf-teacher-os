# 黑板畫 Prompt：BT002 被留在山頂的旅人——臺灣高山的冰河遺孤

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create a Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, drawn with soft colored chalk. The scene depicts a high mountain ridge in Taiwan at dawn, viewed from a slightly elevated perspective looking along the ridgeline.

In the foreground on the left, a gnarled and windswept Yushan juniper (玉山圓柏) sprawls low across broken rocks, its ancient trunk twisted horizontally by centuries of wind, with deep green-blue foliage hugging the ground like a living carpet. Its roots grip the pale grey scree.

Rising behind it in the center-left, several Taiwan fir trees (臺灣冷杉) stand in a cluster, their distinctive narrow spire-shaped crowns reaching upward like dark green cathedral towers against the sky. They form a dark, dense silhouette — the famous "Black Forest" of Taiwan's high mountains.

On the right side of the ridge, a patch of Yushan rhododendrons (玉山杜鵑) blooms with soft white and pale pink flowers among low bushes, their petals catching the first warm light.

In a rocky crevice in the lower right foreground, a tiny Yushan edelweiss (玉山薄雪草) crouches with its distinctive white woolly leaves, almost glowing against the dark rock.

The sky transitions from deep indigo at the top to a warm golden-rose glow at the horizon, suggesting dawn breaking over the Pacific Ocean far below. Wisps of cloud drift below the ridge line, emphasizing the extreme altitude. A few distant mountain peaks emerge from the sea of clouds.

The overall mood is one of quiet resilience and ancient solitude — these plants are survivors stranded on the mountaintop after the ice age retreated. The chalk technique uses soft blending for the sky and clouds, with more defined strokes for the plants to give them character and presence. Colors: deep greens, blue-blacks, warm golds, soft pinks, and chalky whites against the black background.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格粉筆黑板畫，純黑背景。場景為臺灣高山稜線的黎明時分，從稍微升高的視角沿稜線望去。

前景左側，一棵飽經風霜的玉山圓柏匍匐在碎石上，古老的樹幹被數百年的風扭成水平姿態，深藍綠色的枝葉像活地毯般緊貼地面，根部緊抓灰白色碎石。

中央偏左後方，數棵臺灣冷杉成群聳立，獨特的狹窄尖塔形樹冠如深綠色的教堂塔樓直指天際，形成濃密暗色剪影——臺灣高山著名的「黑森林」。

稜線右側，一叢玉山杜鵑在矮灌叢間綻放柔和的白色與淡粉紅色花朵，花瓣承接第一道暖光。

右前方岩縫中，一株小小的玉山薄雪草蹲踞著，白色絨毛葉片在暗色岩石上幾乎發光。

天空從頂端的深靛藍過渡到地平線的暖金玫瑰色光芒，暗示黎明從遙遠的太平洋升起。稜線下方飄著幾縷雲霧，強調極端的海拔高度。幾座遠方山峰從雲海中露出。

整體氣氛是安靜的堅韌與古老的孤寂——這些植物是冰河退去後被留在山頂的倖存者。粉筆技法：天空與雲用柔和暈染，植物用更明確的筆觸賦予個性與存在感。色彩：深綠、藍黑、暖金、柔粉、粉白，映襯純黑背景。
