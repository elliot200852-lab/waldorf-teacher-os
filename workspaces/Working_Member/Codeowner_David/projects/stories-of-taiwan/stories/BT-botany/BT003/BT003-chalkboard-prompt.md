# 黑板畫 Prompt：BT003 千年巨人的呼吸——阿里山神木與臺灣檜木森林

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create a Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background, drawn with soft colored chalk. The scene depicts an ancient Taiwanese hinoki cypress forest (紅檜森林) in the misty cloud-forest zone of central Taiwan, viewed from within the forest.

In the center of the composition stands a massive ancient red cypress (紅檜), its thick trunk rising like a living pillar toward the top of the frame. The bark is deeply furrowed and covered with thick patches of luminous green moss. The trunk is so wide that only part of it fits in the frame, emphasizing the tree's enormous scale. Its branches spread high above, disappearing into soft drifting white mist.

To the right of the main tree, a second gigantic cypress stands slightly behind, creating depth. The forest floor is carpeted with ferns, moss, and fallen logs that are themselves covered in moss — living nurse logs.

In the lower left foreground, a weathered old tree stump cut flat on top (a remnant of past logging) rises about waist-high, its surface covered in thick moss like a living bed. On top of the stump, several small young hinoki saplings are growing — the next generation rising from the old giant's shoulders. The saplings are a vivid fresh green, catching soft light.

Soft white mist drifts between the trees at multiple levels, giving the forest an ethereal breathing quality. The light filters from above in pale golden-white shafts, suggesting morning light piercing through the clouds. The overall palette is deep forest greens, mossy blue-greens, rich brown-black for the cypress bark, soft luminous white for the mist, and chalky fresh green for the young saplings.

The chalk technique uses soft blending for the mist and distant trees, with more defined textured strokes for the cypress bark and the moss-covered stump in the foreground. The mood is one of deep quiet, ancient breathing time, and continuity of life — the old and the new living together in the cloud forest.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格粉筆黑板畫，純黑背景。場景為臺灣中海拔霧林帶的古老紅檜原始林，視角由林中向上望。

構圖中央矗立一棵巨大的老紅檜，粗大的樹幹如活著的柱子向上延伸至畫面頂端。樹皮深深龜裂，覆蓋著厚厚的發光綠苔。樹幹寬到只有部分入畫，強調樹的巨大尺度。枝幹高展，消失在柔和飄動的白色雲霧之中。

主樹右側稍後處佇立第二棵巨大檜木，形成景深。林地上鋪滿蕨類、苔蘚與倒木——倒木本身也覆蓋著苔蘚，成為活著的苗床。

左前方有一個被平整砍斷的老樹頭（過去伐木的遺跡）約腰部高度聳立，頂部覆蓋厚厚苔蘚像活著的床。樹頭上長著數株小小的檜木幼苗——下一代從老巨人的肩膀上升起。幼苗是鮮明翠綠色，承接柔和光線。

柔和的白霧在樹間多層飄動，賦予森林空靈的呼吸感。光線從上方以淡金白色光柱透入，暗示晨光穿越雲霧。整體色調為深森林綠、苔蘚藍綠、檜木樹皮的濃棕黑、霧氣的柔和發光白，以及幼苗的粉筆質清新綠。

粉筆技法：霧與遠樹用柔和暈染，紅檜樹皮與前景的苔樹頭用更明確的紋理筆觸。氣氛：深沉寂靜、古老的呼吸時間、生命的延續——在雲霧森林中老與新共同生活。
