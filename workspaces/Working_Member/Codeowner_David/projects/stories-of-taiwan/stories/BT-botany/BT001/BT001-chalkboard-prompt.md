# 黑板畫 Prompt：BT001 三千公尺的綠色階梯

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create a Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a pure black background using soft colored chalk. The image depicts a vertical cross-section of Taiwan's vegetation zones, like a living elevator from sea level to mountaintop.

On the left side, a gentle mountain slope rises from bottom-left to upper-right. At the very bottom, turquoise and green strokes show mangrove roots reaching into tidal water, with small pen-shaped seedlings (viviparous propagules) hanging from branches. Moving upward, the forest transitions through lush dark-green broadleaf canopies with round crowns (camphor and nanmu trees), then into a misty zone rendered with soft white and gray chalk wisps where tall, majestic cypress trees (red cypress and hinoki) stand like ancient sentinels — their trunks drawn with warm ochre and reddish-brown tones. Higher still, the trees become pointed and conical — dark green spruces and hemlocks with hanging pale-green lichen threads. Near the top, perfectly straight fir trees stand like tall green candles along a ridgeline. At the very summit, low shrubs crouch against wind-swept rocks, and clusters of pale pink-white rhododendron flowers bloom defiantly against the harsh alpine sky.

A thin crescent moon glows in soft yellow chalk at the upper-right corner, hinting at the legend of "the tree that touches the moon." The overall composition flows naturally from warm tropical greens at the bottom to cool alpine tones at the top, with cloud wisps at the middle elevations creating a dreamy transition. The style is organic, hand-drawn, with visible chalk texture and gentle color gradients — no sharp lines, evoking the warmth and artistry of a Waldorf classroom blackboard.

## 中文翻譯

在純黑色背景上，以柔和的彩色粉筆繪製一幅華德福風格的黑板畫。畫面呈現臺灣植被帶的垂直剖面——一座從海平面到山頂的活電梯。

左側，一道柔緩的山坡從左下角升向右上角。最底部，用藍綠色和綠色筆觸表現紅樹林的根系伸入潮間帶水中，枝條上懸掛著筆狀的胎生苗。往上，森林轉變為濃密的深綠色闊葉林，圓潤的樹冠代表樟楠。再往上進入雲霧帶，用柔和的白灰色粉筆渲染霧氣，其間矗立著高大莊嚴的檜木，樹幹以暖赭色和紅褐色繪製，像古老的哨兵。更高處，樹形變為尖錐狀——深綠色的雲杉和鐵杉，枝條垂掛著淡綠色的松蘿。接近頂部，筆直的冷杉像巨大的綠色蠟燭排列在稜線上。最頂端，矮灌木匍匐在風蝕岩石間，成簇的粉白色玉山杜鵑在嚴峻的高山天空下倔強綻放。

右上角一彎新月以柔和的黃色粉筆微微發光，暗示「撞到月亮的樹」的傳說。整體構圖從底部溫暖的熱帶綠色自然過渡到頂部清冷的高山色調，中段雲霧營造夢幻般的轉場。風格有機、手繪，可見粉筆質感與柔和的色彩漸層——沒有銳利線條，喚起華德福教室黑板的溫度與藝術性。
