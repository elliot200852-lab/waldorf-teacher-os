# B012 花的構造 — 素材包

## Kovacs Ch. 12 核心摘要

### Core Teaching Image
The blossom as the culmination of a plant's "inner schooling." The calyx (sepals) = hands first in prayer, then opening to hold a precious gift. Flower parts introduced through a king's coronation regalia — sceptre (pistil) and orb (ovary).

### Storytelling Technique
- Buttercup as the worked example: grows with only green leaves, then stops, forms a mysterious green shell (calyx); if you cut it open too early, nothing inside — only patience reveals the blossom
- Personifies the calyx as hands in prayer → hands holding a gift
- Introduces stamens as a crown around the royal sceptre and orb
- Sun and earth gifts: green pistil + ovary = earth's gift; golden stamens + pollen = sun's gift

### Scientific Facts (Four Concentric Circles)

| Ring | Part | Role |
|------|------|------|
| 1 (outermost) | Calyx / Sepals | Modified green leaves forming protective cup |
| 2 | Petals | Coloured leaves of the flower |
| 3 | Stamens | Fine stalks in a circle, golden heads of pollen |
| 4 (innermost) | Pistil + Ovary | Rod-like pistil above, swollen ovary below |

- Buttercup leaves change as plant grows: large near ground → smaller and finer higher up
- "Calyx" means cup; sepals = the individual green leaves

### Key Quotes
1. "The sepals, that together form the calyx, are at first like hands in prayer, and then they are like hands holding a precious gift, and that is the flower."
2. "Just as it is not always easy to learn, to remember, to think, so it is not so easy for plants to bear blossoms."
3. "The green pistil and the round ovary (itself like a little earth) are the gift of the earth. And the stamens, standing in a circle, with the golden pollen, are the gift of the sun."

## 台灣植物對應資料

### 台灣百合 Lilium formosanum（台灣特有種）

花部構造（單子葉特殊性）：
- 花被片（tepals）：6 枚，無明確區分花萼與花瓣，外輪 3 + 內輪 3
- 花冠呈六角形喇叭狀，白色，外側有紫褐色條紋
- 雄蕊：6 枚，花絲細長，花藥黃色或黃紫色
- 雌蕊：1 枚，柱頭 3 裂，子房上位，3 室
- 無獨立花萼——百合科單子葉植物花被片同型
- 「3 的倍數」結構與雙子葉「4 或 5 的倍數」形成對照
- 分布：海平面到 3,500 公尺
- 花期：春、夏間

### 毛茛 Ranunculus japonicus（buttercup，台灣原生種）
- 保育等級：接近受脅（NT）
- 海拔範圍：200-2,500 公尺
- 花部：黃色，花瓣 5 枚，具蠟質光澤表面
- 典型雙子葉完全花：花萼、花瓣、雄蕊、雌蕊齊全

### 梅花 Prunus mume（中華民國國花）
- 1964 年核定為國花
- 5 瓣，雄蕊多數，雌蕊 1 枚
- 先花後葉——花的準備在看不見時就開始
- 三蕾五瓣象徵三民主義及五權憲法

### 桐花（客家桐花祭）
- 5 瓣，白色，花小而密集
- 雌雄同株或異株——「不完全花」實例
- 花期 4 月底至 5 月初，落花如「五月雪」

### 花的四環結構（台灣教育術語）

| 中文 | 英文 | 說明 |
|------|------|------|
| 花萼 | calyx/sepals | 最外層，保護花蕾 |
| 花瓣 | petals | 薄軟，常有顏色吸引昆蟲 |
| 雄蕊 | stamens | 花藥 + 花絲，產生花粉 |
| 雌蕊 | pistil | 柱頭 + 花柱 + 子房，子房內有胚珠 |

### 台灣諺語
- 「好花毋驚晚開」（台語：Ho hue m kiann ban khui）——好的花不怕晚開，準備充分比急於綻放更重要
- 「十年樹木，百年樹人」——培養需要時間與耐心
