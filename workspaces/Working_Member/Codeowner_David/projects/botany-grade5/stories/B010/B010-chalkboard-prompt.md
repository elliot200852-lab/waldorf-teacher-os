# B010 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述

畫面以深色（深藍至黑）黑板為底。中央偏右，一株台灣百合從大地中升起，白色花冠向上方的太陽敞開，花瓣外側隱約可見紫紅色條紋。花的形態呈現六角星形的幾何感，呼應「星星教花朵形狀」的意象。

左上方，一輪金色太陽散發柔和光芒，光線如絲帶般向下延伸至花朵。右上方天空中，幾顆星星以六角、五角形態微微發光，與花朵的形狀遙相呼應——天上的花與地上的花互為鏡像。

畫面下方是大地，以深褐色與暗綠色表現土壤與草地，台灣百合的根系隱約可見。整體色調溫暖而莊嚴，帶有夢幻感。構圖左右不對稱，留有呼吸空間。

## English Prompt

Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a dark blackboard background. Chalk-like textures with soft, blended colors throughout. Landscape format.

Center-right: a single Taiwan lily (Lilium formosanum) rising from the earth, its white trumpet-shaped bloom opening upward toward a golden sun. The outer petals show subtle purple-red streaks. The flower's six petals form a visible six-pointed star shape, emphasizing the geometric star-pattern in its form.

Upper left: a warm golden sun radiating soft light, with luminous rays streaming downward like gentle ribbons reaching toward the flower. The light feels alive and teaching, not harsh.

Upper right sky area: several small stars glowing faintly in six-pointed and five-pointed shapes, echoing the flower's geometry below. The stars and the flower mirror each other — heavenly flowers above, earthly flowers below.

Lower portion: dark earth in deep browns and muted greens, suggesting soil and grass. The lily's roots are faintly visible beneath the surface, connecting flower to ground.

Overall mood: warm, reverent, dreamy, luminous. The composition is asymmetrical with breathing space. Colors are chalky and soft-edged — white, gold, gentle purple-red, deep blue-green, earth brown. No hard outlines. Everything glows softly as if lit from within. Educational and contemplative, suitable for a Grade 5 classroom.
