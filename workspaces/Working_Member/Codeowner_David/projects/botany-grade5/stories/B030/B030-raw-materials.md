# B030 蜜蜂的精神 — 素材包

## Kovacs 教學核心（英文摘要）

Kovacs closes his 30-lesson Botany course with a profound meditation on the "spirit of the bee" — the invisible intelligence that governs the hive and what it reveals about human consciousness.

**The Group Mind:** Kovacs begins by asking the central question: who directs forty thousand bees? Not the queen (she only lays eggs), not any individual worker (a single bee is not very clever). He introduces the concept of a "higher mind" that hovers over the entire hive, guiding each bee to the right task at the right time. This is not located *in* any bee but *above* them all — an overarching intelligence that makes the superorganism function.

**Human Spirit vs. Bee Spirit:** Kovacs draws a crucial contrast. In the morning verse, children say "we to spirit give a dwelling in our soul." Unlike bees, each human carries their own spirit *within*. We are more conscious than bees, more free — but we can learn from bees to cooperate without envy, jealousy, or greed. This is a masterful moral teaching: the lesson emerges from the comparison, not from preaching.

**Royal Jelly and Caste Determination:** From identical eggs, three types of bee emerge based solely on diet. Larvae fed exclusively on royal jelly (a rich milky fluid secreted from glands in the nurse bees' heads) become queens. Larvae fed royal jelly for three days then switched to "bees' bread" (a mixture of pollen and honey) become workers. Larvae fed mostly bees' bread with minimal royal jelly become drones. Same genetic starting point, radically different outcomes — determined by nourishment.

**The Fate of the Drone:** Kovacs draws a parallel between drones and pollen/stamens in a flower. After pollination, petals and stamens fall away — only the ovary remains to become fruit. Similarly, drones serve their reproductive purpose and then die in autumn, when the guiding mind tells the workers to stop feeding them. The drones die "for the good of the hive" — the stored honey would not sustain workers, larvae, *and* drones through winter.

**Honey and the Beekeeper:** Bees produce more honey than they need. A responsible beekeeper takes only the surplus. If all honey is removed, the beekeeper must substitute sugar water — inferior but survivable. This is an implicit lesson about sustainable harvesting: take what is extra, not what is essential.

**The Greek Reverence — Melitta:** Kovacs ends with the ancient Greek image of the soul as a honeybee. The hive is the bee's home; the kingdom of heaven is the soul's true home. We fly out into life to gather experience (nectar), and return enriched — then fly out again for new experiences. In certain Greek temples, holy priestesses were called *melitta* (honey-bee): a soul that returns to heaven laden with wisdom, as the bee returns laden with nectar. This closing image elevates the entire botany course from natural science to spiritual philosophy, appropriate for the developmental stage of ten-to-eleven-year-olds.

## 台灣植物資料

### 玉山植物帶垂直分布

台灣因南北跨度小但海拔落差大（海平面至 3,952 公尺），在極短距離內呈現完整的植被帶垂直分布：

| 海拔帶 | 主要植群 | 代表物種 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 0-500m | 熱帶常綠闊葉林 | 榕樹（*Ficus*）、茄冬、血桐 |
| 500-1,500m | 暖溫帶闊葉林 | 樟樹（*Cinnamomum camphora*）、楠木、台灣欒樹 |
| 1,500-2,500m | 涼溫帶針闊葉混合林/霧林帶 | 紅檜、扁柏、櫟類（*Quercus*） |
| 2,500-3,100m | 冷溫帶針葉林 | 鐵杉（*Tsuga chinensis*）、雲杉、台灣冷杉 |
| 3,100-3,600m | 亞高山針葉林/灌叢 | 玉山圓柏（*Juniperus squamata*）、玉山杜鵑 |
| 3,600m 以上 | 高山草原/碎石地衣帶 | 玉山薄雪草、高山地衣 |

玉山國家公園面積約 105,490 公頃，約佔國土面積 3%，卻擁有約 50% 台灣原生植物種類。

### 國家公園與森林保育

- **1985 年**：玉山國家公園、太魯閣國家公園成立
- **1992 年**：雪霸國家公園成立
- **全國森林覆蓋率**：約 60.71%（2020 年數據），為全球森林覆蓋率最高的國家之一
- **檜木伐採史**：日治時期（1912-1945）阿里山、太平山、八仙山三大林場大規模伐採紅檜與扁柏；戰後持續至 1991 年全面禁伐天然林

## 台灣文化連結

### 台灣養蜂與蜜蜂保育

台灣的養蜂歷史可追溯至清領時期。連橫《臺灣通史》卷二十八記載：「有蜜蜂，人家畜以取蜜。」台灣原生的東方蜜蜂（*Apis cerana*）分布於全島低至中海拔山區，日治時期引進西方蜜蜂（*Apis mellifera*）後，商業養蜂以西方蜜蜂為主，但山區野蜂仍以本土蜂為主。

東方蜜蜂的特色：
- 體型較西方蜜蜂小（體長約 10-13mm vs. 12-15mm）
- 飛行半徑較短（約 500 公尺 vs. 數公里）
- 更耐潮濕、適應台灣多雨山區
- 善於採集小型花朵的花蜜（如茶花、龍眼花）
- 具備「熱殺球」防禦虎頭蜂的集體行為

宜蘭社區大學曾開設養蜂課程，培訓在地小農以友善方式養殖本土蜂，國家文化記憶庫收錄了相關影像紀錄。

### 從砍伐到保育的省思

台灣的森林保育歷程是一部從「取用」到「歸還」的故事：日治時期的三大林場伐盡千年檜木，戰後林務局持續砍伐至 1991 年。轉折點包括：
- 1985 年國家公園法實施，玉山、太魯閣設立國家公園
- 1991 年全面禁伐天然林
- 2000 年代「國土復育」政策，啟動崩塌地造林與棲地復育
- 2010 年代推動「里山倡議」，鼓勵社區與自然共存

## 台灣俗諺

1. **「食蜜罵蜂母」**——吃了蜂蜜卻罵蜂后。比喻忘恩負義，享受了別人的勞動成果卻反過來嫌棄。台語俗諺。

2. **「有花就有蜂，有樹就有鳥」**——自然界裡，花與蜂、樹與鳥是天然的搭檔，不需要人去安排。呼應蜜蜂與花的共生關係。

3. **「有心栽花花不開，無心插柳柳成蔭」**——刻意去做的事未必成功，順其自然反而開花結果。呼應植物界的運作法則：花不是為了美麗而美麗，它只是做了它該做的事。
