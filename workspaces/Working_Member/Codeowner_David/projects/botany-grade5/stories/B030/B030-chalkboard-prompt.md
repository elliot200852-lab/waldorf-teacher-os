# 黑板畫 Prompt：B030 蜜蜂的精神

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Please generate an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing.

Black background. Soft chalk-like texture throughout. This is a grand panoramic composition representing the entire journey of the plant kingdom — from the depths of the earth to the summit of the mountain, with bees flying through it all.

The composition sweeps from left to right and bottom to top, like a mountain rising:

At the bottom left, in deep indigo and dark green chalk tones, depict the underground world — pale mushrooms and fungi growing in the darkness, their caps glowing faintly with soft cream and ochre chalk. Mosses and ferns emerge just above, rendered in deep forest green with delicate frond patterns.

Rising upward in the middle section, depict a progression of trees: broad-leafed subtropical trees in rich emerald green at the lower elevations, transitioning to conifers — tall spruces and hemlocks in blue-green and sage chalk tones at mid-elevation. Higher still, the trees become sparser, until at the upper reaches only the wind-bent alpine junipers cling to grey-violet chalk-rendered rocks.

At the very summit, rendered in soft silver and pale blue chalk, the mountain peak rises into a luminous golden sky. Between the peak and the sky, scatter tiny lichen forms in pale yellow-green.

Throughout the entire scene — from the flowering meadows in the middle ground to the blossoming trees — golden bees fly in graceful arcing paths, drawn as small glowing forms with translucent wings in warm amber and gold chalk. Their flight paths trace gentle curves connecting different layers of the plant kingdom, as if weaving an invisible thread between the flowers and the hive.

On the right side, nestled among flowering branches, suggest the form of a beehive — warm honey-gold tones glowing from within, bees arriving and departing.

The overall mood is of completion and reverence — the entire plant kingdom gathered in one vista, with the bees as the golden thread connecting it all. The light increases from bottom (deep darkness) to top (radiant gold), echoing the journey from fungus to flower, from darkness to light.

No text. No cartoon elements. Painterly, atmospheric, reverent.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, soft edges, glowing inner light, not photorealistic.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。

黑色背景，全圖呈現柔和的粉筆質感。這是一幅宏大的全景構圖，代表整個植物王國的旅程——從大地深處到山頂，蜜蜂飛翔其間。

構圖從左下到右上如山勢升起：

左下方以深靛藍和暗綠粉筆描繪地下世界——蒼白的蘑菇和真菌在黑暗中生長，菌傘以柔和的奶油色和赭色微微發光。苔蘚和蕨類在稍高處冒出，以深森林綠粉筆描繪精緻的蕨葉圖案。

中段向上攀升，呈現樹木的演替：低海拔的闊葉亞熱帶樹木以濃翠綠描繪，漸次過渡到針葉樹——藍綠和鼠尾草色調的雲杉與鐵杉佇立在中海拔。更高處樹木漸疏，直到最上方只剩被風吹彎的高山圓柏攀附在灰紫色粉筆繪成的岩石上。

最頂端以柔銀和淡藍粉筆描繪山峰，升入金色天空。峰頂與天空之間散布著淡黃綠色的地衣。

整個畫面中——從中景的花草地到盛開的樹木之間——金色蜜蜂以優雅弧線飛翔，畫成溫暖琥珀色和金色的小小發光體，翅膀透明。它們的飛行路徑在植物王國的不同層次之間劃出柔和曲線，彷彿編織一條看不見的線，連接花朵與蜂巢。

右側花枝之間隱約可見蜂巢的輪廓——溫暖的蜂蜜金色從內部發光，蜜蜂進出其間。

整體氛圍是完成與崇敬——整個植物王國匯聚在一幅圖中，蜜蜂是連接一切的金色絲線。光線從底部（深沉黑暗）向頂部（燦爛金色）遞增，呼應了從真菌到花朵、從黑暗到光明的旅程。

無文字。無卡通元素。繪畫性、有氛圍感、帶有敬意。
