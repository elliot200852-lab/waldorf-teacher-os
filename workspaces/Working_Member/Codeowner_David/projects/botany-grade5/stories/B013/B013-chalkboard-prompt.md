# B013 花粉 — 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述

畫面中央是一朵盛開的花，剖面呈現內部結構：底部圓潤的子房如同一顆小小的地球，雌蕊從中央向上伸展，周圍環繞著頂端帶有金色花粉囊的雄蕊。金色花粉粒從雄蕊飄散而出，在空中形成一道光弧。畫面右側，一隻毛茸茸的蜜蜂正飛向花朵，身上沾滿了金色花粉。左上方有一輪溫暖的太陽，光線向下灑落，暗示花粉是太陽賜予的禮物。畫面下方，一顆子房正在膨脹變大，漸漸轉化為一顆龍眼果實的輪廓，呈現授粉後的生命歷程。整體氛圍溫暖、充滿生命力。

## English Prompt

Waldorf chalkboard drawing, chalk on dark green blackboard background. Soft, luminous pastel chalk style with gentle blending and dreamy atmosphere.

Center composition: a large flower shown in partial cross-section, revealing the round ovary at its base drawn like a small glowing earth or orb. The pistil rises from the center, surrounded by several stamens crowned with golden-yellow pollen sacs. Fine golden pollen grains drift upward and outward from the stamens in a gentle arc of sparkling dust, catching rays of light from above.

Right side: a fuzzy honeybee in flight approaching the flower, its body dusted with bright golden pollen. Delicate translucent wings. The bee is rendered with warm amber and soft brown chalk tones.

Upper left: a warm sun radiating soft golden-orange light downward toward the flower, with gentle rays suggesting the sun's blessing flowing into the pollen.

Lower portion: a subtle transformation sequence — a small ovary gradually swelling and morphing into the round shape of a longan fruit, showing the journey from pollination to fruit.

Background: deep dark green-black chalkboard. Scattered tiny pollen grains float through the composition like golden stars. Occasional fine green leaves frame the edges.

Color palette: dominant golden-yellow for pollen and sunlight, warm amber for the bee, soft greens for stems and leaves, gentle rose-pink and white for flower petals, warm earth tones for the ovary-to-fruit transformation. All rendered in the characteristic soft, glowing Waldorf chalk style with visible chalk texture and gentle gradients.
