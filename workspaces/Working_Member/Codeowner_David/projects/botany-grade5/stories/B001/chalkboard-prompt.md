# B001 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：Botany-B001-chalkboard.png

請你生成一張圖片：一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，主題是「植物在太陽與大地之間」。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a deep dark green board. The composition is vertical and centered, divided into three luminous zones.

At the top: a radiant sun rendered in warm golden-yellow and soft orange chalk strokes, with rays fanning outward in gentle arcs — not geometric lines, but living, breathing curves of light. The glow gradually transitions downward into a pale warm sky.

In the center: a single plant grows upward along the vertical axis. The stem is drawn in layered greens — olive, emerald, sage — with visible chalk texture and soft blending. Two or three pairs of simple leaves extend from the stem, gently curving outward like open palms. At the top of the stem, a flower opens toward the sun: its petals are soft rose, peach, and touches of pale gold, drawn with delicate overlapping strokes. The flower is stylized and luminous, not botanically literal, but clearly showing petals radiating from a center.

Below the ground line: the roots extend downward into rich darkness — deep browns, purples, and blue-blacks. The roots branch and taper like quiet rivers flowing into the earth. Small dots of pale yellow are scattered among the roots, suggesting dormant seeds glowing faintly like buried stars.

The background behind the plant subtly suggests the silhouette of a tall mountain (evoking Yushan / Jade Mountain) in muted blue-grey chalk, barely visible, as if emerging from mist on one side of the composition.

Overall style: soft chalk pastels on a dark surface, with visible grain and texture. No hard outlines — all forms emerge from layered color and light. The mood is quiet, reverent, and alive. The drawing should feel hand-made, as if drawn by a teacher on a real classroom blackboard. No text, no labels, no borders.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，繪於深綠色黑板上。構圖為垂直置中，分為三個光域。

上方：一輪太陽以溫暖的金黃色和柔和的橙色粉筆筆觸繪成，光芒以輕柔的弧線向外展開——不是幾何直線，而是有生命力的、呼吸著的光之曲線。光暈向下漸漸過渡為淡暖色天空。

中央：一株植物沿著垂直軸向上生長。莖以層疊的綠色繪成——橄欖綠、翡翠綠、鼠尾草綠——帶有可見的粉筆質感與柔和暈染。兩到三對簡單的葉片從莖上伸出，輕輕向外彎曲，如張開的手掌。莖頂端一朵花朝向太陽綻開：花瓣為柔和的玫瑰色、桃色與淡金色，以細膩的重疊筆觸繪成。花朵經過風格化處理，發出柔光，不求植物學上的寫實，但清晰呈現花瓣從中心向外輻射的結構。

地平線以下：根向下延伸進入深邃的暗色——深褐、紫色與藍黑色。根如靜默的河流般分岔、收細，流入大地。根系之間散布著淡黃色的小點，暗示休眠中的種子微微發光，如埋藏在土裡的星星。

植物背後的背景隱約呈現一座高山的剪影（暗示玉山），以淡藍灰色粉筆繪成，若隱若現，彷彿從構圖一側的霧氣中浮現。

整體風格：深色底面上的柔和粉筆色粉，可見顆粒與質感。沒有硬邊輪廓——所有形體都從層疊的色彩與光中浮現。氛圍安靜、崇敬、充滿生命力。畫面應呈現手繪質感，如同教師在真實教室黑板上繪製。無文字、無標籤、無邊框。
