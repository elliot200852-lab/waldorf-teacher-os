# B001 素材包

## Kovacs 原文核心段落（Ch.1: The Plant Between Sun and Earth）

### 種子如繁星
"Imagine all these millions and millions of seeds held inside the earth during the cold season ... imagine for a moment that each seed is a tiny light, and if you can imagine that we could see through the earth, it would look as though there were millions of stars."

### 思考的火花
"If you struggle to understand something and suddenly you grasp it, you feel 'I've got it!' it is like a sudden spark of light. This is thinking, real thinking, when you feel such a spark of light within yourself."

### 清醒與睡眠的類比
"When we are asleep - we are like the earth in summer. When we are awake - we are like the earth in winter."

### 植物的雙向生長
"One part - the stem, the green leaves, the blossom - grows towards the light ... But the other part of the plant, the root, grows deeper and deeper into the darkness, into the earth."

### 太陽與大地的孩子
"The plant needs both the light and warmth of the sun and the darkness of the earth ... the plant is a child of sun and earth. Just as human children have fathers and mothers, so the plants have the earth as mother and the sun as father."

## 台灣植物對應資料

### 玉山六大植群帶
1. 榕楠林帶（500m 以下）：榕樹、楠木
2. 楠櫧林帶（500-1,500m）：櫧木、楠木
3. 櫟林帶（1,500-2,500m）：殼斗科
4. 鐵杉雲杉林帶（2,500-3,100m）：臺灣鐵杉、臺灣雲杉
5. 冷杉林帶（3,100-3,600m）：臺灣冷杉
6. 高山植群帶（3,600m+）：玉山圓柏、玉山杜鵑

### 關鍵事實
- 玉山國家公園面積佔台灣 3%，卻記錄了 50% 原生植物（2,522 種）
- 玉山圓柏在迎風面匍匐生長，背風面直立
- 台灣從海平面到近 4,000m，不到 100km 水平距離壓縮完整植被帶

### 台灣俗諺
- 「樹頭徛予在，毋驚樹尾做風颱」（根穩則不怕風）
- 「一枝草，一點露」（每棵草都有露水滋養）
