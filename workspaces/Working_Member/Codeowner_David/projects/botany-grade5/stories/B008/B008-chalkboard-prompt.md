# B008 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：Botany-B008-chalkboard.png

請你生成一張圖片：黑板粉筆風格，華德福教育美學，阿里山台灣紅檜神木的雄偉姿態，一棵巨大的古樹佔據畫面中央，層層分枝如「一樓一樓往上」，前景站著一個渺小的人，遠景是霧氣與山脈，深色黑板背景，白色與淡褐色粉筆手繪線條，傳達時間的深邃與生命的偉大。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration rendered in chalk on a deep black blackboard. The composition centers on a single enormous ancient Taiwan red cypress (Chamaecyparis formosensis, also called Alishan Shenmu), its massive reddish-brown trunk rising from the base of the image toward the top, filling the vertical space with quiet authority. The trunk is deeply furrowed with long vertical ridges, rendered in warm ochre and pale brown chalk lines. Branches extend horizontally in distinct tiers — the first tier low and wide, the second higher and slightly shorter, the third even higher, each tier smaller than the last, like floors of a living tower, receding gently into soft mist. The foliage is suggested with loose, feathery chalk strokes in pale sage green and white, not detailed but atmospheric. In the lower left foreground, a single small human figure stands looking up, rendered in simple white chalk lines — just a few strokes: head, shoulders, a person made of light. The contrast of the tiny figure and the ancient tree makes the scale visceral. The background fades into layered mountain ridges in soft gray-blue, half-dissolved in fog. A gentle mist drifts across the middle distance. The overall palette is black background with white, warm ochre, pale sage, and soft gray-brown chalk. The mood is ancient, reverent, and alive. The style is organic and hand-drawn, never rigid — the chalk lines breathe. This is a Waldorf classroom image: it should evoke wonder, not scientific documentation.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板粉筆插圖，繪製於深黑色黑板背景上。構圖中央是一棵單獨的巨大台灣紅檜（阿里山神木），厚實的紅褐色樹幹從畫面底部向上伸展，以安靜的威嚴佔據垂直空間。樹幹布滿深長的縱向紋路，以溫暖的赭黃色與淡褐色粉筆線條表現。枝條水平延展，分成明顯的幾層——第一層低而寬闊，第二層略高、略短，第三層更高，每一層都比下一層小，如同一座活著的塔樓，層層向上消融於霧氣之中。葉片以鬆散羽毛狀的粉筆筆觸暗示，淡鼠尾草綠與白色，不求細膩，只求氛圍。畫面左下前景，站著一個渺小的人影，仰頭望向大樹，以簡單的白色粉筆線條勾出——幾筆頭、肩、身體，是一個由光線組成的人。微小人影與古老大樹的對比，讓比例感透過皮膚傳遞。背景化為層疊的山脈輪廓，以柔和的灰藍色呈現，半溶於霧氣之中。一縷輕霧橫貫中景。整體色調為黑色背景搭配白色、暖赭色、淡鼠尾草綠與柔灰褐粉筆。氛圍：古老、莊嚴、充滿生命力。風格有機而手繪，從不僵硬——粉筆線條本身在呼吸。這是一張華德福教室用圖：它要喚起敬畏，而不是科學記錄。
