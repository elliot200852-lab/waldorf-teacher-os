# 黑板畫 Prompt：B025 茶、糖與咖啡

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing. Black background. Soft chalk-like texture throughout.

The composition is divided into three gentle zones flowing from left to right, unified by a warm golden light that arcs across the upper portion of the image like a sunrise.

On the left: a terraced hillside of tea bushes rendered in soft sage-green and moss-green chalk strokes, with gentle undulating rows. A few small white-pink blossoms dot the tips of the shrubs. Faint misty clouds drift across the hillside, suggesting high mountain altitude. The mood here is quiet, meditative.

In the center: tall sugar cane stalks rise dramatically upward, drawn in warm yellow-green and golden-ochre chalk. The stalks are dense, close together, reaching toward the light — they should convey height and abundance. At the base, suggest rich dark earth in deep brown tones.

On the right: a small coffee tree with dark green glossy leaves and clusters of bright red coffee cherries, rendered in deep green with touches of crimson and vermillion chalk. The tree is smaller than the cane, more intimate in scale.

Connecting all three sections at the bottom: a faint, luminous golden thread or path, suggesting that these three plants are linked across the world. Above, the warm amber-gold light source radiates softly, blessing all three plants.

No text. No cartoon elements. No photorealism. The style should evoke layered chalk on a blackboard — soft edges, glowing inner light, atmospheric depth, and a sense of reverence for the living world.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, painterly, luminous.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。黑色背景，全圖呈現柔和的粉筆質感。

構圖分為三個溫和的區域，從左到右流動，由一道溫暖的金色光線統一連貫，光線像日出一樣在圖片上方劃出弧形。

左側：階梯狀的茶園山坡，以柔和的鼠尾草綠和苔綠色粉筆筆觸描繪，一排排輕柔起伏的茶樹。幾朵白粉色的小花點綴在茶樹頂端。淡淡的雲霧飄過山坡，暗示高山的海拔。這裡的氛圍安靜、冥想般。

中央：高聳的甘蔗莖劇性地向上伸展，以溫暖的黃綠色和金赭色粉筆描繪。甘蔗莖密集、緊靠，朝向光源生長——要傳達高度和豐饒的感覺。底部暗示以深棕色調呈現肥沃的深色土壤。

右側：一棵小咖啡樹，深綠色的光澤葉片和一串串鮮紅的咖啡漿果，以深綠色為主，搭配朱紅和朱砂色粉筆的點綴。咖啡樹比甘蔗小，比例上更親密。

底部連結三個區域的是一條隱約的、發光的金色線條或小徑，暗示這三種植物跨越世界彼此相連。上方，溫暖的琥珀金色光源柔和地散發光芒，祝福著這三種植物。

無文字。無卡通元素。非寫實。風格應喚起黑板上層層堆疊的粉筆——柔和的邊緣、發光的內在光芒、有氛圍的深度，以及對生命世界的敬意。
