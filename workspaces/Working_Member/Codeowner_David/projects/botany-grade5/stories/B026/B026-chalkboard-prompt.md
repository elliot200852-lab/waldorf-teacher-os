# 黑板畫 Prompt：B026 草與穀物

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Create a Waldorf chalkboard drawing on a pure black background. Soft, luminous chalk texture throughout. The composition is a golden field of ripe grain — rice or wheat — occupying the lower two-thirds of the image. Each stalk is drawn with fine, upward-reaching yellow-gold and warm amber chalk strokes, standing erect, slender, and unbranched. The grain heads bow gently under their own weight, heavy with seeds. The stalks rise from dark earth rendered in deep brown and umber tones at the very bottom.

Above the field, the sky transitions from warm golden light near the horizon to deep indigo-blue at the top. A soft, diffuse sun glow — pale gold and white — sits low on the horizon, backlighting the grain stalks so they appear to glow from within. A few subtle wind lines suggest a breeze moving through the field, creating the sense of a wave passing through the grain.

In the foreground, one single stalk stands slightly taller and more distinct than the others — perfectly upright, its narrow leaf wrapped tightly around the stem like a sheath, embodying the grass family's pure vertical aspiration.

No text. No cartoon elements. Painterly, atmospheric, reverent. The mood is one of quiet abundance and hidden strength — the immense power concentrated in tiny grains.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, soft edges, glowing inner light, not photorealistic.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。純黑背景，全圖呈現柔和、發光的粉筆質感。構圖是一片金黃色的成熟穀田——稻穗或麥穗——佔據畫面下方三分之二。每一根莖稈以纖細的、向上伸展的金黃色與琥珀色粉筆筆觸描繪，直立、纖細、不分枝。穗頭在自身重量下微微彎垂，穀粒飽滿。莖稈從最底端以深棕色和赭色調描繪的黑土中升起。

穀田上方，天空從地平線附近的溫暖金光過渡到頂部的深靛藍色。一團柔和、漫散的太陽光暈——淡金色和白色——低懸在地平線上，逆光照射穀稈，使它們彷彿從內部發光。幾道微妙的風紋暗示微風吹過田間，形成波浪般穿過穀田的動感。

前景中，一根莖稈比其他的稍高、稍清晰——完全筆直，窄葉緊緊捲繞著莖，像鞘一樣，體現禾本科植物純粹的垂直志向。

無文字。無卡通元素。繪畫性、有氛圍感、帶有敬意。整體氛圍是安靜的豐饒與隱藏的力量——巨大的力量集中在小小穀粒之中。
