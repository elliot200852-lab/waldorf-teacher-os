# B021 蕁麻——黑板畫 Prompt

請你生成一張圖片。

畫面中央是一株蕁麻植株，從黑色背景中生長而出。蕁麻的莖幹直立，葉子兩兩對生，每一對與上下一對呈十字交叉排列，葉緣有清晰的鋸齒。以淡綠色粉筆描繪葉片，以更深的綠色勾勒葉脈。在靠近莖頂的位置，用極淡的紫色畫出細小的穗狀花序。

畫面左下角，以放大的方式呈現一根焮毛的構造：一根細長的中空刺毛，底部膨大如球，頂端尖銳如針，用白色和淺黃色粉筆繪製，讓人聯想到玻璃針管。

畫面右上方，一隻紅蛺蝶正從蕁麻旁飛過，翅膀張開，以溫暖的紅色和橘色粉筆繪製，與蕁麻的綠色形成對比——這是蕁麻養育的「會飛的花朵」。

整體風格為華德福黑板畫：黑色背景、粉筆質感、柔和的色彩漸層、不含任何文字或標籤。

## English Prompt

Generate an image. A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing on a solid black background with soft chalk textures. In the center, an upright nettle plant (Urtica) with pairs of opposite leaves arranged in a decussate pattern (each pair crossing the one below at right angles). Leaves are rendered in pale green chalk with deeper green veins and finely serrated edges. Near the stem tip, tiny pale-purple flower spikes. In the lower-left corner, an enlarged detail of a single stinging hair (trichome): a hollow, tapering needle-like cell with a bulbous base, drawn in white and pale-yellow chalk, evoking a glass hypodermic needle. In the upper-right area, a Red Admiral butterfly in flight with wings spread, rendered in warm red and orange chalk, contrasting with the green nettle. No text, no labels, no human figures. Soft color gradients throughout, faithful to Waldorf chalkboard drawing aesthetics.
