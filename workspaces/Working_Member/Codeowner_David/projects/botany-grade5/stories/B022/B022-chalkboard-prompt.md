# 黑板畫 Prompt：B022 橡樹

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

Please generate an image in Waldorf chalkboard drawing style for a Grade 5 botany lesson about the oak family.

Black background representing a chalkboard surface. Chalk-texture strokes throughout.

Center composition: a mature Taiwan ring-cupped oak (Quercus glauca / Cyclobalanopsis glauca) with a broad, rounded canopy. The trunk is thick and deeply furrowed, rendered in warm ochre and brown chalk. The deep green leaves catch warm amber-golden light from the upper right. Leaves are oval with serrated upper edges, shown in layered strokes of deep green and sage.

Lower foreground: scattered acorns with their distinctive bowl-shaped cupules (殼斗), rendered in golden brown. One acorn is sprouting — a pale white root curling downward into dark soil, and a single folded green leaf emerging upward. A small squirrel (Taiwan red-bellied squirrel) is burying one acorn in soft brown earth at the base of the tree.

Upper left corner: ghostly outline of acorns at different life stages — from green unripe to mature brown, showing the quiet passing of seasons.

Atmosphere: warm, still, patient. The mood is one of slow, deep strength — a tree that does not hurry. Soft chalk haze blurs the canopy edges into the dark background. No text. No borders.

## 中文翻譯

黑色背景模擬黑板質感，全圖以粉筆筆觸繪製。

構圖中心：一棵成熟的台灣青剛櫟（殼斗科），樹冠寬闊圓潤。樹幹粗壯、有深刻紋路，以暖赭色與褐色粉筆呈現。深綠色的葉片在右上方的暖金光線下閃著光澤，葉片橢圓、上半部有鋸齒，以深綠和鼠尾草綠分層疊筆。

前景下方：散落的橡實，帶著特徵性的碗形殼斗，以金褐色繪製。一顆橡實正在發芽——蒼白的細根向下捲入深色土壤，一片摺疊的嫩綠葉片向上推開土層。一隻台灣赤腹松鼠在樹根旁的鬆軟土中埋藏橡實。

左上角：橡實不同成長階段的輪廓——從青綠未熟到成熟褐色，安靜訴說著季節的流轉。

氛圍：溫暖、靜止、有耐心。那是一棵不急的樹的氣息。樹冠邊緣以柔和的粉筆暈染融入深色背景。無文字，無邊框。
