# B007 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：Botany-B007-chalkboard.png

請你生成一張圖片：黑板粉筆風格，華德福教育美學，一株筆筒樹蕨的嫩芽正從土壤中伸出，呈現問號形（fiddle head）的捲曲姿態，褐色轉黃綠色，背景是台灣低山丘陵的霧氣與遠方站立的筆筒樹高大身影，傳達「第一次說我」的覺醒意象，深褐→黃綠→深綠的色彩過渡，黑板黑色背景，粉筆手繪質感。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration rendered in chalk on a dark blackboard background. The focal point is a single young fern fiddlehead — a tightly curled, brownish-green spiral — emerging from the dark earth, its tip pointing upward like a question mark. The spiral is just beginning to uncurl at the top, revealing the first glimpse of fresh yellow-green. Around the base, the dark moist soil is visible with fine root threads. In the soft-focus background, several tall tree ferns (Cyathea lepifera) rise in the misty air of a Taiwan hill forest — their massive fronds spread wide at the top, their straight trunks marked with circular leaf scars. A gentle atmospheric mist dissolves the background into soft grays and greens. The color progression moves from deep earth brown at the base, through warm golden-green in the unfurling coil, to deep forest green in the distant fronds. The entire composition evokes the feeling of a child saying "I" for the first time — a quiet, profound awakening. Chalk lines are organic and flowing, never rigid. Mood: contemplative, ancient, alive.

## 中文翻譯

一幅華德福風格的黑板粉筆插圖，繪製於深色黑板背景上。焦點是一株幼嫩的蕨類嫩芽（fiddlehead）——一個緊緊捲曲的褐綠色螺旋，從深暗的土壤中伸出，頂端朝上如一個問號。螺旋頂部剛開始展開，隱約可見第一抹新鮮的黃綠色。基部周圍可見濕潤的深色土壤與細絲狀根。柔焦背景中，幾棵高大的筆筒樹在台灣丘陵的霧氣中直立——巨大的羽狀葉片在頂端展開，直立的樹幹上清晰可見圓環狀葉痕。輕柔的大氣霧靄將背景化為柔和的灰色與綠色。色彩由基部的深大地棕，過渡到捲曲螺旋的溫暖金綠，再到遠方葉片的深森林綠。整個構圖喚起孩子第一次說出「我」的感受——安靜、深刻、充滿生命力。粉筆線條有機流動，從不僵硬。氛圍：沉思、古老、充滿生命力。
