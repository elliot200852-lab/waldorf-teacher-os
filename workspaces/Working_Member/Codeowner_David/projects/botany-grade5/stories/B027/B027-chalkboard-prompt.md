# 黑板畫 Prompt：B027 葉與花

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Please generate an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing.

Black background. Soft chalk-like texture throughout. The central subject is a Taiwan Rain Tree (Koelreuteria elegans) depicted in its full four-season colour cycle, arranged as a single majestic tree that transitions through its colours from left to right across the canopy.

On the left side of the crown, lush green bipinnate compound leaves rendered in deep emerald and sage chalk tones — the working season. Moving rightward, clusters of tiny golden-yellow flowers cascade downward like rain, drawn in warm cadmium yellow and pale gold chalk strokes. Further right, the canopy shows inflated lantern-shaped capsule fruits in vivid crimson and rose chalk — each little lantern glowing with inner warmth. At the far right edge, dried brown capsules in muted umber and raw sienna chalk tones, some drifting downward.

The trunk is strong, upright, with textured bark in warm grey-brown chalk. At the base, suggest a Taiwan streetscape or schoolyard setting with a faint chalk outline of a low wall or path.

Above the tree, a soft golden sun glow at the upper portion, transitioning to deep indigo-blue sky. The overall mood is of quiet transformation and the passage of time — one tree telling the story of an entire year.

No text. No cartoon elements. Painterly, atmospheric, reverent.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, soft edges, glowing inner light, not photorealistic.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。

黑色背景，全圖呈現柔和的粉筆質感。主角是一棵台灣欒樹，以四季色彩變化為主題，描繪為一棵雄偉的大樹，樹冠從左到右漸次呈現四種顏色。

左側樹冠是茂密的綠色二回羽狀複葉，以深翠綠和鼠尾草綠色粉筆描繪——那是工作的季節。往右移動，成簇的金黃色小花如雨般向下灑落，用溫暖的鎘黃和淡金色粉筆筆觸描繪。再往右，樹冠上出現膨脹的燈籠狀蒴果，以鮮豔的深紅和玫瑰色粉筆繪製——每個小燈籠散發著溫暖的內在光芒。最右側，乾枯的褐色蒴果以沉穩的赭色和生赭色粉筆描繪，部分正向下飄落。

樹幹粗壯直立，以溫暖的灰褐色粉筆呈現有紋理的樹皮。樹的底部隱約勾勒出台灣的街道或校園場景——一道低矮圍牆或小徑的淡淡輪廓。

樹的上方，柔和的金色陽光在畫面上部暈開，向上漸漸融入深靛藍色的天空。整體氛圍是安靜的轉變與時光的流逝——一棵樹述說著整整一年的故事。

無文字。無卡通元素。繪畫性、有氛圍感、帶有敬意。
