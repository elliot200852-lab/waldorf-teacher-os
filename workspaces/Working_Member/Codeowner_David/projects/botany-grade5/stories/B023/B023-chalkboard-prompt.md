# 黑板畫 Prompt：B023 樺樹

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

Please generate an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing.

Black background representing a school chalkboard. Soft chalk-texture style, slightly rough and dusty, as if drawn by hand with coloured chalk. No hard digital lines — all edges should be gentle and atmospheric.

The scene: a single slender Formosan Alder tree (Alnus formosana) standing alone on a grey, rocky landslide slope. The tree's trunk is pale grey-brown, thin and straight, rising quietly from rubble and broken stones. Small, fan-shaped leaves in soft green flutter gently at the tips of delicate branches. The tree is slightly off-centre, leaning very slightly into a faint wind.

Beneath the tree, barely visible but present: hints of tiny root nodules suggested by small rounded forms at the base of the trunk, glowing faintly warm — suggesting hidden life underground.

The background slope is grey-white rubble fading into shadow at the edges. Above the tree, the sky is deep indigo-black. A faint light source illuminates the tree from above-left, as if moonlight or early dawn is catching the white-grey trunk, making it glow against the dark.

Mood: quiet courage, solitude, the first living thing in a ruined landscape. Waldorf watercolour-chalk aesthetic. No text. No border. Landscape orientation.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖像。

黑色背景，模擬學校黑板。軟粉筆質感，邊緣柔和如手繪，不要銳利的數位線條。

畫面：一棵細長的台灣赤楊，單獨站在灰白色的崩塌礫石坡上。樹幹淡灰褐色，細而直，從碎石堆中靜靜升起。細枝頂端有柔和的嫩綠色小葉，在微風中輕輕飄動。樹稍偏離中心，微微向一側傾。

樹根下方，若隱若現地暗示著細小的根瘤——以幾個微小的圓形形態呈現，散發淡淡的暖光，暗示地下有生命在默默運作。

背景的礫石坡是灰白色，向邊緣漸漸淡入黑暗。樹上方是深靛藍色夜空。從左上方投射來一縷微光——如月光或黎明前的第一道光——照在灰白的樹幹上，讓它在黑暗中發光。

氛圍：安靜的勇氣、孤獨、廢墟中第一個生命。華德福水彩粉筆美學。無文字，無邊框，橫向構圖。
