# B018 玫瑰 — 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述

以華德福黑板畫風格（黑色背景、粉筆質感）描繪一朵盛開的野玫瑰。

核心構圖：
- 畫面中央偏上：一朵野玫瑰（五瓣），花瓣為深桃紅至緋紅色，層次分明
- 花心可見金黃色雄蕊
- 花朵下方：深綠色羽狀複葉（7片小葉排列在葉柄上），葉緣有鋸齒
- 莖上有小刺（褐色）
- 左下角可見木質化的根系延伸入黑暗的土壤中，根分叉再分叉，像一棵倒過來的小樹
- 右上角有淡金色的陽光暈染

色彩：以深桃紅、緋紅（花瓣）、深綠（葉）、褐色（莖與根）、金黃（花心與陽光）為主。黑色背景上的粉筆光暈效果。

風格：華德福黑板畫傳統——柔和的粉筆邊緣、不畫硬線條、色彩從深到淺自然暈開。整體氛圍莊嚴而溫暖，呈現玫瑰「花中之后」的尊貴感。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing (black background, chalk pastel texture) of a blooming wild rose.

Central composition: A single wild rose flower with exactly five petals in deep rose-pink to crimson, with visible golden-yellow stamens at the center. Below the flower: dark green compound leaves (7 leaflets arranged on a common stem) with serrated edges. Small thorns visible on the brownish woody stem.

In the lower left, the root system extends into dark soil, branching and re-branching like an upside-down tree — showing the rose's deep connection to the earth.

Upper right corner: a soft golden glow suggesting warm summer sunlight.

Color palette: deep rose-pink and crimson (petals), golden-yellow (stamens and light), dark green (leaves), brown (woody stems and roots). Soft chalk-glow edges against the black background.

Style: Traditional Waldorf chalkboard art — soft pastel edges, no hard outlines, colors blending naturally from dark to light. The overall mood is regal and warm, conveying the rose as the "queen of flowers." The drawing should feel reverent and alive, not botanical-diagram-like.

No text, no labels, no human figures. Pure botanical illustration in chalk.
