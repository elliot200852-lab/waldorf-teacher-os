# 黑板畫 Prompt：B028 蜜蜂

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Please generate an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing.

Black background. Soft chalk-like texture throughout. The scene depicts the miraculous moment of a bee swarm in flight, converging upon a tree branch.

At the centre-right of the composition, a sturdy tree branch extends horizontally. Clinging to it is a large, luminous cluster of bees — a swarm — rendered as a glowing golden-amber mass with soft, warm tones of ochre, cadmium yellow, and honey gold. Individual bees at the edges of the cluster are suggested with tiny wing strokes in pale cream chalk, giving the mass a living, breathing quality. The swarm hangs like a heavy golden fruit from the branch.

Surrounding the swarm, dozens of individual bees are still in flight, drawn as small elongated forms with translucent wings catching light — rendered in golden yellow and warm white chalk strokes against the dark sky. Their flight paths create gentle curving trails of soft chalk dust, suggesting movement and excitement.

In the lower-left corner, partially visible, the outline of a traditional wooden beehive (a Langstroth-style box) in warm brown and grey chalk, with a small entrance at the bottom from which a stream of golden bees pours upward and outward.

Above the scene, a warm golden sun glow at the upper-left, its rays rendered in soft radiating lines of pale yellow and cream. The sky transitions from warm golden-blue near the sun to deep indigo-blue at the edges.

At the bottom-right, suggest a single open flower — perhaps a longan blossom cluster — in soft cream-white chalk, with a bee landing upon it, connecting the bee world to the plant world.

No text. No cartoon elements. Painterly, atmospheric, reverent.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, soft edges, glowing inner light, not photorealistic.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。

黑色背景，全圖呈現柔和的粉筆質感。場景描繪蜜蜂分群的奇妙時刻——蜂群飛向一根樹枝聚集。

畫面中央偏右，一根粗壯的樹枝水平延伸。攀附在上面的是一大團發光的蜜蜂群——用赭石、鎘黃和蜂蜜金色的溫暖色調繪製成金琥珀色的發光團塊。蜂群邊緣的蜜蜂以淡奶油色粉筆的細小翅膀筆觸暗示，讓整團蜂群看起來活生生地在呼吸。蜂群像一顆沉甸甸的金色果實掛在樹枝上。

蜂群周圍，數十隻蜜蜂仍在飛行中，畫為帶有透明翅膀的小型橢圓體，翅膀上捕捉著光線——以金黃和暖白色粉筆筆觸繪於深色天空中。牠們的飛行軌跡形成柔和的弧形粉筆塵跡，暗示著動感與興奮。

左下角，部分可見的傳統木製蜂箱輪廓，以溫暖的褐色和灰色粉筆繪製，底部有一個小入口，一條金色的蜜蜂流從中向上湧出。

場景上方左側，柔和的金色陽光暈開，光線以淡黃和奶油色的放射線條呈現。天空從太陽附近的暖金藍色過渡到邊緣的深靛藍色。

右下角，暗示一簇開放的花朵——也許是龍眼花——以柔和的乳白色粉筆繪製，一隻蜜蜂正停落其上，將蜜蜂的世界與植物的世界連結在一起。

無文字。無卡通元素。繪畫性、有氛圍感、帶有敬意。
