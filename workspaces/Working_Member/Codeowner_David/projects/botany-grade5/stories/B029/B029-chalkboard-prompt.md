# 黑板畫 Prompt：B029 蜂巢

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

請你生成一張圖片

Please generate an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing.

Black background. Soft chalk-like texture throughout. The central subject is a cross-section view of a beehive showing the interior honeycomb structure.

In the centre, a large honeycomb panel rendered in warm golden-amber and honey-yellow chalk tones, showing the perfect hexagonal cells in careful geometric detail. Some cells are capped with pale wax (in creamy white chalk), some are open showing golden honey inside, and a few cells near the bottom contain tiny white larvae curled in C-shapes.

Several honeybees are depicted working on and around the honeycomb — some crawling across the surface tending to cells, one bee appearing to do the waggle dance with motion lines suggested by soft chalk strokes, and two or three bees in flight approaching the hive from the right side, carrying tiny pollen loads on their legs rendered in bright golden-yellow dots.

At the top of the composition, a subtle warm glow emanates from behind the honeycomb, suggesting the warmth maintained inside the hive. At the bottom, faint outlines of flowers — longan blossoms with small creamy-white petals in clusters — connect the hive to its floral source.

The overall mood is one of industrious harmony, warmth, and geometric perfection. The hexagonal pattern should feel both mathematically precise and organically alive.

No text. No cartoon elements. Painterly, atmospheric, reverent.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, soft edges, glowing inner light, not photorealistic.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。

黑色背景，全圖呈現柔和的粉筆質感。主題是蜂巢的橫切面，展示內部蜂巢結構。

畫面中央是一大片蜂巢板，以溫暖的金琥珀色和蜜黃色粉筆繪製，仔細描繪出完美的六角形巢室。部分巢室以奶白色粉筆封蓋蠟蓋，部分開口可見金色蜂蜜，底部幾個巢室中可見蜷曲成 C 形的白色小幼蟲。

數隻蜜蜂在蜂巢片上和周圍工作——有的爬行在表面照料巢室，一隻蜜蜂正在跳搖擺舞、以柔和粉筆筆觸暗示動態線條，右方有兩三隻蜜蜂飛來，腿上攜帶的花粉以亮金黃色小點表現。

構圖頂部，蜂巢後方散發出微妙的溫暖光芒，暗示蜂巢內部維持的恆溫。底部有淡淡的花朵輪廓——龍眼花的乳白色小花簇——將蜂巢與花蜜來源連結在一起。

整體氛圍是勤勉的和諧、溫暖與幾何之美。六角形圖案既有數學的精確感，又帶有有機生命的活力。

無文字。無卡通元素。繪畫性、有氛圍感、帶有敬意。
