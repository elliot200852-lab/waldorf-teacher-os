# B019 薔薇科延伸 — 黑板畫 Prompt

請你生成一張圖片。

黑色背景上，以柔和的粉筆質感繪製薔薇科家族的花與果比較圖。畫面中央偏上方是一朵盛開的玫瑰，花瓣層層疊疊，以粉紅色與深紅色粉筆描繪。從玫瑰向左右延伸出弧形的枝條，左側依序掛著一朵白色梅花和一串粉色桃花，右側依序是一朵白色蘋果花和一簇深桃紅色的山櫻花（鐘形下垂）。每朵花都清楚呈現五片花瓣的結構。畫面下方，對應每朵花的位置，分別繪有對應的果實：左側是青色的梅子和粉紅的桃子，右側是紅綠相間的蘋果和暗紅色的小櫻桃。中央下方是一顆小小的紅色薔薇果。所有果實以柔和的色粉筆描繪，有微微的光澤感。整體構圖呈現弧形的家族樹意象，花在上、果在下，展現薔薇科從花到果的多樣面貌。不含任何文字、標籤或人物。

## English Prompt

Generate an image. On a solid black background, draw a Waldorf-style chalkboard illustration of the Rose Family (Rosaceae) comparing flowers and fruits. At the top center, a layered pink-and-crimson rose in full bloom. Arching branches extend left and right: on the left, a white plum blossom and a cluster of pink peach blossoms; on the right, a white apple blossom and pendulous bell-shaped deep-pink cherry blossoms (Prunus campanulata). Each flower clearly shows five petals. Below each flower, the corresponding fruit: a green plum, a rosy peach, a red-green apple, small dark cherries, and a tiny red rosehip at center bottom. All rendered in soft chalk-pastel texture with subtle luminosity against the dark ground. The composition forms a gentle arch — flowers above, fruits below — evoking a family tree of Rosaceae diversity. No text, labels, or human figures.
