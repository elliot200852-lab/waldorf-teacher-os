# B020 高麗菜（十字花科）— 黑板畫 Prompt

請你生成一張圖片。

畫面中央是一顆高麗菜的剖面，從正中間切開，露出層層包裹的葉片結構——外層葉片大而翠綠，向內漸漸變淺，最內層幾乎是乳白色。葉片的弧線由外向內旋轉收攏，形成優美的螺旋紋理。

高麗菜的上方，畫一朵放大的十字花科花朵：四片花瓣均勻排列成十字形，花瓣為淡黃色，中央有細小的雄蕊。

畫面下方，左側畫一顆蘿蔔（白色長錐形，帶綠色葉冠），右側畫一顆花椰菜（白色花托，外圍綠葉），暗示十字花科家族的多樣性。

整體風格為華德福黑板畫：黑色背景、粉筆質感、色彩柔和但飽滿、線條溫潤有手繪感。不含任何文字、標籤、人物。

## English Prompt

Generate an image. A Waldorf chalkboard drawing on a solid black background with soft chalk texture. Center: a cross-section of a cabbage head, cut in half, revealing concentric layers of leaves — outer leaves in deep green chalk, gradually lightening to pale cream at the core, with graceful spiral curves. Above the cabbage: an enlarged four-petaled cruciform flower in soft yellow chalk, petals arranged in a clear cross shape with delicate stamens at the center. Lower left: a white daikon radish with green leaf crown. Lower right: a cauliflower head with white florets and surrounding green leaves. No text, no labels, no human figures. Warm, hand-drawn chalk aesthetic with rich but gentle colors blending into the black background.
