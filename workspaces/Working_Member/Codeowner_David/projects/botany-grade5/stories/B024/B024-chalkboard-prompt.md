# 黑板畫 Prompt：B024 棕櫚

## English Prompt（Gemini 製圖用）

Please generate an image in the style of a Waldorf chalkboard drawing.

Black background. Soft chalk-like texture throughout. The central subject is a tall, unbranched cycad or palm tree — drawn in luminous yellow-green and warm white chalk strokes — standing erect on a rocky hillside. Its crown of large, feathery pinnate leaves fans outward at the top like an open hand reaching toward light. The trunk is single, strong, and vertical — no branches, no lateral spreading.

In the background, suggest the warm glow of a tropical or subtropical sun: a soft amber-gold radiance at the upper right, blending into deep indigo-blue at the top of the image. The ground beneath the tree shows rough, rocky shale in muted brown and grey chalk tones.

At the lower edge, a faint suggestion of the ocean horizon or a coastal line, rendered in pale blue-grey chalk. The overall mood is of deep stillness and ancient dignity — a living column between earth and sky. No text. No cartoon elements. Painterly, atmospheric, reverent.

Style reference: Waldorf school blackboard drawing — layered chalk on black, soft edges, glowing inner light, not photorealistic.

## 中文翻譯

請生成一張華德福黑板畫風格的圖片。

黑色背景，全圖呈現柔和的粉筆質感。主角是一棵高大、不分枝的蘇鐵或棕櫚樹——用明亮的黃綠色與溫暖的白色粉筆筆觸描繪——直立在岩石山坡上。頂端的巨大羽狀葉如張開的手掌向光伸展。樹幹單一、粗壯、垂直，沒有側枝，沒有橫向延伸。

背景暗示熱帶或亞熱帶的陽光：右上角柔和的琥珀金色光暈，向圖片頂端漸漸融入深靛藍色。樹下的地面以沉穩的棕色和灰色粉筆勾勒出粗糙的頁岩質地。

圖片下緣隱約透出海岸線的輪廓，以淡藍灰色粉筆輕描。整體氛圍是深沉的靜默與古老的尊嚴——一根立於大地與天空之間的生命柱。無文字。無卡通元素。繪畫性、有氛圍感、帶有敬意。
