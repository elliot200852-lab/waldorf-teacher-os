# B009 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：Botany-B009-chalkboard.png

請你生成一張圖片：

一棵古老的樟樹或榕樹，根深深扎入大地，樹幹從土地中升起，彷彿是大地本身被緩緩舉起的力量。樹皮上附著地衣與苔蘚，枝幹間有鳥巢，根部周圍可見菌菇與細根交織的地下世界。整體感受是「大地化為樹身，樹身承載萬物」的靜謐與龐大。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalk illustration on a deep black chalkboard background. The composition centers on a single ancient, massive tree — either a camphor tree or a banyan — whose roots plunge deep into the earth below and whose trunk rises powerfully upward, filling nearly the full height of the image. The roots are drawn with sweeping, organic curves, intertwined with fine fungal threads and small mushrooms emerging at the base, suggesting the underground world of the mycorrhizal network. The soil line is visible as a warm, dark brown band across the lower third of the image, with the roots disappearing into it like fingers reaching into clay.

The trunk is wide and deeply furrowed, drawn in warm ochre and raw umber chalk lines, with patches of grey-green lichen clinging to the bark and tufts of soft moss nestled in the shaded folds. Mid-canopy, a simple bird's nest rests in a fork of the branches. The canopy itself is rendered in loose, layered strokes of deep green and warm yellow-green, not dense but breathing — open to sky.

The overall palette is earthy and warm: deep black background, warm browns and ochres for wood and soil, moss greens and grey-greens for lichen and foliage, soft cream-white for highlighted chalk lines. The light source is diffuse and golden, coming from above, as if filtered through a forest canopy.

The atmosphere is mystical but grounded — this is not a scientific diagram, but a living image. The tree feels ancient, rooted, patient. The drawing style is loose and gestural, characteristic of Waldorf blackboard art: no outlines, built from layered color and light, feeling as if the image is emerging from the dark rather than drawn upon it.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格粉筆插圖，背景為深黑色黑板。畫面主體是一棵單獨的古老大樹——樟樹或榕樹——根部深深扎入地底，樹幹有力地向上升起，幾乎佔滿整個畫面的高度。根系以流動、有機的曲線描繪，底部交織著細密的菌根絲網與小菌菇，暗示地下的菌根世界。土壤線以溫暖的深棕色橫貫畫面下三分之一，根部如手指般沒入其中。

樹幹寬闊而深皺，以溫暖的赭石色和焦棕色粉筆描繪，樹皮上附著灰綠色地衣，陰暗的皺折間長著柔軟的苔蘚。中段枝幹的分叉處，有一個簡單的鳥巢。樹冠以深綠色與暖黃綠色的疊層筆觸繪成，不密實，而是呼吸著、透光的。

整體色調溫暖而大地感：深黑背景，木材與土壤用暖棕色和赭色，地衣與葉片用苔蘚綠與灰綠色，高光線條用柔和的奶白色。光源漫射而金黃，自上方透入，彷彿穿越林冠照下。

氛圍神秘而踏實——這不是科學圖解，而是一個有生命的圖像。樹感覺古老、深根、沉靜。繪畫風格鬆動而帶有手勢感，是華德福黑板畫的特色：沒有輪廓線，由疊層色彩與光線構成，彷彿圖像是從黑暗中浮現，而非畫在其上。
