# B017 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：Botany-B017-chalkboard.png

「請你生成一張圖片：華德福黑板畫風格，純黑色背景，粉筆質感。畫面以一顆正在裂開的種子為中心，位於深色泥土層中。種子的外殼向兩側裂開，露出內部淡黃色的胚芽。從種子底部，一條白色的根向下伸展，帶有細細的根毛，扎入更深的土壤。從種子頂部，一株嫩綠色的芽向上穿破土面，頂端展開兩片心形的子葉，像兩隻小手合掌後張開。土壤線以上是深藍色的夜空，左上方有一彎銀白色的弦月，灑下淡淡的月光。整體色調：土層用深褐色與赭色，根用白色與淡黃色，芽與子葉用嫩綠與黃綠色，月亮用銀白色。粉筆筆觸柔和、發光，有華德福黑板畫特有的暈染質感。不要文字，不要寫實風格。」

## English Prompt

Waldorf chalkboard drawing on a pure black background with authentic chalk textures and soft luminous strokes. The composition is vertical and centered, divided into underground and above-ground realms by a subtle soil line in earthy brown and ochre chalk.

Underground (lower two-thirds): Dark, rich earth rendered in deep browns, umber, and black. At the center of the earth, a single seed is splitting open — the seed coat cracks apart on both sides, revealing the pale golden-yellow embryo within. From the base of the seed, a white taproot descends downward into the deeper soil, with delicate, hair-thin lateral roots branching off to the sides, drawn in soft cream and white chalk. The root reaches confidently toward the bottom of the image, conveying the plant's first act: anchoring itself to the earth.

From the top of the seed, a pale green shoot pushes upward through the soil, breaking through the soil line into the world above. At the tip of the shoot, two heart-shaped cotyledons unfurl symmetrically — like two small hands that were pressed together in prayer and are now opening outward. The cotyledons are rendered in tender yellow-green and soft spring green, with a faint golden glow at their edges where the moonlight catches them. They are the heralds, the first to emerge, announcing what is to come.

Above ground (upper third): A deep indigo-blue night sky. In the upper left corner, a slender crescent moon in silvery white chalk radiates a gentle, diffused glow downward. The moonlight softly illuminates the cotyledons and the soil surface, creating a quiet sense of awakening — the moment between darkness and life.

Color palette: earth browns and raw umber below; cream and white for the root; yellow-green and spring green for the shoot and cotyledons; silver-white for the moon; deep indigo for the sky. The overall mood is one of quiet emergence — the very first moment a seed opens its book.

No text. No photorealism. Pure chalk-on-blackboard aesthetic with visible chalk grain, soft blending, and the characteristic luminous glow of Waldorf blackboard art. Under 200 words of visual content.

## 中文翻譯

華德福黑板畫，純黑色背景，真實粉筆質感與柔和發光筆觸。構圖為垂直置中，以土褐色與赭色粉筆繪製的微妙土壤線分為地下與地上兩個世界。

地下部分（下方三分之二）：以深褐色、棕褐色和黑色繪製的深邃泥土。畫面中央，一顆種子正在裂開——種皮向兩側綻裂，露出內部淡金黃色的胚芽。從種子底部，一條白色主根向下伸入更深的土壤，兩側分出細如髮絲的側根，以柔和的奶油色與白色粉筆繪製。根自信地向畫面底部延伸，傳達植物的第一個動作：扎根大地。

從種子頂部，一株淡綠色的嫩芽向上穿過土壤，突破土面進入上方的世界。芽的頂端，兩片心形的子葉對稱地展開——像兩隻原本合掌祈禱的小手正在張開。子葉以嫩黃綠色和柔和的春綠色繪製，邊緣因月光照射而帶有淡淡的金色光暈。它們是先鋒官，最先出現，宣告即將到來的一切。

地上部分（上方三分之一）：深靛藍色的夜空。左上角有一彎銀白色的弦月，以銀白色粉筆繪製，向下散發柔和的光暈。月光輕柔地照亮子葉和土壤表面，營造出一種安靜的甦醒感——黑暗與生命之間的那個瞬間。

色彩：下方為土褐色與生赭色；根為奶油色與白色；芽與子葉為黃綠色與春綠色；月亮為銀白色；天空為深靛藍色。整體氛圍是安靜的萌發——種子翻開那本書的最初時刻。

無文字。非寫實。粉筆黑板畫美學，可見粉筆顆粒感，柔和暈染，華德福黑板畫特有的發光質感。
