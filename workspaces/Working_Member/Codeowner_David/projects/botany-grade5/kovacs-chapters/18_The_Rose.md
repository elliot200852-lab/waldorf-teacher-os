# 18. The Rose

There are so many types of flowers and blossom-bearing plants that we
could spend the whole term talking about the different kinds, and still
we could not cover all of them.

> 開花的植物種類實在太多太多了，我們就算花整個學期來講不同的種類，也講不完。

We have learned about the tulip because it is a beautiful flower, which
everybody knows and everybody has seen. The tulip belongs to one
group of flowering plants (the group which is like younger children), the
group that has parallel-veined leaves, and has only one seed-leaf or
cotyledon.

> 我們學了鬱金香，因為它是一種大家都認識、都見過的美麗花朵。鬱金香屬於開花植物的其中一個家族（就像年幼孩子的那一群），葉脈平行，只有一片種子葉，也就是一片 cotyledon（子葉）。

From the other group - the more perfect plants, with network leaves,
and two seed-leaves - we will look at another beautiful flower that every-
body knows. We will look at the rose.

> 從另一個家族——比較完美的植物，有網狀葉脈、兩片種子葉的那一群——我們來看另一種大家都認識的美麗花朵。我們來看 rose（玫瑰）。

People have always called the rose the "queen" of all flowers, for no
other plant has such beautiful petals, and no other plant has such a
sweet scent. Why do we say about some people: they have "rosy" cheeks,
their cheeks are "like a rose"? What is it that gives "rosy" cheeks? It is the
blood flowing under the skin that gives rosy cheeks. When we run, our
blood flows faster and we all have "rosy" cheeks, for our own blood is the
colour of a red rose.

> 人們一直稱玫瑰為所有花朵的「女王」，因為沒有任何植物有那樣美的花瓣，也沒有任何植物有那樣甜美的芳香。為什麼我們說有些人臉頰「紅潤」，臉頰「像玫瑰一樣」？是什麼讓臉頰紅潤呢？是流在皮膚底下的血液讓臉頰紅潤的。當我們跑步的時候，血液流得更快，我們每個人的臉頰都會「紅潤」起來，因為我們自己的血液，就是紅玫瑰的顏色。

The rose plant is not as hasty as the tulip; it takes its time to bloom.
The roses don't appear in spring; they appear in summer when the sun is
at its height, when sun's light and sun's warmth are at their strongest.

> 玫瑰不像鬱金香那樣急躁，它會慢慢來，從容地開花。玫瑰不在春天出現，它們在夏天出現，在太陽升到最高的時候，在陽光和溫暖都最強盛的時候。

The rose plant also takes great care over its green leaves. Each leaf
has a "network of veins," and each leaf has an edge of tiny teeth, and
these small leaves are set on a common stem so that, together, they are
really one large leaf. You will often find seven smaller leaves growing
from a common stem, making one larger leaf. The rose's leaves are quite
different from the simple, straight-edged leaves of the tulip!

> 玫瑰對它的綠葉也非常用心。每一片葉子都有「網狀的葉脈」，每一片葉子的邊緣都有小小的鋸齒，而這些小葉子排列在一根共同的葉柄上，合在一起其實是一片大葉子。你常常會看到七片小葉子從同一根葉柄長出來，組成一片大葉。玫瑰的葉子和鬱金香那種簡單、直邊的葉子可真是太不一樣了！

it is not quite at home on the earth; it just touches the earth. The tulip is
like a person who runs on tiptoe, but the rose is like someone who walks
with a firm, strong step.

> 它在大地上並不真的安居；它只是輕輕碰觸大地而已。鬱金香就像一個踮著腳尖跑的人，而玫瑰就像一個腳步穩重、踏實的人。

It is not only by the roots that you can see that the rose loves the
earth. You remember that the wood of trees is really earth, and that the
trunk of a tree is really like earth reaching upwards. The rose is a "wood-
plant." It draws the earth upwards and has slender wooden trunks and
branches, as a result. It is a shrub, as we call all wood-plants that are not
proper trees.

> 不只是從根可以看出玫瑰愛大地。你還記得，樹的木頭其實就是大地的力量，樹幹就像大地往上伸展。玫瑰是一種「木本植物」。它把大地的力量往上拉，所以長出了纖細的木質枝幹。它是一種 shrub（灌木），我們把所有不算是正式大樹的木本植物都叫做灌木。

The branches of a rose plant do not just shoot up towards the light,
they bow, they form an arc and the tip turns down slightly towards the
earth again. That is quite different from the tulip; the tulip stalk has no
time to form strong wood, the tulip has no wood at all, it does not care
for earth, and the stalk goes straight up, away from the earth towards
the light.

> 玫瑰的枝條不是直直往光的方向衝上去的，它們會彎下來，形成一道弧形，枝尖稍稍轉回朝向大地。這跟鬱金香完全不同；鬱金香的莖沒有時間長出堅實的木質，鬱金香根本沒有木頭，它不在乎大地，莖就直直往上，離開大地，朝向光。

So you see, the rose does show that it loves the earth, and in its flow-
er you can see that the rose is also a loving child of the sun. The tulip
gets the calyx and the flower mixed up - its calyx becomes the flower.
That never happens with the rose. The calyx leaves, the green sepals, are
quite different from the petals - they close together and form a shell,
like the chrysalis of the butterfly, and in that shell the rose petals are
born, as the butterfly is born in the chrysalis.

> 所以你看，玫瑰確實表現出它愛大地，而在它的花朵裡，你也能看到玫瑰同樣是太陽深愛的孩子。鬱金香把花萼和花瓣搞混了——它的花萼就變成了花。玫瑰絕不會這樣。花萼的綠色萼片和花瓣完全不同——萼片合攏在一起，形成一個殼，就像蝴蝶的蛹一樣，而在這個殼裡面，玫瑰的花瓣誕生了，就像蝴蝶在蛹裡誕生一樣。

The tulip, and all flowers which belong to that family: lily, hyacinth,
narcissus, crocus, all those which do not care so much for the earth, they
follow the six-pointed star. But the rose loves the earth, and it follows
the five-pointed star. The 56wild rose - the briar-rose - has five petals.
The garden roses have much more than five petals, but if you count the
petals of a garden rose, you will find that its number can always be di-
vided by five. The rose knows the five-times-table!

> 鬱金香，以及屬於那個家族的所有花——lily（百合）、hyacinth（風信子）、narcissus（水仙）、crocus（番紅花）——這些不太在乎大地的花，都遵循六角星的規律。但玫瑰愛大地，它遵循的是五角星。野玫瑰——briar-rose（野薔薇）——有五片花瓣。園藝玫瑰的花瓣比五片多得多，但如果你數數園藝玫瑰的花瓣，你會發現數目總是可以被五整除。玫瑰會背五的乘法表呢！
