# 19. The Rose Family.

The rose is the plant that loves the earth as much as it loves the sun, and
so, in the rose, the forces of the sun and the forces of the earth are in
perfect harmony. Because sun and earth are balanced in the rose, it is
the most perfect of all plants. Other plants may have more colourful
petals, and others may have a stronger scent, but this perfect harmony
of sun-forces and earth-forces exist in no other plant.

> 玫瑰是一種像愛太陽一樣愛大地的植物，所以在玫瑰身上，太陽的力量和大地的力量完美地和諧共存。因為太陽與大地在玫瑰身上取得了平衡，它是所有植物中最完美的。其他植物或許有更鮮豔的花瓣，有的或許有更濃郁的芳香，但這種太陽力量與大地力量的完美和諧，在別的植物身上是找不到的。

Think back to the people of ancient India who did not really care
much for life on earth. The holy men of India did not work hard, they
spent their time in prayer and their minds were so concentrated on
heaven, that hunger and thirst, cold and heat, life on earth itself, meant
very little to them. In the tulip, in the white lily, and in the narcissus, that
only have thin roots, without wood, you can picture life in ancient India.

> 回想一下古代 India（印度）的人們，他們並不太在意地上的生活。印度的聖者不辛苦勞動，他們把時間花在祈禱上，心思全都專注在天上，飢渴、冷熱、地上的生活，對他們來說都不太重要。在鬱金香、白百合和水仙花裡——它們只有細細的根，沒有木質——你可以想像古代印度的生活。

But it was different when you learned about Persia. Remember how
the sun god, Ahura Mazda, showed King Jemshid a golden dagger in a
dream, and the King understood that he had to make a fine plough. The
Persians loved the sun, and they loved their sun-god, Ahura Mazda, but
they also loved the earth. They were the first farmers,
the first people to
plant crops and flowers and fruit, and they changed the earth to make it
a home for human beings.

> 但是當你學到 Persia（波斯）的時候就不一樣了。還記得太陽神 Ahura Mazda（阿胡拉·馬茲達）在夢中向 King Jemshid（傑姆希德王）展示了一把金色匕首，國王明白了他必須造一把精良的犁。波斯人愛太陽，也愛他們的太陽神阿胡拉·馬茲達，但他們同樣也愛大地。他們是最早的農夫，最早種植莊稼、花卉和水果的人，他們改變了大地，讓它成為人類的家園。

You can easily see that the Persians were like the rose: they both love
the sun and the earth. And the amazing thing is this: it was the Persians
who changed the wild roses with only five petals, into the garden roses
with many petals. And no one knows how they did it; no one today has
the wisdom to change a briar-rose so that it becomes a garden-rose. You
can "graft" a garden-rose onto the young stem of a wild rose, and it will
become a garden rose, but you must have a garden-rose plant first.

> 你很容易就能看出來，波斯人就像玫瑰一樣：他們都同時愛太陽和大地。而最神奇的是：正是波斯人把只有五片花瓣的野玫瑰，變成了有許多花瓣的園藝玫瑰。沒有人知道他們是怎麼做到的；今天沒有人有那樣的智慧，能把一株野薔薇變成園藝玫瑰。你可以把園藝玫瑰「嫁接」到野玫瑰的嫩枝上，它就會長成園藝玫瑰，但你得先有一株園藝玫瑰才行。

So you see, the apple tree is a kind of rose which says: "I am not going
to please human eyes with my blossoms, I am not going to please
human noses with the scent of my petals, I am going to do more for
them; I am going to please them with the refreshing taste of my fruits'
juice."

> 所以你看，蘋果樹就像一種玫瑰，它說：「我不打算用我的花朵來取悅人的眼睛，我不打算用花瓣的香氣來取悅人的鼻子，我要為他們做更多的事；我要用我果實清甜的汁液來讓他們開心。」

To make the fruit, the strength of the earth is needed (you remember
the ovary is a little earth) and so the apple plant needs more strength
from the earth than the rose plant. That's why the apple plant must be-
come a tree, it needs more wood, and that means more earth than the
rose plant needs. The rose, which gives more strength to the lovely flow-
er, can remain a shrub, with slender wooden stems, but only hips can
grow on it. The apple, and all the other fruit trees, must have more help
from the earth, and so they have to be trees.

> 要結出果實，就需要大地的力量（你還記得，ovary（子房）就像一小塊大地）。所以蘋果需要比玫瑰更多來自大地的力量。這就是為什麼蘋果植株必須長成一棵大樹——它需要更多木質，而那意味著需要比玫瑰更多的大地力量。玫瑰把更多力量給了美麗的花朵，所以它可以只是一叢灌木，有纖細的木質枝幹，但只能長出薔薇果。蘋果和其他所有果樹，需要大地更多的幫助，所以它們必須長成大樹。

All the members of the rose family - the garden rose, the apple tree,
and the peach tree, and so on - all give us delight and joy in their dif-
ferent ways. And just as the rose came from Persia, these fruit trees have
also come to us from Persia, from that ancient land when people loved
the earth as well as the sun.

> 薔薇科的所有成員——園藝玫瑰、蘋果樹、桃樹等等——都以各自不同的方式帶給我們喜悅和歡樂。就像玫瑰來自波斯，這些果樹也是從波斯傳到我們這裡的，從那個同時愛著大地與太陽的古老國度。

There is yet another important difference between apple blossoms
and rose blossoms. The apple tree wants to have a 59juicy fruit, and
therefore, has plenty of nectar in its blossoms - that is the sweet juice
the bees seek to make honey. And so, when the apple tree is in blossom,
you can always hear the hum of the many bees that come to collect the
nectar. The rose is a "drier" plant, the hips are not very juicy, and the
rose-blossom has no nectar for the bees. The bees do come to the rose
to collect some pollen, as they also need pollen for their food, but you
will never see as many bees around a rosebush as you will see around
the apple tree. So, the rose gives all its strength to beauty, whereas, the
apple and the other fruit trees give most of their strength to produce
their juicy fruits.

> 蘋果花和玫瑰花之間還有一個重要的不同。蘋果樹想要結出多汁的果實，所以它的花裡有很多 nectar（花蜜）——就是蜜蜂用來釀蜜的那種甜甜的汁液。所以當蘋果樹開花的時候，你總是能聽見許多蜜蜂嗡嗡飛來採蜜的聲音。玫瑰是比較「乾」的植物，薔薇果汁液不多，玫瑰花裡也沒有花蜜給蜜蜂。蜜蜂確實也會來玫瑰這裡採一些 pollen（花粉），因為它們也需要花粉當食物，但你絕不會在玫瑰叢旁看到像蘋果樹旁那麼多的蜜蜂。所以，玫瑰把所有的力量都給了美，而蘋果和其他果樹則把大部分力量用來結出多汁的果實。
