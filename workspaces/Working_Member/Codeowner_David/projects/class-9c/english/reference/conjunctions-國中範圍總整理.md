# 國中英語連接詞總整理（Conjunctions & Connectors）

## 一、對等連接詞（Coordinating Conjunctions）-- FANBOYS

對等連接詞連接兩個文法地位相同的單字、片語或子句。口訣：**F-A-N-B-O-Y-S**。

### 1. for ☆

- 中文：因為（較正式，書面語）
- 用法：用於補充說明原因，語氣比 because 弱，通常放在第二個子句前。
- 例句：
  - She brought an umbrella, for it looked like rain.
  - He went to bed early, for he had a test the next morning.

### 2. and ★

- 中文：和、而且
- 用法：連接兩個相同性質的字詞、片語或子句，表示「並列」或「添加」。
- 例句：
  - I like basketball and baseball.
  - She studied hard, and she passed the exam.

### 3. nor ☆

- 中文：也不
- 用法：用於否定句中，連接第二個否定項目。注意 nor 後面的主詞和動詞要倒裝。
- 例句：
  - He doesn't like math, nor does he like science.
  - She cannot sing, nor can she dance.

### 4. but ★

- 中文：但是、可是
- 用法：連接兩個相反或對比的概念。
- 例句：
  - The test was hard, but I did my best.
  - I wanted to go out, but it was raining.

### 5. or ★

- 中文：或者、還是
- 用法：提供選擇或替代方案。
- 例句：
  - Do you want tea or coffee?
  - Hurry up, or you will be late for school.

### 6. yet ☆

- 中文：然而、但是（語氣比 but 強）
- 用法：表示出乎意料的對比，較正式。
- 例句：
  - She is young, yet she is very responsible.
  - The question seemed easy, yet many students got it wrong.

### 7. so ★

- 中文：所以、因此
- 用法：表示結果或推論。
- 例句：
  - It was raining, so we stayed home.
  - I was hungry, so I made myself a sandwich.

## 二、從屬連接詞（Subordinating Conjunctions）

從屬連接詞將一個子句變成「從屬子句」（也叫附屬子句），使它依附於主要子句。

> **標點規則提醒**：
> - 從屬子句在前 → 加逗號：**When I got home, I started cooking.**
> - 從屬子句在後 → 不加逗號：**I started cooking when I got home.**

---

### A. 時間（Time）

#### when ★

- 中文：當......的時候
- 例句：
  - When I was a kid, I loved climbing trees.
  - Please call me when you get home.

#### while ★

- 中文：當......的時候（強調同時進行）
- 例句：
  - While I was doing homework, my brother was watching TV.
  - She listens to music while she studies.

#### before ★

- 中文：在......之前
- 例句：
  - Wash your hands before you eat.
  - Before the bell rang, the students were already in their seats.

#### after ★

- 中文：在......之後
- 例句：
  - After school, we usually play basketball.
  - I felt much better after I took a nap.

#### until ★

- 中文：直到......為止
- 例句：
  - We waited until the rain stopped.
  - Don't leave until I come back.

#### as soon as ☆

- 中文：一......就......
- 例句：
  - As soon as I saw her, I knew something was wrong.
  - He called his mom as soon as he arrived at school.

#### since ☆

- 中文：自從......以來（時間用法，常搭配完成式）
- 例句：
  - I have lived here since I was five.
  - Since we moved to Yilan, we have made many friends.

---

### B. 因果（Cause & Effect）

#### because ★

- 中文：因為
- 用法：最常用的因果連接詞，直接說明原因。
- 例句：
  - I was late because I missed the bus.
  - Because it was so cold, we decided to stay inside.

#### since ☆

- 中文：既然、由於（因果用法，語氣較輕）
- 用法：原因通常是雙方已知的事實。
- 例句：
  - Since you are here, let's start the meeting.
  - Since it's Sunday, the library is closed.

#### as ☆

- 中文：由於、因為（正式，書面語）
- 例句：
  - As it was getting dark, we headed home.
  - As he didn't study, he failed the test.

---

### C. 條件（Condition）

#### if ★

- 中文：如果
- 例句：
  - If it rains tomorrow, we will cancel the field trip.
  - I will help you if you ask me.

#### unless ★

- 中文：除非（= if ... not）
- 例句：
  - Unless you hurry, you will miss the train.
  - You can't pass unless you study harder.

#### as long as ☆

- 中文：只要
- 例句：
  - You can borrow my book as long as you return it by Friday.
  - As long as you try your best, I will be proud of you.

---

### D. 讓步（Concession）

#### although ★

- 中文：雖然
- 用法：最正式的「雖然」，書面與口語皆可。注意：although 和 but 不能同時出現在一個句子裡。
- 例句：
  - Although it was cold, she went jogging.
  - He kept trying although he failed many times.

#### though ★

- 中文：雖然（比 although 略口語）
- 用法：可以放句首或句中，口語中也可放句尾（此時等同 however）。
- 例句：
  - Though he is young, he is very mature.
  - The movie was long. It was interesting, though.

#### even though ★

- 中文：即使、儘管（語氣比 although 更強）
- 例句：
  - Even though I was tired, I finished all my homework.
  - She smiled even though she was sad.

#### even if ☆

- 中文：即使（假設性，事情不一定發生）
- 用法：與 even though 的差別在於 even if 帶有假設語氣。
- 例句：
  - Even if it rains, we will still go hiking.
  - I will support you even if everyone else disagrees.

---

### E. 目的（Purpose）

#### so that ☆

- 中文：以便、為了
- 用法：後面接目的子句，常搭配 can / will / could / would。
- 例句：
  - She spoke louder so that everyone could hear her.
  - I set an alarm so that I wouldn't oversleep.

#### in order that △

- 中文：為了（較正式，書面語）
- 例句：
  - He saved money in order that he could buy a new phone.
  - The teacher explained it again in order that the students would understand.

---

### F. 比較（Comparison）

#### than ★

- 中文：比
- 用法：搭配比較級使用。
- 例句：
  - She runs faster than I do.
  - This book is more interesting than that one.

#### as...as ★

- 中文：和......一樣
- 用法：原級比較，中間放形容詞或副詞原級。否定用 not as...as。
- 例句：
  - He is as tall as his father.
  - This test is not as difficult as the last one.

## 三、配對連接詞（Correlative Conjunctions）

配對連接詞成對出現，前後連接的元素在文法上必須對稱（平行結構）。

### both...and ★

- 中文：......和......都
- 例句：
  - Both my sister and I like reading.
  - She is both smart and hardworking.

### either...or ★

- 中文：不是......就是......；要麼......要麼......
- 例句：
  - Either you come with us, or you stay home.
  - You can have either the cake or the pie.

### neither...nor ☆

- 中文：既不......也不......
- 例句：
  - Neither Tom nor Jerry was at school today.
  - She speaks neither Japanese nor Korean.

### not only...but also ☆

- 中文：不但......而且......
- 例句：
  - He is not only a good student but also a great basketball player.
  - Not only did she finish the project, but she also helped her classmates.

## 四、連接副詞（Conjunctive Adverbs）-- 認識級

連接副詞用來連接兩個**獨立的句子**，表達邏輯關係。它們不是真正的連接詞，不能像 and / but / because 那樣直接連接兩個子句。

> **標點規則**：
> - 句號或分號分開兩句，連接副詞後面加逗號。
> - 正確：**I was tired. However, I kept studying.**
> - 正確：**I was tired; however, I kept studying.**
> - 錯誤：~~I was tired, however, I kept studying.~~（這是 comma splice 錯誤）

### however ☆

- 中文：然而、不過
- 例句：The exam was hard. However, most students passed.

### therefore ☆

- 中文：因此、所以
- 例句：He didn't practice enough. Therefore, he didn't make the team.

### besides △

- 中文：此外、而且
- 例句：I don't want to go out. Besides, it's raining.

### moreover △

- 中文：而且、再者（正式）
- 例句：The hotel was expensive. Moreover, the service was terrible.

### furthermore △

- 中文：此外、再者（最正式）
- 例句：The plan is too costly. Furthermore, it will take too long.

### instead ☆

- 中文：反而、代替
- 例句：He didn't go to the party. Instead, he stayed home and read.

### otherwise ☆

- 中文：否則、不然
- 例句：You should leave now. Otherwise, you will be late.

## 五、教學筆記

### 從屬 vs 對等：核心差異

```
對等連接詞（and, but, or, so ...）
  → [獨立子句] + 連接詞 + [獨立子句]
  → 兩邊地位相等，拿掉連接詞，兩句話各自成立。

從屬連接詞（because, when, if, although ...）
  → [主要子句] + [從屬子句]
  → 從屬子句不能單獨存在，必須依附主要子句。
```

簡單判斷法：把連接詞和它帶的子句單獨拿出來，如果不能獨立成句，那就是從屬子句。

- "Because I was tired." → 不完整，從屬子句。
- "I was tired." → 完整，獨立子句。

---

### 標點規則總整理

| 結構 | 標點 | 範例 |
|------|------|------|
| S, **FANBOYS** S. | 逗號在連接詞前 | I was tired**,** but I kept going. |
| **從屬** S, S. | 從屬子句在前加逗號 | When I got home**,** I took a shower. |
| S **從屬** S. | 從屬子句在後不加逗號 | I took a shower when I got home. |
| S. **However,** S. | 連接副詞前用句號或分號 | It rained**. However,** we still went out. |

### 會考常見考法

**1. 填空選擇正確連接詞**

考題通常給出兩個子句，要求學生判斷邏輯關係後選出正確的連接詞。

- 常考對比：because vs. although、if vs. unless、and vs. but
- 關鍵：先判斷兩句之間是「因果」「轉折」「條件」還是「時間」關係。

**2. 句子合併**

給出兩個獨立的短句，要求用指定的連接詞合併成一句。

- 注意事項：合併後不能出現重複的主詞或矛盾的邏輯。
- 例：He was sick. He went to school. → Although he was sick, he went to school.

**3. 閱讀理解中的邏輯關係判斷**

長篇閱讀中，連接詞是理解段落邏輯的關鍵線索。

- however / but 出現 → 後面通常是作者真正想說的重點。
- because / so 出現 → 注意因果關係的方向。
- if / unless 出現 → 注意條件與結果的對應。

---

### 學習策略建議

1. **先穩固四大天王**：and / but / or / so 是一切的基礎。
2. **再擴展因果與條件**：because / if / although 是會考高頻。
3. **時間連接詞搭配時態練習**：when / before / after / until 與過去式、未來式的搭配。
4. **配對連接詞注意平行結構**：both...and / either...or 前後詞性要一致。
5. **連接副詞認識即可**：知道 however = but（但用法不同）、therefore = so 就夠了。
