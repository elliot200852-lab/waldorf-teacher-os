# 9C English Week 6 — Words That Connect (Teacher's Key)

---

## Part 1: Meet the New Connectors — 答案

學生應圈出的連接詞：

1. **When** the alarm went off...
2. **before** I got back from the trip...
3. **After** he found out the truth...
4. **although** the situation was terrible...

---

## Part 2: Choose Your Task — 參考答案

---

### Task D — Matching 答案

| 題號 | 連接詞 | 配對 | 完整句子 |
|:-----|:------|:-----|:--------|
| 1. | **After** | → 她告訴了所有人 | After she found out the secret, she told everyone. |
| 2. | **Although** | → 我們沒有放棄比賽 | Although it was raining hard, we didn't give up the game. |
| 3. | **before** | → 我放學回來之前 | I usually walk the dog before I get back from school. |
| 4. | **If** | → 你永遠不會知道 | If you give up now, you will never know. |
| 5. | **before** | → 老師進來之前 | He finished all his homework before the teacher came in. |
| 6. | **Although** | → 她最後還是笑了 | Although she tries to be serious, she always ends up laughing. |

**評分彈性：**
- 第 3 題：「before I get back from school」或「after I get back from school」皆合理，語意不同但文法正確。若學生填 after 且能解釋（放學回來之後才遛狗），給分。
- 第 5 題：before 和 after 皆可能合理——「before the teacher came in」（老師來之前做完）較自然。
- 第 6 題：although 和 when 皆可接受。Although 強調對比（想嚴肅但還是笑），when 強調時間（每次想嚴肅的時候就笑）。

### Task C — Fill in the Blank 答案

**1.** **Although** I came down with a cold, I still went to school **because** we had a test that day.

**2.** A: Why did you stay up so late?
B: **When/After** I found out the movie was only 30 minutes long, I decided to watch it. But it turned out to be two hours.

**3.** **If** you work out every day, your body will get stronger.

**4.** She called her mom right **after** she got back to the dorm.

**5.** **Although** the project didn't work out as planned, the team learned a lot from the experience.

**6.** Make sure you finish your homework **before** you go out to play.

**7.** A: What happened to Jake?
B: He ended up in the hospital **because** he broke down during the marathon.

**8.** **When** she heard the news, she couldn't believe it.

**評分彈性：**
- 第 1 題第一格：although 為最佳答案。若學生填 when 語意可通但失去「雖然...還是...」的對比關係，扣半分或請學生再想。
- 第 2 題：when 和 after 皆合理。When 強調得知的那個時刻，after 強調得知之後的決定。
- 第 7 題：because 和 after 皆可接受。Because 說明原因（因為他崩潰了），after 說明時序（崩潰之後進醫院）。
- 第 8 題：when 和 after 皆可。

### Task B — Sentence Builder 參考答案

#### Phrasal Verb Check 中文意思

| Phrasal Verb | 中文意思 |
|:---|:---|
| come down with | 生病、染上（感冒等） |
| give up | 放棄 |
| find out | 發現、查出 |
| get back | 回來、回到 |
| break down | 故障、崩潰 |
| work out | 運動；（事情）順利解決 |

#### 造句範例

**1.** Connector: **when** / Phrasal verb: **come down with**
> When I came down with a fever, my mom stayed home to take care of me.

**2.** Connector: **although** / Phrasal verb: **give up**
> Although the test was really hard, I didn't give up.

**3.** Connector: **before** / Phrasal verb: **find out**
> Before she found out the truth, she thought everything was fine.

**4.** Connector: **after** / Phrasal verb: **get back**
> After we got back from the school trip, everyone was exhausted.

**5.** Connector: **because** / Phrasal verb: **break down**
> The bus was late because it broke down on the highway.

**6.** Connector: **if** / Phrasal verb: **work out**
> If you work out every morning, you'll feel more energetic.

**評分標準：**
- 句子包含指定連接詞 + 指定動詞片語 = 基本得分
- 語意通順、文法正確 = 滿分
- 片語用法正確但連接詞位置不對（例如放在句中而非句首）= 仍給分，口頭提醒連接詞的位置彈性
- 學生用了指定片語但意思用錯（如 work out 當「運動」但語境需要「解決」）= 給半分，標注提醒

## Closing: My Connector Sentence — 評分說明

- 使用了今天的新連接詞（when / before / after / although）之一 = 得分
- 句子完整且語意通順 = 滿分
- 寫了句子但用了 if 或 because（上週舊成員）= 給分但口頭鼓勵下次試新的
- 空白未寫 = 標記追蹤，下次課前補寫
