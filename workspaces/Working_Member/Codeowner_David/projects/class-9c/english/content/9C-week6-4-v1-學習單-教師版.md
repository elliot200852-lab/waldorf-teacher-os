# A Rice Sandwich — Reading & Responding (Teacher's Key)

---

## Part 1: Connector Hunt 答案

學生需從文本中找出至少 5 個連接詞並判斷關係類型。以下為文本中可找到的主要連接詞（供教師對照，學生找到其中任意 5 個即可）：

| # | Connector | 出處段落 | 關係類型 |
|---|:----------|:---------|:---------|
| 1 | because | 第一段：because their mothers aren't home | reason 原因 |
| 2 | but | 第二段：isn't far but it's not close | contrast 對比 |
| 3 | so | 第二段：so I could eat in the canteen | reason/purpose 目的 |
| 4 | as if | 第三段：as if I'm starting trouble | comparison 比較 |
| 5 | But | 第四段：But Nenny says... | contrast 對比 |
| 6 | on the other hand | 第四段：Kiki and Carlos, on the other hand | contrast 對比 |
| 7 | because | 第四段：because she likes to go home | reason 原因 |
| 8 | if | 第四段：especially if it's raining | condition 條件 |
| 9 | ever since | 第四段：ever since they saw that movie | time 時間 |
| 10 | If | 第五段：If I ate at school... | condition 條件 |
| 11 | when | 第五段：when I came home finally | time 時間 |
| 12 | after | 第六段：after three days of this | time 時間 |
| 13 | because | 第六段：because we don't have lunch meat | reason 原因 |
| 14 | until | 第七段：Everything is fine until... | time limit 時間界線 |
| 15 | since | 第七段：since I am shy | reason 原因 |
| 16 | till | 第七段：till Sister Superior gives the okay | time limit 時間界線 |
| 17 | while | 第九段：while the Sister Superior read my letter | time 同時 |
| 18 | even though | 第十二段：even though I knew that wasn't my house | contrast 對比 |
| 19 | when | 第十二段：when nuns yell at me | time 時間 |
| 20 | even if | 第十二段：even if they're not yelling | contrast 對比 |
| 21 | while | 第十四段：lots of boys and girls watched while I cried | time 同時 |

**批改提示：**
- 學生找到 5 個且關係類型正確 → 滿分
- 關係類型用中文或英文皆可
- 若學生找到 and、or、so 等對等連接詞也可接受，但鼓勵他們多找從屬連接詞
- 特別留意 even though / even if / until 這三個較難的連接詞——能找到這些的學生表示閱讀理解力強

### Task D — What Happened? (Story Map) 答案

正確順序：

| 順序 | Event |
|:-----|:------|
| **4** | Esperanza ate her sandwich in the canteen and cried. |
| **1** | Esperanza asked her mother to make her a sandwich and write a letter. |
| **3** | The nun told Esperanza to go upstairs and see Sister Superior. |
| **2** | Esperanza's mother said no at first. |
| **5** | Sister Superior made Esperanza point to her house from the window. |
| **6** | Esperanza got in line with the stay-at-school kids. |

**更正：** 正確順序應為：

| 順序 | Event |
|:-----|:------|
| **6** | Esperanza ate her sandwich in the canteen and cried. |
| **2** | Esperanza asked her mother to make her a sandwich and write a letter. |
| **4** | The nun told Esperanza to go upstairs and see Sister Superior. |
| **1** | Esperanza's mother said no at first. |
| **5** | Sister Superior made Esperanza point to her house from the window. |
| **3** | Esperanza got in line with the stay-at-school kids. |

**批改提示：**
- 全對 6 分，每題 1 分
- 注意：題目設計上「asked her mother」(2) 和「mother said no」(1) 順序容易混淆。嚴格來說 Esperanza 先有了念頭、然後跟媽媽說、媽媽先拒絕。但兩者互換可接受，因為故事中 Esperanza 的「念頭」和「開口問」幾乎同時發生
- Bonus 畫臉不計分但可觀察學生的情感理解——開頭應是期待/興奮的表情，結尾應是難過/失望的表情

### Task C — Reading Check + Connector Fill 參考答案

**1.** Why do the special kids eat in the canteen? [because]

> The special kids eat in the canteen **because** their mothers aren't home or home is too far away.

**2.** What did Esperanza's mother do after three days of asking? [after]

> **After** three days of Esperanza's asking, her mother finally said okay and wrote a letter to the principal.

**3.** What happened when the nun saw Esperanza in line? [when]

> **When** the nun saw Esperanza in line, she asked who sent her and told her to go see Sister Superior.

**4.** Esperanza pointed to the wrong house. Why? [because]

> Esperanza pointed to the wrong house **because** she was too scared and upset to say no to Sister Superior. / **Because** the nun was yelling at her and she was crying, she just nodded.

**5.** How did Esperanza feel at the end, even though she got what she wanted? [although]

> **Although** Esperanza finally got to eat in the canteen, she felt sad and disappointed because it was nothing special and she had been humiliated.

**批改提示：**
- 每題 2 分，共 10 分
- 評分重點：(1) 連接詞使用正確 1 分；(2) 內容回答合理 1 分
- 文法錯誤不扣分（重點是理解與連接詞運用）
- 第 4 題接受多種合理解釋：害怕、無力反抗、被權威壓迫等
- 第 5 題只要表達出「得到了卻不開心」的落差感即可

### Task B — Deep Thinking + Sentence Craft 參考答案

**1.** Esperanza spent three days convincing her mother. What arguments did she use? Do you think they were good arguments?

> Esperanza used several arguments to convince her mother. First, she said she was not strong like a Spartan — she was skinny and couldn't even blow up a balloon. Then she said **if** she ate at school, there would be less dishes to wash. Finally, she told her mother that she would miss her **when** the chair was empty at noon. I think her arguments were clever **because** she mixed practical reasons with emotional ones.

**教師筆記：** 重點看學生是否能識別出 Esperanza 的三層說服策略：(1) 示弱、(2) 實用好處、(3) 感情牌。能看到這個結構的學生理解力較強。

**2.** The mother's letter to Sister Superior is very short and simple. What does the letter tell you about the mother?

> The letter tells us that the mother's English is not very strong, **because** she writes in simple sentences and uses expressions like "I hope to God." **Although** the letter is short, it shows she cares about her daughter — she worries that Esperanza is too skinny and might faint. The letter also shows that the mother feels small in front of the school's authority, **since** she writes very politely and humbly.

**教師筆記：** 關鍵觀察——媽媽的英文能力、對女兒的愛、對權威的卑微。能同時看到這三點的學生已達到很好的文本分析深度。

**3.** At the end, Esperanza finally got to eat in the canteen — but it was "nothing special." Why do you think she felt that way?

> Esperanza felt that way **because** the experience of getting to the canteen was so humiliating that it ruined everything. **Although** she finally got what she wanted, she had to cry in front of Sister Superior and point to a house that wasn't even hers. **When** she sat down to eat, the sandwich was already cold and greasy. The canteen was never really about the food — it was about belonging. **But** after what happened, she didn't feel like she belonged at all.

**教師筆記：** 這是全篇最核心的問題。理想回答會觸及「歸屬感」的主題——Esperanza 想要的不是食物，而是被接納。接受任何合理的情感分析。

**4.** Think of a time when you really wanted something... (個人經驗題)

**教師筆記：** 此題無標準答案。評分重點：
- 使用了 although 或 even though（1 分）
- 連接詞用法正確（1 分）
- 內容有真實感受，不是敷衍（1 分）
- 造句內容貼近「期待落空」的主題（加分）

**批改提示（Task B 整體）：**
- 每題 3 分，共 12 分
- 連接詞使用（有劃線標記）1 分 + 內容深度 2 分
- 不要求完美文法，重點是思考品質與連接詞的自然運用

### Task A — Writer's Craft Analysis 參考方向

此為開放題，無標準答案。以下為各選項的教師參考方向：

**Option 1 — The Letter**
改寫重點：學生能否把口語化的短信改成正式書信（Dear Sister Superior, I am writing to request... My daughter Esperanza... I would be most grateful...）。比較時的關鍵洞察：正式版更「正確」但失去了原版的真誠與脆弱感——那個「I hope to God she does not faint」比任何正式用語都更有力量。

**Option 2 — The Last Line**
分析重點：(1) "which was nothing special" 回扣第一段的期待；(2) "the bread already greasy and the rice cold" 是感官描寫，讓讀者「看見」而非被告知 Esperanza 的感受；(3) 短句的力量——Cisneros 沒有解釋 Esperanza 的心情，讓讀者自己感受。

**Option 3 — Esperanza's Voice**
模仿重點：短句節奏、第一人稱、口語感、具體細節（不說「我很難過」而是描述一個畫面）。連接詞的自然使用。好的模仿會有 Cisneros 的節奏感——句子長短交替，在關鍵處用極短句。

**批改提示（Task A 整體）：**
- 總分 5 分
- 語言品質 2 分 + 分析/模仿深度 3 分
- 這是挑戰層任務，能完成且有自己觀點的學生表現已很好
- 特別鼓勵 Option 3（創意寫作），即使語言不完美，有真實感受就值得肯定
