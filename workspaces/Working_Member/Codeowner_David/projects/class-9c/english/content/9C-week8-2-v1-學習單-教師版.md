## The Long Sentence — 長句手術

Read this sentence from *The First Job*. It's ONE sentence. Use a pencil slash (/) to cut it into short sentences.

> So the next morning I put on the navy blue dress that made me / look older and borrowed money for lunch and bus fare because / Aunt Lala said I wouldn't get paid till the next Friday. / I went in and saw the boss of the Peter Pan photo finishers on North Broadway where Aunt Lala worked / and lied about my age like she told me to, / and sure enough, I started that same day.

How many short sentences did you find? **10**

### 🔎 Task C — 填空 Fill In（答案）

1. Esperanza put on a dress **that** made her look older.
2. She borrowed money **because** she wouldn't get paid for a week.
3. The photo shop was on North Broadway **where** her aunt worked.
4. She didn't know **where** the envelopes were going.
5. She lied about her age **like** her aunt told her to.
6. She wouldn't get paid **till** next Friday.

提示答案：「原因」→ because ｜「地點」→ where ｜「描述哪一個」→ that ｜「直到什麼時候」→ till

---

### 🔬 Task B — 合併句子 Combine（參考答案）

**1.** [where / and]
→ I went to the office **where** my teacher works **and** handed in my homework.

**2.** [because / till]
→ I couldn't buy lunch **because** I forgot my wallet, and I had to wait **till** my friend lent me money.

**3.** [that / and / like]
→ I wore the jacket **that** was my brother's **and** walked to school **like** my mom told me to.

**4.** [where / because / and]
→ I sat in the library **where** nobody could see me **because** I was embarrassed **and** ate my sandwich quietly.

## 今天學到的 What I Learned Today

```
where 有兩種功能：
1. 間接問句：I didn't know where + S + V
   （我不知道 + 什麼地方）
2. 描述地點：名詞 + where + S + V
   （哪一個地方 → 描述它）
```
