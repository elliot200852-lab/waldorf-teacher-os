# The Truth about Ruthie — 秘密與失落

## 教師教案（Edna's Ruthie 第二堂）

**年級**：九年級 ｜ **時長**：45 分鐘 ｜ **語言焦點**：間接問句的探索與連接詞的轉折（模式 A）
**文本**：Sandra Cisneros, *The House on Mango Street* — "Edna's Ruthie" (後半段：從 "There were many things..." 到結尾)
**前堂**：Week 8-4 (Meet Ruthie - 第一印象)
**本堂定位**：撥開 Ruthie 表面的「怪異」，進入她深層的悲劇底色。學生從文本的細節中，發現 Ruthie 其實擁有正常大人的條件（有房子、結過婚、有過工作邀約），但她卻選擇「退化」成小孩。透過間接問句（為什麼她要住在這裡？她是否會離開？），深入討論大人的「卡關」。

---

## 發展對應

九年級學生開始面對未來的選擇與壓力。他們恐懼自己長大後也會「失敗」或「卡住」。Ruthie 是一個很好的投射對象：她擁有選擇權，卻選擇了逃避。帶領學生不帶批判地看見成人世界的複雜與軟弱，是幫助他們放下非黑即白（對錯二元）思維的關鍵。

---

## ⏱ 時間提醒

> **45 分鐘核心節奏：回顧前堂（3m）→ 後半段共讀（15m）→ 偵探任務：Ruthie 的反差（15m）→ 懸念討論與收尾（12m）。**
>
> 雖然有文法重點（間接問句），但不要變成文法課。用間接問句來包裝「我們對 Ruthie 感到的困惑」。

## 課程流程

### Part 1：回顧前堂（3 分鐘）

**教師問：**
> "Last time, we drew Ruthie. What do we remember about her?"
> "Does she act like an adult?" (No, she plays, she acts like a wild animal.)

> "Today we read the second half. And we will find out... she is not a child. She has a very adult past."

---

### Part 2：共讀與文本深研（15 分鐘）

**發下學習單。請學生翻到文本後半段（從 "There were many things Ruthie could have been..." 開始）。**

**教師領讀重點段落，並請學生畫線（Detective）**：
> "Listen carefully. Ruthie had a life before Mango Street. What did she have?"

（學生聽並可能指出：she got married, she has a pretty house, job offers.）

🎤 **教師朗誦結尾（The Walrus and the Carpenter 橋段）**：
用平緩但帶著遺憾的語氣朗誦最後一段，特別停頓在：
> *"She took a long time looking at me before she opened her mouth, and then she said, You have the most beautiful teeth I have ever seen, and went inside."*

**引導感受（心）：**
> "Esperanza memorizes a beautiful, sad poem for her. Does Ruthie talk about the poem?"
> "No, she talks about teeth. Why? What does this tell us about Ruthie?" (Maybe she can't face deep emotions. Maybe she changes the subject to escape.)

---

### Part 3：偵探任務：Ruthie 的反差（分析與文法）（15 分鐘）

進入學習單的這部分。

**1. 對比真相（利用連接詞 But/Because）**
教師指向黑板，並帶領學生看學習單 Tasks。
- Ruthie 有很多工作機會，**but** 她從來不接受。
- 她有自己的真實房子，**but** 她睡在媽媽家客廳的沙發上。
- 她保證下週要去看牙醫，**but** 當下週到了，她沒有去。

> "What do you notice? What word keeps appearing?" (But)
> "Ruthie's life is full of 'buts'. She has the ability, BUT she doesn't do it."

**2. 這個謎團（間接問句）**
> "Because of all these 'buts', Esperanza has questions. We also have questions."

引導學生用 `I don't understand + 疑問詞 / if` 來表達對 Ruthie 的困惑：
- "Why is Ruthie living on Mango Street?" -> *I don't understand **why Ruthie is living on Mango Street** if she doesn't have to.*
- "Will her husband take her home?" -> *I wonder **if her husband will ever take her home**.*

(入門層填空，挑戰層自由造句。)

---

### Part 4：懸念討論與對齊（12 分鐘）

**教師問（開放式討論，不下定論）：**
> "We don't know the exact answer. Why do you think she stays? Why doesn't she go back to her own house?"

可以接受學生的任何回答（Maybe she is sick, maybe her husband hit her, maybe she is just scared to be a real adult）。

**教師收尾：**
> "Sometimes, adults get stuck. They have choices, but they can't make them. They have houses, but they can't live in them. Ruthie is physically an adult, but inside, she is hiding."

> "Remember Papa? He was a strong adult, but he cried like a kid when his father died. Adults are not always strong. Next class, we will put Papa and Ruthie together... on a stage."
