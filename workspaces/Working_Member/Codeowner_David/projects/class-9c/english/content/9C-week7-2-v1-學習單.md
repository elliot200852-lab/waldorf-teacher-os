## Part 0: Correlative Connectors / 配對連接詞

These connectors always come in **pairs**.

| Pair | 中文 | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| **both ... and** | 兩者都 | Both the story and the poem talk about choices. |
| **either ... or** | 不是...就是 | Either you stick to the plan, or you come up with a new one. |
| **neither ... nor** | 兩者都不 | Neither the rain nor the cold stopped them. |
| **not only ... but also** | 不僅...而且 | She is not only smart but also kind. |

**both...and** and **either...or** --> try using these!
**neither...nor** and **not only...but also** --> just recognize them in reading.
