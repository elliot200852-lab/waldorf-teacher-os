## Part 2: Connector Fill-in / 連接詞填空

Choose the correct connector for each blank. Each connector may be used more than once.

**Connector bank:** because / if / when / after / although / even though / since / until

**1.** The special kids get to eat in the canteen ____________ their mothers aren't home.

**2.** I got it in my head to ask my mother for a sandwich ____________ I saw the other kids eating in the canteen.

**3.** ____________ Nenny could eat at school, she didn't want to — she preferred going to Gloria's house.

**4.** My mother finally said okay ____________ three days of listening to my arguments.

**5.** Everything was fine ____________ the nun looked at me and asked who sent me.

**6.** ____________ I was too shy to talk, I just held out the letter.

**7.** I nodded yes ____________ I knew that wasn't really my house.

**8.** ____________ the canteen was nothing special, lots of kids watched me eat my cold rice sandwich.
