# Phrasal Verb Review & Quiz — Week 6 (Teacher's Key)

---

## Part 1: Speed Sort — 參考答案

| "Make / Create" | "Destroy / Remove" | "Body / Daily life" | "Control / Manage" |
|:----------------|:-------------------|:--------------------|:-------------------|
| 1 (think up) | 2 (throw away) | 3 (stay up) | 4 (switch off) |
| 6 (talk into) | 10 (wear out) | 5 (wash up) | 7 (split into) |
| | | 8 (watch out) | 9 (stick to) |

**彈性說明：**
- think up（想出）→ Make / Create
- talk into（說服某人去做）→ Make / Create（使某事發生）或 Control / Manage 皆可接受
- wear out（用壞/精疲力竭）→ Destroy / Remove
- watch out（小心）→ Body / Daily life
- stick to（堅持計畫）→ Control / Manage
- split into（分成幾組）→ Control / Manage

學生若能合理解釋為何放在另一欄，即為正確。

## Part 2: After the Quiz — 參考答案

### Task A: Mini-Dialogue Writer — 範例

> A: We need to **think up** a theme for our school fair booth.
>
> B: How about a tea stand? But we should **stick to** a simple menu.
>
> A: Good idea. Can you **talk** Jenny **into** helping us make the signs?
>
> B: Sure — I'll ask her after class.

（學生使用任何 #175–#200 範圍內的片語，語境合理即可。底線標記 3 個以上。）

---

### Task D: Picture Match — 答案

1. It's 1:00 AM. You are still reading a comic book. → **stay up**
2. Your room is a mess. Mom is coming home. → **tidy up**
3. A car is coming too fast near the school. → **watch out** (也可接受 slow down)
4. You're about to run a race. You stretch first. → **warm up**
5. The movie is over. You press the button on the remote. → **switch off**
6. Your friend is talking too fast. You can't understand. → **slow down**

**注意：** 第 3 題 watch out 和 slow down 都合理——watch out 是對旁觀者的警告，slow down 是對駕駛的要求。兩者皆給分。
