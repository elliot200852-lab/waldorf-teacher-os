## Part 2: Connector Fill-in 答案

**1.** The special kids get to eat in the canteen **because** their mothers aren't home.

**2.** I got it in my head to ask my mother for a sandwich **after / when** I saw the other kids eating in the canteen.

**3.** **Although / Even though** Nenny could eat at school, she didn't want to — she preferred going to Gloria's house.

**4.** My mother finally said okay **after** three days of listening to my arguments.

**5.** Everything was fine **until** the nun looked at me and asked who sent me.

**6.** **Since / Because** I was too shy to talk, I just held out the letter.

**7.** I nodded yes **even though** I knew that wasn't really my house.

**8.** **Although** the canteen was nothing special, lots of kids watched me eat my cold rice sandwich.

---

## 評分彈性

| 題號 | 最佳答案 | 亦可接受 | 說明 |
|:-----|:--------|:---------|:-----|
| 2 | after | when | after 強調順序，when 強調時間點，皆合理 |
| 3 | although | even though | 語意相同，even though 語氣更強 |
| 5 | until | — | 此題只有 until 正確（一切都很順利「直到」...） |
| 6 | since | because | 皆表原因，since 較文學感 |
| 7 | even though | although | even though 更貼合語境（明知不是卻點頭），although 稍弱但可接受 |
| 8 | although | even though | 皆可 |

**整體評分：** 每題 1 分，共 8 分。接受替代答案者給滿分。
