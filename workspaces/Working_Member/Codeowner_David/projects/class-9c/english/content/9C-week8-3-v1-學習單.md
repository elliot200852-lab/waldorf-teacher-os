---

## 關係代名詞速查 Quick Reference

```
who   → 人        The old man [who said it was his birthday]
which → 東西/地方  The shop [which was on North Broadway]
that  → 都可以     The dress [that made me look older]

功能：在名詞後面加「標籤」，告訴你這是什麼樣的人/東西
中文：「的」→ 那個說今天是他生日[的]老人
```

### 📖 Task D — 圈選辨認 Find & Circle

Read each sentence. **Circle** the relative pronoun (who / which / that) and **underline** the noun it describes.

**Example:**
- I wore the dress <u>**that**</u> made me look older. → describes: _dress_

1. Esperanza met an old man ··who·· said it was his birthday.
   → describes: ______________

   → describes: ______________

3. The dress ··that·· she wore was navy blue.
   → describes: ______________

4. Aunt Lala was the person ··who·· found her the job.
   → describes: ______________

5. The envelopes ··that·· she sorted came from everywhere.
   → describes: ______________

6. She met a man ··who·· seemed very kind.
   → describes: ______________

7. The coatroom ··which·· was at the back of the building was dark.
   → describes: ______________

8. The lie ··that·· she told was about her age.
   → describes: ______________

9. Esperanza had a mother ··who·· didn't want her to work.
   → describes: ______________

10. The birthday cake ··which·· he mentioned was probably not real.
    → describes: ______________

**提示 Hint：** 圈出 who/which/that，然後往前看——它前面的那個名詞就是它在描述的對象。

### 🔎 Task C — 合併句子 Combine Two into One

Combine each pair of sentences using **who**, **which**, or **that**. Write the combined sentence on the line.

**Example:**
- I met an old man. + He said it was his birthday.
- → I met an old man **who** said it was his birthday.

1. Esperanza wore a dress. + The dress made her look older.
   → _______________________________________________________________

2. She went to a building. + The building was on North Broadway.
   → _______________________________________________________________

3. She met a man. + The man worked in the same place.
   → _______________________________________________________________

4. Aunt Lala told her to lie. + Aunt Lala was her aunt.
   → _______________________________________________________________

5. The envelopes came from somewhere. + The envelopes needed to be sorted.
   → _______________________________________________________________

6. Esperanza ate lunch alone. + The lunch was a sandwich from home.
   → _______________________________________________________________

7. The coatroom was dark. + The coatroom was at the back of the shop.
   → _______________________________________________________________

8. She had a feeling. + The feeling stayed with her all day.
   → _______________________________________________________________

**提示 Hint：**
- 合併的對象是「人」→ 用 **who**
- 合併的對象是「東西/地方」→ 用 **which** 或 **that**
