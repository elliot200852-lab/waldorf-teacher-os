# Meet Ruthie — 圖像建立與表面特徵

## 教師教案（Edna's Ruthie 第一堂）

**年級**：九年級 ｜ **時長**：45 分鐘 ｜ **語言焦點**：關係代名詞 (who) 與動詞片語的有機察覺（模式 A）
**文本**：Sandra Cisneros, *The House on Mango Street* — "Edna's Ruthie" (第一至第四段)
**前堂**：Week 8-3
**本堂定位**：春季課程的最終文本。帶領學生從視覺上「看見」Ruthie 這個角色，透過她與環境的格格不入（動詞片語），以及她與常人的差異（關係代名詞描述），初步建構這個像小孩一樣的大人圖像。

---

## 發展對應

九年級正處於青春期星芒體強烈運作的階段，他們對「大人」有一套既定的成見：大人應該負責、大人總是生氣、大人高高在上。Edna 代表傳統成見（screaming, throwing out），而 Ruthie 則是這套成見的破口（only grown-up who likes to play）。本節課透過「視覺化」這兩個角色的反差，讓學生在圖像中感受到世界的另一種可能性。

---

## ⏱ 時間提醒

> **45 分鐘核心節奏：聆聽建立（5m）→ 共讀與尋找特徵（15m）→ 繪製 Ruthie（20m）→ 展示與收尾（5m）。**
>
> 繪製時間 (Draw Ruthie) 需要 20 分鐘讓學生進入意志與手部活動。不要在文法拆解上花太多時間，模式 A 以「在語境中看見」為主。

## 課程流程

### Part 1：開場（聲音建立）（5 分鐘）

**教師說：**
> "Today we meet a new neighbor on Mango Street. In your life, do you know any adults who still want to play like a kid?"

🎤 **教師朗誦**（只讀第一段，學生先不看文本）：
> *"Ruthie, tall skinny lady with red lipstick and blue babushka, one blue sock and one green because she forgot, is the only grown-up we know who likes to play..."*

**教師問：**
> "What colors did you hear?" (紅、藍、綠)
> "What did she forget?" (襪子顏色不一樣)

---

### Part 2：共讀與偵探任務（15 分鐘）

**發下文本與學習單。**

**教師說：**
> "Now let's read the first four paragraphs together."

（學生輪流接力朗讀 2-4 段，或由教師帶領合唱朗讀重點句子。）

**🔎 分析與文法導入（模式 A 有機整合）**：
請學生看學習單上的【Detective Task】。不需要教文法結構公式。

**教師說：**
> "If we want to know WHO they are, we look at the word 'who'." (指出文本中的 who)
> "Underline the sentence describing Edna. Underline the sentence describing Ruthie."

**引導比較：**
- **Edna**: "the lady **who owns the big building**" (她擁有這棟建築，象徵權力)
- **Ruthie**: "the only grown-up **who likes to play**" (她喜歡玩，象徵純真與不用負責)

**動詞片語：**
> "But what do they *do*? Let's find their actions."
請學生圈出帶有 up/out/around 等介系詞的動詞動作：
- Edna: **threw out** a pregnant lady (殘酷)
- Ruthie: **takes** her dog **for a walk**, keeps **looking around** (像是初次進屋的野生動物)

> "Look at these actions. Does Ruthie act like a normal 30-year-old woman?"

---

### Part 3：動手做：Draw Ruthie（20 分鐘）

這是一個將文字轉化為圖像（HANDS）的環節，確保有意志層面的投入。

**教師說：**
> "We have all the details. Red lipstick, blue babushka, mismatched socks. Now, I want you to draw her. But don't just draw a person. Draw her IN HER WORLD."

> "Is she in front of Edna's big building? Is she with Bobo the dog? Is she looking around like a wild animal?"

- **A/C 類學生（高分組）**：在畫的背面，用剛剛找出的關係代名詞句型，寫一句話描述自己的畫（例如：*This is Ruthie, who is looking around nervously.*）。
- **B/D 類學生（低分組）**：確保畫作包含文本提到的視覺特徵，並在背面抄寫最喜歡的一句文本。

**教師巡迴（Coach 不下指導棋）：**
- 「What is a babushka? It's a headscarf.」
- 「Where is the dog?」
- 「You drew her smiling, does she look happy when she goes into stores?」

---

### Part 4：展示與收尾預告（5 分鐘）

**展示：**
請 2-3 名學生舉起畫作展示，讀出他們背面的句子。

**教師收尾：**
> "So this is Ruthie. She's Edna's daughter. She doesn't have to worry about rent, she doesn't have to worry about rules. Edna kicks out everyone, but she can't throw out Ruthie."

> "It sounds like a perfect, stress-free life, right?"

（停頓）

> "But in the second half of the story... we find out Ruthie has a secret. She is not a teenager. She is married, and she has her own house. Why is she sleeping on her mother's couch?"

> "Next time, we will find out."
