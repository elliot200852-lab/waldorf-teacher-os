# Phrasal Verb Quiz — 動詞片語測驗

**班級**：九年級 C 班
**週次**：第六週
**用途**：寒假作業動詞片語講義驗收測驗
**版本**：基礎版（三選一）＋ 進階版（四選一），共 20 題

---

## 基礎版（三選一）

Choose the best answer to fill in the blank.

**1.** We need to ______ a plan for the school trip. Any ideas?
　(A) think over　(B) think up　(C) talk over

**2.** Please ______ the jacket before you buy it. The size might be wrong.
　(A) try out　(B) try on　(C) wear out

**3.** ______ for the wet floor — someone just mopped it.
　(A) Warm up　(B) Wake up　(C) Watch out

**4.** He ______ me ______ going to the concert, even though I had homework.
　(A) talked ... into　(B) talked ... over　(C) split ... into

**5.** Can we ______ the project details before the meeting tomorrow?
　(A) talk over　(B) talk into　(C) think up

**6.** I ______ all night to finish my report. Now I can't stop yawning.
　(A) stayed out　(B) stayed up　(C) woke up

**7.** Mom said, "______ before dinner — your hands are dirty!"
　(A) Warm up　(B) Tidy up　(C) Wash up

**8.** The class will ______ three groups for the science experiment.
　(A) split up　(B) split into　(C) switch off

**9.** Please ______ the light. It's too dark in here.
　(A) switch off　(B) switch on　(C) throw away

**10.** Let me ______ it ______ for a few days before I give you an answer.
　(A) think ... up　(B) think ... over　(C) talk ... into

**11.** Don't ______ these old books — we can donate them.
　(A) use up　(B) throw away　(C) tidy up

**12.** If you have a question, please ______ so the teacher can hear you.
　(A) speak for　(B) slow down　(C) speak up

**13.** ______ me ______ at six tomorrow. I have an early bus.
　(A) Wake ... up　(B) Warm ... up　(C) Wash ... up

**14.** We ______ all the paper. Can someone bring more from the office?
　(A) wore out　(B) threw away　(C) used up

**15.** ______ your room before your grandma comes, please.
　(A) Throw away　(B) Tidy up　(C) Try on

**16.** ______, you're driving too fast! The speed limit is 50.
　(A) Slow down　(B) Stay out　(C) Switch off

**17.** No matter what happens, I'll ______ you. That's what friends do.
　(A) stick to　(B) stick with　(C) split up

**18.** I ran so much that my new shoes are already ______.
　(A) used up　(B) thrown away　(C) worn out

**19.** Please ______ your phones during class. No distractions.
　(A) switch on　(B) switch off　(C) throw away

**20.** Let's do some stretches and ______ before the race starts.
　(A) warm up　(B) wake up　(C) wash up

## 進階版（四選一）

Choose the best answer to fill in the blank.

**1.** We had a long discussion, but in the end she ______ me ______ changing my topic.
　(A) talked ... over　(B) talked ... into　(C) thought ... over　(D) split ... into

**2.** The coach told us to ______ three teams and practice in different areas.
　(A) split up　(B) stick with　(C) split into　(D) switch off

**3.** "Don't panic — just ______ and tell me exactly what happened."
　(A) speak for　(B) slow down　(C) stay out　(D) switch off

**4.** I want to ______ this new recipe before the cooking contest next week.
　(A) try on　(B) try out　(C) tidy up　(D) think up

**5.** He was so sick this morning that he ______ right after breakfast.
　(A) wore out　(B) used up　(C) threw up　(D) woke up

**6.** The teacher said, "If you don't agree, ______ and share your opinion."
　(A) speak for　(B) speak up　(C) slow down　(D) stay out

**7.** We ______ all the glue sticks during art class. There's nothing left.
　(A) threw away　(B) wore out　(C) tidied up　(D) used up

**8.** You really need to ______ your plan. Don't keep changing it every day.
　(A) stick to　(B) stick with　(C) split up　(D) switch on

**9.** It's midnight — why are you still ______? You have school tomorrow!
　(A) staying out　(B) staying up　(C) waking up　(D) watching out

**10.** Let's ______ the advantages and disadvantages before we make a final decision.
　(A) think up　(B) talk into　(C) talk over　(D) throw away

**11.** ______! Someone left a skateboard on the stairs.
　(A) Wake up　(B) Warm up　(C) Slow down　(D) Watch out

**12.** My alarm didn't go off, so nobody ______ me ______ this morning.
　(A) woke ... up　(B) warmed ... up　(C) washed ... up　(D) wore ... out

**13.** I don't ______ the whole class — this is just my personal view.
　(A) speak up　(B) speak for　(C) talk over　(D) talk into

**14.** ______ the TV when you leave. Don't waste electricity.
　(A) Switch on　(B) Throw away　(C) Switch off　(D) Slow down

**15.** Who ______ the idea of having a movie night every Friday?
　(A) thought over　(B) thought up　(C) talked over　(D) tried out

**16.** These batteries are completely ______. We need to buy new ones.
　(A) thrown away　(B) used up　(C) worn out　(D) tidied up

**17.** This is between me and her — please ______ it.
　(A) stick to　(B) stay up　(C) stay out of　(D) speak for

**18.** Don't ______ that plastic bag. We can use it again.
　(A) tidy up　(B) throw away　(C) use up　(D) try out

**19.** Go ______ — dinner is almost ready. Just wash your hands.
　(A) tidy up　(B) warm up　(C) wash up　(D) wake up

**20.** Three hours of hiking in the sun completely ______ the children ______. They all fell asleep in the car.
　(A) used ... up　(B) threw ... away　(C) wore ... out　(D) woke ... up

### 進階版答案

| 題號 | 答案 | 片語 | 解析 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| 1 | (B) | talk into | talk over 是討論、think over 是考慮、split into 是分組。talk into 是說服某人做某事。 |
| 2 | (C) | split into | split up 是分手/分開行動、stick with 是持續支持、switch off 是關掉。split into 是分成幾組。 |
| 3 | (B) | slow down | speak for 是代表發言、stay out 是待在外面、switch off 是關掉。slow down 是慢下來冷靜。 |
| 4 | (B) | try out | try on 是試穿衣服、tidy up 是整理、think up 是想出。try out 是試用新事物。 |
| 5 | (C) | throw up | wore out 是用壞、used up 是用光、woke up 是醒來。throw up 是嘔吐。 |
| 6 | (B) | speak up | speak for 代表發言、slow down 慢下來、stay out 待在外面。speak up 是大聲說出自己的意見。 |
| 7 | (D) | use up | threw away 丟掉（主動丟棄）、wore out 用壞（磨損）、tidied up 整理。use up 是用光消耗品。 |
| 8 | (A) | stick to | stick to 強調堅持計畫/規則不改變。stick with 偏向持續做某事或支持某人。split up 分手、switch on 打開。 |
| 9 | (B) | stay up | staying out 是待在外面不回家、waking up 是醒來、watching out 是小心。stay up 是熬夜。 |
| 10 | (C) | talk over | think up 想出新點子、talk into 說服、throw away 丟掉。talk over 是討論商量。 |
| 11 | (D) | watch out | wake up 醒來、warm up 暖身、slow down 慢下來。watch out 是小心警告。 |
| 12 | (A) | wake up | warm up 暖身、wash up 洗手、wore out 用壞。wake up 是叫醒。 |
| 13 | (B) | speak for | speak up 大聲說、talk over 討論、talk into 說服。speak for 是代表某人發言。 |
| 14 | (C) | switch off | switch on 打開、throw away 丟掉、slow down 慢下來。switch off 是關掉電器。 |
| 15 | (B) | think up | thought over 仔細考慮、talked over 討論、tried out 試用。think up 是想出新點子。 |
| 16 | (C) | worn out | thrown away 是被丟掉、used up 是用光（消耗品）、tidied up 是被整理。worn out 強調磨損用壞。 |
| 17 | (C) | stay out of | stick to 堅持、stay up 熬夜、speak for 代表發言。stay out of 是不要介入。 |
| 18 | (B) | throw away | tidy up 整理、use up 用光、try out 試用。throw away 是丟掉。 |
| 19 | (C) | wash up | tidy up 整理房間、warm up 暖身、wake up 醒來。wash up 是洗手洗臉。 |
| 20 | (C) | wear out | used up 用光消耗品、threw away 丟掉、woke up 醒來。wear out 是使精疲力竭。 |

**出題範圍**：講義 #175–#200（26 個片語），本次測驗涵蓋 20 個作為正確答案。
**未作為正確答案但出現為幹擾項**：split up、stay out (of)（進階版第 17 題為正確答案）、try on、switch on。
**補充說明**：throw up 僅出現於進階版第 5 題；stick with 與 stick to 分別在基礎版和進階版各出一題，測試學生區辨兩者的能力。
