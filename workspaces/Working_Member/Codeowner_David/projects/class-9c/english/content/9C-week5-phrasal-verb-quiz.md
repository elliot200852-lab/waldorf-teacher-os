# Phrasal Verb Quiz — 動詞片語測驗

**班級**：九年級 C 班
**週次**：第五週
**用途**：寒假作業動詞片語講義驗收測驗
**版本**：基礎版（三選一）＋ 進階版（四選一），共 25 題

---

## 基礎版（三選一）

Choose the best answer to fill in the blank.

**1.** After months of extra practice, her effort finally ______. She got the best grade in class.
　(A) paid back　(B) paid off　(C) passed out

**2.** I can't ______ to the school website. I forgot my password.
　(A) log out　(B) sign up　(C) log in

**3.** ______ a second! I need to tie my shoes before we go.
　(A) Hang up　(B) Hang on　(C) Hang out

**4.** Did you ______ for the school trip yet? Today is the last day.
　(A) sign in　(B) sign up　(C) join in

**5.** The teacher said, "Let's ______ today's lesson with a song."
　(A) kick in　(B) knock out　(C) kick off

**6.** It was so hot that one runner ______ near the finish line.
　(A) passed away　(B) passed out　(C) moved in

**7.** Don't look back. It's time to ______ and try new things.
　(A) move in　(B) move on　(C) hang on

**8.** That long hike really ______ me ______. I fell asleep at eight.
　(A) knocked ... out　(B) shut ... down　(C) laid ... off

**9.** The factory ______ 50 workers because business was bad.
　(A) kicked off　(B) laid off　(C) shut out

**10.** The old bookstore ______ last month. Now it's empty.
　(A) signed in　(B) logged out　(C) shut down

**11.** Can I ______ your card game? It looks fun!
　(A) join in　(B) kick off　(C) sign up

**12.** We like to ______ at the park after school on Fridays.
　(A) hang up　(B) hang on　(C) hang out

**13.** The dark clouds ______ a big storm tonight.
　(A) point out　(B) point to　(C) pay off

**14.** She is ______ for a new phone. She already has three thousand dollars.
　(A) paying back　(B) saving up　(C) ringing up

**15.** The cashier will ______ your items at the counter.
　(A) ring up　(B) sign in　(C) point out

**16.** I borrowed a hundred dollars from Tom. I need to ______ him ______ today.
　(A) pay ... off　(B) pay ... back　(C) save ... up

**17.** Remember to ______ when you leave the computer lab.
　(A) log in　(B) shut out　(C) log out

**18.** My cousin will ______ to her new apartment this weekend.
　(A) move on　(B) move in　(C) hang out

**19.** Mom was still talking, but I accidentally ______ the phone.
　(A) hung up　(B) hung on　(C) hung out

**20.** Stop ______ — the teacher is looking right at us!
　(A) joking around　(B) kicking off　(C) pointing out

**21.** His pet dog ______ last year. He still misses it every day.
　(A) passed out　(B) shut down　(C) passed away

**22.** The medicine will ______ in about thirty minutes. Just rest.
　(A) kick off　(B) kick in　(C) knock out

**23.** Thank you for ______ my mistake. I didn't notice it at all.
　(A) pointing out　(B) pointing to　(C) shutting out

**24.** Don't ______ your classmates. Everyone should have a chance to speak.
　(A) shut down　(B) lay off　(C) shut out

**25.** Please ______ at the front desk before the meeting starts.
　(A) sign up　(B) log in　(C) sign in

---

### 基礎版答案

| 題號 | 答案 | 片語 | 解析 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| 1 | (B) | pay off | 努力有回報。pay back 是還錢，passed out 是昏倒。 |
| 2 | (C) | log in | 登入網站。log out 是登出，sign up 是報名。 |
| 3 | (B) | hang on | 等一下。hang up 是掛電話，hang out 是閒晃。 |
| 4 | (B) | sign up | 報名活動。sign in 是簽到，join in 是加入進行中的活動。 |
| 5 | (C) | kick off | 開始活動。kick in 是開始起作用，knock out 是擊倒。 |
| 6 | (B) | pass out | 昏倒。pass away 是去世，moved in 是搬進。 |
| 7 | (B) | move on | 繼續前進、放下過去。move in 是搬進，hang on 是等一下。 |
| 8 | (A) | knock out | 累垮（口語）。shut down 是關閉，laid off 是裁員。 |
| 9 | (B) | lay off | 裁員。kick off 是開始，shut out 是排除。 |
| 10 | (C) | shut down | 關閉停止運作。signed in 是簽到，logged out 是登出。 |
| 11 | (A) | join in | 加入進行中的活動。kick off 是開始，sign up 是報名。 |
| 12 | (C) | hang out | 閒晃聚在一起。hang up 是掛電話，hang on 是等一下。 |
| 13 | (B) | point to | 指向、顯示。point out 是指出錯誤，pay off 是有回報。 |
| 14 | (B) | save up | 存錢。pay back 是還錢，ring up 是結帳。 |
| 15 | (A) | ring up | 結帳。sign in 是簽到，point out 是指出。 |
| 16 | (B) | pay back | 還錢給某人。pay off 是還清或有回報，save up 是存錢。 |
| 17 | (C) | log out | 登出。log in 是登入，shut out 是排除。 |
| 18 | (B) | move in | 搬進。move on 是繼續前進，hang out 是閒晃。 |
| 19 | (A) | hang up | 掛電話。hung on 是等一下，hung out 是閒晃。 |
| 20 | (A) | joke around | 開玩笑胡鬧。kick off 是開始，point out 是指出。 |
| 21 | (C) | pass away | 去世（委婉說法）。pass out 是昏倒，shut down 是關閉。 |
| 22 | (B) | kick in | 開始起作用。kick off 是開始活動，knock out 是擊倒。 |
| 23 | (A) | point out | 指出錯誤。point to 是指向，shut out 是排除。 |
| 24 | (C) | shut out | 排除在外。shut down 是關閉，lay off 是裁員。 |
| 25 | (C) | sign in | 簽到登記。sign up 是報名，log in 是線上登入。 |

## 進階版（四選一）

Choose the best answer to fill in the blank.

**1.** She studied every night for two months, and it finally ______ — she passed the test.
　(A) paid back　(B) paid off　(C) kicked in　(D) knocked out

**2.** You need to ______ with your student account before you can check your grades online.
　(A) sign up　(B) sign in　(C) log in　(D) log out

**3.** "______ — I think I dropped my key somewhere back there."
　(A) Hang out　(B) Hang up　(C) Move on　(D) Hang on

**4.** If you want to take the cooking class, you have to ______ before Friday.
　(A) sign in　(B) sign up　(C) log in　(D) join in

**5.** The host smiled and said, "Let's ______ this year's talent show with a dance!"
　(A) kick in　(B) knock out　(C) kick off　(D) join in

**6.** She skipped breakfast and ______ during P.E. class. The nurse came right away.
　(A) passed away　(B) passed out　(C) knocked out　(D) laid off

**7.** He finally decided to ______ and stop worrying about what happened last year.
　(A) move in　(B) move on　(C) hang on　(D) log out

**8.** The three-hour basketball game completely ______ the whole team ______. Everyone went straight to bed.
　(A) knocked ... out　(B) kicked ... off　(C) shut ... down　(D) laid ... off

**9.** The company ______ many workers last month, so several families in town lost their income.
　(A) shut out　(B) knocked out　(C) kicked off　(D) laid off

**10.** The school website ______ for two hours yesterday because of a technical problem.
　(A) logged out　(B) shut down　(C) signed in　(D) hung up

**11.** "We're playing volleyball in the gym — do you want to ______?"
　(A) sign up　(B) kick off　(C) join in　(D) hang out

**12.** On weekends, I usually ______ with my friends at the night market and eat a lot.
　(A) hang on　(B) hang up　(C) join in　(D) hang out

**13.** The broken windows and the dusty floor both ______ one thing: nobody has lived here for a long time.
　(A) point out　(B) point to　(C) shut out　(D) pay off

**14.** Tom is ______ for a trip to Japan. He puts five hundred dollars in his piggy bank every month.
　(A) paying back　(B) paying off　(C) saving up　(D) ringing up

**15.** The clerk will ______ your things at the counter. Please wait in line.
　(A) point out　(B) sign in　(C) ring up　(D) pay back

**16.** I still owe Lisa two hundred dollars. I should ______ her ______ before the weekend.
　(A) pay ... off　(B) save ... up　(C) ring ... up　(D) pay ... back

**17.** Always ______ of the app when you use a shared tablet at school.
　(A) shut down　(B) log out　(C) sign up　(D) log in

**18.** They are going to ______ to the apartment next to the park next month.
　(A) move on　(B) hang out　(C) move in　(D) sign in

**19.** She was so angry that she ______ the phone without even saying goodbye.
　(A) hung out　(B) hung on　(C) knocked out　(D) hung up

**20.** "Please stop ______. This is a serious discussion and we don't have much time."
　(A) joking around　(B) kicking off　(C) hanging out　(D) pointing to

**21.** Our neighbor's old cat ______ last winter. The whole family was very sad for weeks.
　(A) passed out　(B) shut down　(C) laid off　(D) passed away

**22.** Give it a few more minutes — the cold medicine should ______ soon.
　(A) kick off　(B) kick in　(C) pay off　(D) knock out

**23.** My friend ______ that I was wearing two different socks this morning. How embarrassing!
　(A) pointed to　(B) shut out　(C) pointed out　(D) saved up

**24.** It's not fair to ______ the quieter students from the group discussion. Let them speak too.
　(A) shut down　(B) lay off　(C) kick off　(D) shut out

**25.** All visitors must ______ at the guard's desk and get a name tag before entering.
　(A) sign up　(B) log in　(C) sign in　(D) join in

### 進階版答案

| 題號 | 答案 | 片語 | 解析 |
|:-----|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| 1 | (B) | pay off | pay back 還錢、kick in 起作用、knock out 擊倒。pay off 表示努力得到回報。 |
| 2 | (C) | log in | sign up 是第一次報名、sign in 是現場簽到、log out 是登出。log in 是線上登入。 |
| 3 | (D) | hang on | hang out 閒晃、hang up 掛電話、move on 往前走。hang on 是等一下。 |
| 4 | (B) | sign up | sign in 現場簽到、log in 線上登入、join in 加入進行中的活動。sign up 是事前報名。 |
| 5 | (C) | kick off | kick in 藥效發作、knock out 擊倒、join in 中途加入。kick off 是開始活動。 |
| 6 | (B) | pass out | passed away 去世、knocked out 擊倒（他動）、laid off 裁員。pass out 是昏倒。 |
| 7 | (B) | move on | move in 搬進、hang on 等一下、log out 登出。move on 是放下繼續前進。 |
| 8 | (A) | knock out | kicked off 開始、shut down 關閉、laid off 裁員。knock out 口語表示累垮。 |
| 9 | (D) | lay off | shut out 排除、knocked out 擊倒、kicked off 開始。lay off 是裁員。 |
| 10 | (B) | shut down | logged out 個人登出、signed in 簽到、hung up 掛電話。shut down 是系統關閉。 |
| 11 | (C) | join in | sign up 是事前報名、kick off 開始、hang out 閒晃。join in 是加入進行中的活動。 |
| 12 | (D) | hang out | hang on 等一下、hang up 掛電話、join in 加入活動。hang out 是朋友間閒晃。 |
| 13 | (B) | point to | point out 指出錯誤、shut out 排除、pay off 回報。point to 是指向、顯示。 |
| 14 | (C) | save up | paying back 還錢、paying off 還清、ringing up 結帳。save up 是存錢。 |
| 15 | (C) | ring up | point out 指出、sign in 簽到、pay back 還錢。ring up 是結帳。 |
| 16 | (D) | pay back | pay off 還清債務、save up 存錢、ring up 結帳。pay back 是還錢給某人。 |
| 17 | (B) | log out | shut down 關閉整個系統、sign up 報名、log in 登入。log out 是登出。 |

| 19 | (D) | hang up | hung out 閒晃、hung on 等一下、knocked out 擊倒。hang up 是掛電話。 |
| 20 | (A) | joke around | kicking off 開始、hanging out 閒晃、pointing to 指向。joke around 是胡鬧。 |
| 21 | (D) | pass away | passed out 昏倒、shut down 關閉、laid off 裁員。pass away 是去世（委婉）。 |
| 22 | (B) | kick in | kick off 開始活動、pay off 有回報、knock out 擊倒。kick in 是開始起作用。 |
| 23 | (C) | point out | pointed to 指向、shut out 排除、saved up 存錢。point out 是指出錯誤。 |
| 24 | (D) | shut out | shut down 關閉、lay off 裁員、kick off 開始。shut out 是把人排除在外。 |
| 25 | (C) | sign in | sign up 事前報名、log in 線上登入、join in 加入活動。sign in 是現場簽到。 |
