# Reader's Theater: Script B "The Bingo Night"

> **Characters (人物):**
> 1. **Esperanza (E)**: 說書人兼觀察者。
> 2. **Ruthie (R)**: 充滿猶豫，不敢做決定。
> 3. **Edna's Voice (Edna)**: 躲在紗門後面的冷酷聲音 (不需要出現在舞台中心)。
> 4. **Papa (P)**: 身體極度疲勞，剛剛下夜班回家。

**(The scene: Evening. The car motor is running loudly. Ruthie is standing on the steps, looking at the car. Papa walks slowly toward his house, rubbing his eyes in the dark.)**

**E**: It is Friday night. Edna's friends came to visit, and they asked Ruthie **if** she wanted to play bingo. The car motor was running, **but** Ruthie just stood on the steps. 

**R**: *(Looking back at the screen door nervously)* Should I go, Ma? I don't know **what I should do**. The car is waiting...

**Edna**: *(From behind the screen, annoyed)* I don't care! Go **if** you want. 

**R**: *(Looking at the ground)* What do you think, Ma? Should I **go away** with them? 

**Edna**: Do what you want! How should I know?

**E**: The car waited fifteen minutes, and then they left. The lady **who has lots of job offers** couldn't even make a simple decision. 

**P**: *(Walking heavily, almost bumping into Ruthie)* Excuse me. I need to go home **before** I collapse. 

**R**: *(Jumps up)* Oh! You are the man **who wakes up in the dark**, right? You look so tired. The moon is beautiful like a balloon! Do you want to **look around** with me?

**P**: *(Sighs, barely keeping his eyes open)* No, thank you, Ruthie. I don't have the energy. I drank my coffee at 4 AM, and I worked all day. I have a family to feed. 

**R**: But you works too hard! You should play. I am going to see the dentist next week, and my husband is taking me to my real house. I have a house, you know. 

**P**: I wonder **why you stay here**. You have choices. *(He looks at her strangely)* You don't have to carry anything. You just... play. I wish I could play. 

**R**: *(Whispering)* I am scared. 

**P**: *(Doesn't hear her)* What did you say?

**R**: Nothing! I just want to say... you have the most beautiful shoes I have ever seen. *(She runs back to her couch)*

**E**: My Papa **who never stops working** looks at Ruthie **who never starts living**. They are both adults, **but** they are trapped in different ways. 

**P**: *(Shakes his head slowly)* Está loco... (He's crazy / This is crazy). *(He slowly opens the door to go to sleep)*

**(End of Script B)**
