# Reader's Theater: Script A "The Broken Umbrella"

> **Characters (人物):**
> 1. **Narrator (N)**: 說書人，聲音清晰。
> 2. **Papa (P)**: 剛得知父親過世，非常疲憊與悲傷。聲音低沉。
> 3. **Ruthie (R)**: Edna 的女兒，行為像小孩。說話輕快跳躍。
> 4. **Esperanza (E)**: 觀察者。

**(The scene: A rainy street corner on Mango Street. Papa is walking slowly. Ruthie is standing in the rain, looking at the sky.)**

**N**: It is early morning. The man **who usually wakes up in the dark** is walking down the street. It is raining, **but** he forgot his umbrella. He looks like a coat that just crumpled to the floor. 

**E**: My Papa just found out his abuelito is dead. I don't know **what I should do**. I just watch him walk away.

**P**: *(Walking slowly, whispering to himself)* I have to buy the plane tickets. I have to tell the uncles... I have to be strong **because** I am the oldest. 

**N**: Soon, he sees a woman on the corner. It is Ruthie. The only grown-up **who likes to play**. 

**R**: *(Looking up, catching raindrops)* One, two, three... The clouds look like white balloons! Oh, look! That one looks like Marlon Brando!

**P**: *(Stops, wiping rain from his face)* Excuse me, miss. You are getting wet. You should go inside **before** you catch a cold. 

**R**: *(Turns her head, smiling)* But the rain is beautiful! My husband is going to take me to a real house this weekend. **If** he comes, I will show him the rain. 

**P**: *(Looks confused)* But... you live in Edna's building. Are you okay? I wonder **why you are standing here alone**. 

**R**: Because my teeth hurt! Do you like candy? I have some soft candy. You look so sad. Did someone **throw** your duck **out**? 

**P**: *(Smiles a little, looking very tired)* No. No duck. My father... he passed away. I have to take care of everything. But I am just so tired. 

**R**: Books are wonderful! I used to write children's books, did I tell you? *(She runs around him, laughing)* You don't have to take care of everything. Let's trace the raindrops!

**E**: The man **who has to carry the family** looks at the woman **who refuses to grow up**. He doesn't understand  her, and she doesn't understand him. 

**P**: I must **go back**. My children are waiting for me. We have to send the dead away. 

**R**: *(Suddenly stops playing. Looks at his face quietly)* You... have the most beautiful eyes I have ever seen. 

**N**: She turns around and walks into Edna's building. Papa stands in the rain for a moment, and then he slowly walks home, back to the dark room. 

**(End of Script A)**
