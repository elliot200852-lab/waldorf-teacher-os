### Subordinating Connectors 從屬連接詞

| Connector | 中文 | Category |
|-----------|------|----------|
| **if** | 如果 | Condition 條件 |
| **unless** | 除非 | Condition 條件（反面） |
| **because** | 因為 | Cause 原因 |
| **so (that)** | 為了 | Purpose 目的 |
| **when** | 當...的時候 | Time 時間 |
| **before** | 在...之前 | Time 時間 |
| **after** | 在...之後 | Time 時間 |
| **until** | 直到 | Time 時間（終點） |
| **although** | 雖然 | Concession 讓步 |
| **even though** | 即使 | Concession 讓步（更強） |

</div>
<div class="column">

### Coordinating Connectors 對等連接詞

| Connector | 中文 | What it does |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **and** | 和、而且 | addition 添加 |
| **but** | 但是 | contrast 對比 |
| **or** | 或者 | choice 選擇 |
| **so** | 所以 | result 結果 |

> **Notice:** "so" appears in both lists! As a coordinating connector, it means "therefore / 所以." As part of "so that," it becomes subordinating and means "in order to / 為了."

### Task D -- Choose the Correct Connector

Each dialogue has a blank. Two options are given. **Circle** the correct one.

**1.**
> A: Are you going to look into that problem?
> B: Yes, I'll look into it **<span class="answer">when</span>** I have some free time.

( **when** / ~~but~~ ) ✔ when — 時間連接詞，表示「當...的時候」

**2.**
> A: We need to set up the classroom for the presentation.
> B: I know, **<span class="answer">but</span>** we don't have enough chairs.

( ~~until~~ / **but** ) ✔ but — 對等連接詞，表示轉折「但是」

**3.**
> A: Why did they put off the field trip?
> B: They put it off **<span class="answer">because</span>** it was raining all week.

( **because** / ~~or~~ ) ✔ because — 原因連接詞，回答 why

**4.**
> A: Will you carry on with the project?
> B: I'll carry on **<span class="answer">until</span>** the teacher tells us to stop.

( ~~so~~ / **until** ) ✔ until — 時間終點，表示「直到...為止」

**5.**
> A: We'll run out of paper **<span class="answer">if</span>** we don't get more today.
> B: I'll go buy some.

( **if** / ~~so~~ ) ✔ if — 條件連接詞，表示「如果」

### Task C -- Error Correction + Fill in the Blank

**Part A: Error Correction** — The connector in bold is WRONG. Cross it out and write the correct one.

**1.** A: Did you figure out why the computer crashed?
B: Not yet. ~~**Or**~~ I spent two hours looking into it, I still couldn't find the problem.

Correct connector: **<span class="answer">Although / Even though</span>** — 讓步連接詞，表示「雖然花了兩小時，但仍然找不到」。Or 是選擇，語意不通。

**2.** A: He always puts off his homework.
B: I know. ~~**When**~~ he never studies, he somehow passes every test.

Correct connector: **<span class="answer">Although / Even though</span>** — 讓步連接詞，表示「雖然從不唸書，卻還是考過」。When 表示時間，語意不合（他不是「當他不讀書的時候」通過考試，而是「即使不讀書」也能通過）。

**Part B: Fill in the Blank** — Choose the best connector from the full list.

**3.** We need to talk it over **<span class="answer">before</span>** we make a final decision.

✔ before — 時間連接詞，「在做最終決定之前」要先討論

**4.** She carried on working **<span class="answer">although / even though</span>** she was exhausted.

✔ although / even though — 讓步連接詞，「雖然她筋疲力盡」仍繼續工作

### Task B -- Sentence Combining

Combine the two short sentences into ONE sentence using the connector given. You may need to change word order.

**1.** Connector: **unless**

<span class="answer">**We need to set up the equipment early unless we want to run out of time.**</span>
或：Unless we set up the equipment early, we will run out of time.

**2.** Connector: **until**

<span class="answer">**She will carry on practicing piano until she can play the whole song perfectly.**</span>

**3.** Connector: **even though**

<span class="answer">**Even though he put off the assignment for two weeks, he still got a good grade.**</span>
或：He still got a good grade even though he put off the assignment for two weeks.

**4.** Connector: **but**

<span class="answer">**I wanted to stick to my plan, but something unexpected came up.**</span>
