# The Truth about Ruthie — 秘密與失落

> 📖 **Reading Material**
> There were many things Ruthie could have been if she wanted to. Not only is she a good whistler, but she can sing and dance too. She had lots of job offers when she was young, but she never took them. She got married instead and moved away to a pretty house outside the city. Only thing I can't understand is why Ruthie is living on Mango Street if she doesn't have to, why is she sleeping on a couch in her mother's living room when she has a real house all her own...

---

## 🔎 Detective Task: The Power of "BUT"
在文本的後半段，作者大量使用了 **but**（或者是轉折的暗示）來表現 Ruthie 生活的矛盾。
請將 Ruthie「所擁有的/所說的」與她「實際做的」連起來。

| The Reality (真相) | | The Action (她的實際行為) |
|:---|:---:|:---|
| 1. She has a real house all her own. | **BUT** | a. she stays on Mango Street. |
| 2. She promises to see the dentist next week. | **BUT** | b. she is sleeping on her mother's couch. |
| 3. Her husband is going to take her home. | **BUT** | c. when next week comes, she doesn't go. |
| 4. She had lots of job offers. | **BUT** | d. she never took them. |

## 🔬 Deep Challenge: The Mystery of Ruthie (Indirect Questions)
因為這些各種矛盾與 "but"，Esperanza（和我們）對 Ruthie 充滿了疑問。
我們可以把心裡的問號（Why...? What...? Will...?）放進句子裡，這就是**間接問句**。

*鷹架 (Heads-up)：間接問句不用倒裝，保持原本的主詞＋動詞順序！*

1. **Why is Ruthie living on Mango Street?** (原問句：Ruthie 為什麼住在芒果街？)
   👉 The only thing I can't understand is **why Ruthie is living on Mango Street**.
   *(請注意，is 跑到 Ruthie 後面了！)*

2. **Why is she sleeping on a couch?** (原問句：她為什麼要睡沙發？)
   👉 We are wondering __________________________________________________ .

3. **Will her husband take her home?** (原問句：她老公會來接她回家嗎？)
   *(沒有疑問詞時，用 if 來連接，意思是「是否」)*
   👉 I don't know **if** _________________________________________________ .

4. **What happened to her?** (原問句：她到底發生了什麼事？)
   👉 No one knows ____________________________________________________ .
