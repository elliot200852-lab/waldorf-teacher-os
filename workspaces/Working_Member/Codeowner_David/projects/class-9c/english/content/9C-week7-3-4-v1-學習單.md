## Part 1: Group Reading / 分組閱讀

Your group has been assigned a paragraph. Read it together, then answer the question.

**Your group:** ☐ Group 1 &nbsp; ☐ Group 2 &nbsp; ☐ Group 3 &nbsp; ☐ Group 4

### Group Questions

| Group | Paragraph | Question |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Getting the job (lying about her age) | What does Esperanza want? What is she willing to do to get it? |
| 2 | The work (white gloves, matching negatives) | What does Esperanza NOT know? How does she figure things out? |
| 3 | Lunchtime (eating in the washroom) | Why does Esperanza eat in the washroom? What does this tell us about her? |
