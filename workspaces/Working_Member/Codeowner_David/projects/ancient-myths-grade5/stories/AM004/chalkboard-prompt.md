# AM004 黑板畫 Prompt：種姓制度與聖牛

## 中文 Prompt

**圖像主題選項**：本課有兩個可選的黑板畫主題，教師可擇一使用：

**主題 A：普魯沙——宇宙原人被分解的瞬間**
畫面中央是一個巨大的宇宙人形，充滿整個畫面，如星雲般向外延伸。他的身體由光與火組成，正在從整體緩緩分化為四個發光區塊——口（白色光芒，婆羅門）、雙臂（玫瑰紅光芒，剎帝利）、大腿（琥珀黃光芒，吠舍）、雙足（深色大地光芒，首陀羅）。背景是深邃的星空，祭火的火焰在四周燃起。

**主題 B：神牛卡瑪達努——婆私吒森林中的神聖顯現**
畫面中央是一頭雄偉的白色母牛，靜立在森林中的祭火旁。牛的身體內隱約可見眾神的輪廓。左側是樸素靜坐的婆私吒，右側是森林邊緣被擊退的戰士軍隊。整體氣氛安靜而神聖，光從牛的身體向外散射。

**教學建議**：主題 A 更適合在課程開始時介紹宇宙秩序的起源；主題 B 適合在講完神牛故事後，作為該段落的視覺停頓。

## English Prompt

### Option A — Purusha, the Cosmic Person at the Moment of Sacred Dissolution

A large-format chalkboard art illustration in the style of traditional Waldorf classroom chalk drawings.

**Composition**: A vast, luminous cosmic human figure (Purusha) fills the entire vertical canvas, arms gently outstretched like a tree or a star. His body is not solid flesh but made of swirling light and sacred fire, slowly dissolving outward into four distinct glowing regions. His face is serene, eyes closed, expression of peaceful sacrifice.

**Color zones representing the four varnas**:
- Head and mouth area: soft white and pale gold light (Brahmin — knowledge, prayer)
- Shoulders and arms: warm rose and deep red tones (Kshatriya — strength, protection)
- Hips and thighs: amber and ochre yellow tones (Vaishya — earth, harvest, trade)
- Feet and lower body: dark earth tones with warm undertone, not cold (Shudra — foundation, service)

**Background**: Deep indigo night sky filled with stars and sacred fire (yajna) flames rising gently at the lower edges of the image. Soft smoke curls suggest chanting priests just beyond the frame.

**Art style**: Chalkboard art style with soft chalk lines on a very dark blue-black background. Lines are gentle, slightly luminous, never harsh. The overall mood is cosmic, sacred, and warm — like a teacher drawing slowly by firelight.

**Do not include**: text, labels, sharp digital edges, photorealistic rendering, or modern stylistic elements.

For children's Waldorf classroom, Grade 5.

### Option B — Kamadhenu, the Sacred Cow Appearing in the Forest

A large-format chalkboard art illustration in the style of traditional Waldorf classroom chalk drawings.

**Composition**: Center stage is a magnificent white cow (Kamadhenu) standing calmly beside a sacred fire (yajna). She is large, gentle, luminous — almost glowing from within. Inside or around her body, faint outlines of divine beings can be sensed (not clearly drawn, just suggested by light). To her left, a simple ascetic (Vasishtha) sits in quiet meditation, robes plain, expression peaceful. To the right, at the forest edge, the outlines of a retreating army fade into darkness.

**Color and light**: The cow is white with warm amber and rose highlights catching the firelight. The sacred fire burns in warm gold and orange tones. The forest background is deep green-black. The army on the right dissolves into cooler shadows.

**Symbolic details**: The cow's four hooves are shown clearly, each touching the earth with presence. Her eyes are large, dark, and compassionate. Her tail gently curves. Flowers or sacred grass (kusha) around the base of the fire.

**Art style**: Chalkboard art style with soft chalk lines on a very dark blue-black background. Warm amber and rose tones illuminate the central scene. Lines are gentle and slightly luminous, never harsh. The mood is sacred, quiet, and powerful — spiritual strength made visible.

**Do not include**: text, labels, sharp digital edges, photorealistic rendering, or modern stylistic elements.

For children's Waldorf classroom, Grade 5.
