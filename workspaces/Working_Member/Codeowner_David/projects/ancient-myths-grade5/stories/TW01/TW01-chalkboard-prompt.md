# TW01 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述（教師參考）

畫面以暖橘色與深赭色為底，呈現高山間壯闊的場景。天空中有兩個太陽，左側那顆完整而炙熱，右側那顆正在被箭矢射中、光芒四散破碎。中景是兩個人物的剪影：一位高大的父親與一位少年，扛著弓箭，正翻越起伏的山脊線。山脊兩側可見幾棵纖細的果樹幼苗，有的剛從土中抽出嫩芽，有的已掛著小小的果實，沿著山路排成一排，延伸至遠方。構圖由下往上、由左往右帶動視線，象徵漫長的旅途與向著光走去的意志。整體筆觸為粉筆質感，線條粗獷而溫暖，不求細緻，求氣勢與情感的傳達。

## Gemini Image Prompt（English）

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing with warm chalk texture on a dark ochre-orange background. The scene depicts a vast mountain landscape under a sky with two suns: one blazing full and fierce on the left, the other on the right being struck by an arrow, its light shattering and scattering like broken glass. In the middle ground, two silhouetted figures — a tall father and a young son — stand on a mountain ridge, both carrying bows and quivers, facing the direction of the suns. Along the winding mountain path behind them, a row of small fruit tree saplings grows at intervals: some just sprouting from the earth, some bearing tiny round fruits (citrus or pomelo), each tree smaller as it recedes into the distance, suggesting a trail marked by seeds planted with care. The composition moves diagonally upward from lower-left to upper-right, evoking a long, determined journey. The style is expressive chalk-on-blackboard: rough, luminous strokes, warm amber and burnt sienna tones, with accents of pale gold and dusty white for the suns and sky. No hard outlines — shapes emerge from light and shadow. The mood is solemn and tender, like a memory held gently by a people who have walked a very long road.

## 備用 Prompt（若主 prompt 被拒）

A Waldorf chalkboard illustration in warm chalk style. Two suns hang in a golden sky above a mountain range. A father and son walk together along a mountain path, carrying bows and arrows. Along the path, small citrus saplings grow at regular intervals, stretching into the distance. The overall mood is peaceful and purposeful. Chalk-on-blackboard texture, warm amber tones, expressive soft lines, no photorealism.
