# AM008 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文說明

畫面為寬幅橫構圖，呈現光明與黑暗的太初對峙。左側是純淨的金色光焰區域，光從中心向外放射，層次由亮金、暖橘到柔和的黃白色，彷彿有一股巨大的智慧力量在光中凝聚。右側是深紫與墨黑交融的黑暗區域，暗色中隱約可見扭曲的形體與陰影翻湧，有一種躁動不安的力量正在向光明的方向推擠。

兩者之間是一條窄窄的虛空地帶，既是分隔也是即將碰撞的邊界。在光明一側的下方，隱約浮現六個創造物的輪廓——石質穹頂、水波、大地、植物枝葉、一頭公牛的身影、一個人的剪影——排列如種子般安靜等待。整體氛圍莊嚴肅穆，是暴風雨來臨前的寧靜，善與惡即將正面交鋒的一刻。

## English Prompt（供 Gemini 生成）

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, chalk on dark blackboard, soft wet-on-wet watercolor quality blended into chalk texture. Wide horizontal composition.

On the left side, a radiant golden luminous presence — pure light emanating from the center outward in layered waves of bright gold, warm amber, and soft white-yellow. The light feels alive, intelligent, expansive. No human figure, just the essence of radiant wisdom and truth.

On the right side, a churning mass of deep purple-black darkness — swirling, restless shadows with hints of violet and cold indigo. The dark forms twist and press toward the light, conveying aggression and chaos. No distinct figure, just the presence of a destructive force.

Between the two, a narrow vertical band of empty void — the threshold where light and dark have not yet touched, a line of tension and anticipation.

In the lower portion beneath the golden light, six faint silhouettes emerge like seeds in a row: a dome-like sky vault, rippling water, flat earth, a small tree with spreading branches, the outline of a great bull, and a simple standing human figure. These are barely visible, delicate, as if just beginning to exist.

Color palette: warm gold and amber (left, light) → empty black void (center gap) → deep violet, indigo, and black (right, darkness). The six creation silhouettes glow faintly in pale gold against the dark board.

Overall mood: solemn, primordial, the charged stillness before a cosmic collision. The moment just before darkness strikes.

No text. No visible faces. Emphasize the contrast between radiant order and churning chaos, with the fragile beauty of new creation caught between them.
