# AM014 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述

畫面以水平構圖分為三層，呼應故事追尋永生的三個層次——塵世、水面、遠方之光。

**上層（畫面上方 1/3）：遠方之光**
深藍黑色的夜空上，有微弱卻堅定的晨星（啟明星或昴宿星）以淡金色細線勾出。最右上角有一縷曙光從地平線透出，象徵烏魯克城牆的方向——那是最終的回歸地。

**中層（畫面中央 1/3）：死亡之水**
畫面中央橫貫一片深黑色的海——「死亡之水」。水面平靜得像一面黑鏡，看不見底，但隱約有星光倒映其中。水面上有一艘小舟漂浮，船上兩個身影——前方的船夫烏爾沙那比（輪廓模糊、戴著帽兜），後方的吉爾伽美什手中握著一根長篙（120 根長篙中的一根），身披獅皮、面朝左方（朝向畫面中央偏左）。長篙插入水中，不碰手。舟的右側水面上，一條蛇的輪廓正從水中浮出（預示永生草被奪走的一幕），蛇身用淺綠與金黃的蠟色層疊，身體有舊皮脫落的痕跡。

**下層（畫面下方 1/3）：塵世的草與岸**
畫面底部是泉水邊的岸地——深苔綠、土褐色層疊，一叢叢草木有機生長。草叢中有一株特別明亮的草——綠色偏金黃、微微發光，那就是「返老還童的永生草」。草旁邊的水面倒映著一點光。整幅畫中唯一較溫暖的光點，就在這株草上。

**整體氛圍**
畫面左側整體偏冷——深藍、黑、苔綠，象徵死亡與追尋的寒冷。畫面右側略帶暖金——烏魯克城牆的方向，象徵歸家、溫度、與人世的擁有。色彩以色塊層疊堆積，不畫硬邊，光從內部透出。這是一幅關於「失去」與「看見自己擁有的」的畫。

風格為華德福蠟塊畫：無硬邊，色彩以面塊堆疊，光從內部透出。

## English Prompt (for Gemini)

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, wax block crayon on dark blue-black background. No hard outlines; all forms emerge from layered color fields, soft edges, inner light glowing through pigment.

The composition is divided horizontally into three layers, reflecting the story's quest for immortality — earthly shore below, water of death in the middle, distant light above.

UPPER THIRD — The Distant Light: A deep midnight blue night sky holds a faint but steadfast morning star (Venus or the Pleiades) traced in pale gold fine lines. In the upper-right corner, a faint dawn glow breaks from the horizon, suggesting the direction of Uruk's city walls — the final place of return.

MIDDLE THIRD — The Waters of Death: A vast horizontal stretch of black water crosses the center — the "waters of death" (mê mūti) — still as a dark mirror, with faint starlight reflected on its surface. A small boat drifts on the water, carrying two figures: in front, the boatman Urshanabi (hooded, silhouetted), and behind him Gilgamesh, wrapped in a tawny lion skin, holding a long wooden punting pole (one of 120) facing left toward center-left. The pole plunges into the water — his hand never touches the surface. On the right side of the boat, the silhouette of a serpent rises from the water, its body in pale green and gold wax strokes showing shed skin curling away (foreshadowing the moment when the serpent steals the plant of youth).

LOWER THIRD — The Earthly Shore: The foreground is the shore of a freshwater spring — layered moss green, deep forest green, earthy brown. Organic vegetation grows in clusters. Among the grasses, one luminous plant stands out — green touched with warm gold, faintly glowing — this is the plant of rejuvenation (sammu nikti). A single point of reflected light rests on the water beside it. This single warm glow is the one warm note in the whole composition.

OVERALL ATMOSPHERE — The left side of the image is cooler: deep blue, black, moss green, representing death and the coldness of the quest. The right side carries a slight warm gold — the direction of Uruk's city walls — representing home, warmth, and what was always already possessed. Colors are built through layered color fields, no hard outlines, inner light glowing through pigment.

Mood: melancholy, ancient, mythic — the moment before loss becomes acceptance. The feeling of a hero who has traveled to the edge of the world and is about to understand that what he sought was behind him all along.

Waldorf chalkboard drawing, wax block crayon, layered color fields, no hard outlines, dark background, mythic atmosphere, ancient Mesopotamia, tripartite horizontal composition, serpent and plant symbolism.
