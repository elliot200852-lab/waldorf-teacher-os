# AM005 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述

俱盧之野的清晨，五個剪影並肩站立在廣闊平原的邊緣，遠方地平線上隱約可見旌旗與軍陣的輪廓。天光微亮，大地靜默，這是決戰前最後一刻的凝止。五個人的身形各有特徵——高大的、修長的、沉靜的——五種性格在黑暗中匯聚成一道脊梁。

## English Prompt

A chalkboard drawing style illustration rendered in white chalk lines on a dark green and black background. Five silhouettes of warriors standing side by side on a vast open plain, facing the horizon. The figures are distinct in posture — one broad-shouldered and powerful, one tall and still holding a bow, one carrying a sword, two slightly smaller but steady — representing five brothers united before a great battle. In the far distance, faint outlines of banners, chariots, and armies gather along the horizon line of the Kurukshetra battlefield. The sky above transitions from deep darkness to a pale gray-white dawn glow. No text, no titles, no labels. Indian epic landscape atmosphere. Waldorf school art style with soft, gestural chalk strokes. Emphasis on the emotional weight of five humans standing at the threshold of an enormous decision. The composition is wide and low, evoking the immensity of the plain and the smallness yet dignity of the five figures.

## 教學說明

這張黑板畫在說故事結束後留在黑板上，讓孩子在安靜中望著五個剪影完成蠟塊畫作業——五兄弟站在戰場邊緣的意象，呼應孩子內心「在最大的考驗面前，我是誰」的發展問題。教師不加說明，讓畫面自己說話。
