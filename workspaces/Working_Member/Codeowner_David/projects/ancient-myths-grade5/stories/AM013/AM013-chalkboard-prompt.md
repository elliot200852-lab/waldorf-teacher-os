# AM013 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文描述

畫面以垂直中軸分為兩個世界，呼應故事的核心對位——文明與荒野。

**左半部：城牆內的王**
烏魯克的城牆以厚重的磚砌弧線從左下角升起，牆頭有燈火或星光點綴。吉爾伽美什立於城牆內側，身形高大、頂天立地，身著深藍或土黃的王袍，腰帶有金色裝飾線條。他的姿態直立而驕傲，面朝右側（朝向荒野）。背景是深藍夜空，隱約可見兩河流域的星象（大熊座或獵戶座的輪廓），以淡金色細線輕輕勾出。城牆的磚紋用暖橙色層疊，有厚重的人造秩序感。

**右半部：荒野中的野人**
畫面右側是未馴化的大地——低矮的草木輪廓，以深綠、苔綠、土褐色層疊，植被茂密而有機。恩奇都蹲踞或站立於荒野中央，身形粗獷，全身布滿長毛的線條（用白色或米黃色短筆觸表現），與周圍的草木幾乎融為一體。旁側可有一頭羚羊或獅子的輪廓，與他親密靠近。恩奇都的眼神朝向左方（朝向城市）。

**中軸過渡**
兩個世界之間沒有清晰邊界，而是以色彩自然漸層過渡——磚橙/金黃漸入深綠/土褐，代表文明與荒野之間那道模糊而命運性的邊界。

整幅畫背景為深藍黑色（黑板底色），星空籠罩兩個世界，暗示兩者都在更大的宇宙秩序之下。風格為華德福蠟塊畫：無硬邊，色彩以面塊堆疊，光從內部透出。

## English Prompt (for Gemini)

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, wax block crayon on dark blue-black background. No hard outlines; all forms emerge from layered color fields, soft edges, inner light glowing through pigment.

The composition is divided vertically into two worlds by a gradual color transition rather than a hard line.

LEFT HALF — The King inside the City Walls: A massive ancient city wall rises from the lower left, constructed of warm orange and amber brick, with slight arched forms suggesting Mesopotamian architecture. Inside the wall stands Gilgamesh, a towering king-figure, upright and proud, dressed in deep blue robes with golden belt details. His face turns rightward toward the wilderness. Above and behind him, a deep midnight blue sky holds a faint constellation pattern (Orion or Ursa Major) traced in pale gold fine lines, suggesting a civilization that reads the stars.

RIGHT HALF — The Wild Man in the Wilderness: Dense, organic vegetation fills the right side — layered in forest green, moss green, and earth brown. Enkidu crouches or stands among the wild growth, his body massive and primal, covered in short white or cream strokes suggesting body hair that blends with the surrounding plants. A gazelle or lion rests nearby in quiet companionship. Enkidu's gaze turns leftward toward the city.

CENTER — the boundary between the two worlds dissolves in a gradient: warm amber-orange fading into deep moss green, representing the threshold where civilization and nature meet.

Overall atmosphere: ancient, mythic, warm yet mysterious. The dark sky arcs over both worlds. Mood: the moment before two fates collide.

Waldorf chalkboard drawing, wax block crayon, layered color fields, no hard outlines, dark background, mythic atmosphere, ancient Mesopotamia, split composition.
