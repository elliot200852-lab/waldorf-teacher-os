# AM011 黑板畫 Prompt：波斯的智者

## 中文 Prompt

以黑板為底色的華德福式粉筆畫風格。畫面主體是一片深邃的夜空，從頂部延伸至畫面大半：天空以深藍與深紫色層疊渲染，星星以白色與淡黃色點綴其中，大小不一，帶著柔和的光暈。畫面右上方有一顆特別明亮的星，光芒向四周輻射，像一盞懸在天空中的燈。

前景是一片起伏平緩的沙漠或高原，地平線低矮，以赭色與棕色描繪。三個身穿長袍的人影沿著地平線行走，姿態從容而堅定，朝向那顆明亮的星的方向。人影以柔和的暖色粉筆勾勒輪廓，不需細節，只需剪影的感覺。遠方隱約可見低矮的山脈輪廓。

整體氛圍：靜謐、神聖、充滿期待。光線的焦點在天空的星，不在地面。顏色對比柔和而非銳利，符合華德福黑板畫的夢幻、發光質感。粉筆線條可見，不求精細寫實，求情感共鳴與畫面溫度。

## English Prompt

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, dark background like a blackboard, drawn with colored chalk, luminous ethereal quality. The scene depicts a vast, deep night sky rendered in layered deep blues and purples, occupying most of the composition. Stars of varying sizes glow softly in white and pale yellow, each with a gentle halo. In the upper right, one star shines with exceptional brightness, its rays radiating outward like a lantern suspended in the heavens.

In the foreground, a gently undulating desert or high plain stretches across the lower portion of the image, rendered in warm ochre and brown tones, with a low horizon line. Three robed figures walk steadily across this plain, moving toward the direction of the bright star. Their forms are silhouetted in warm chalk tones — no fine detail, only the quiet dignity of their posture and movement. Faint outlines of distant mountains are visible on the horizon.

The mood is silent, sacred, and full of anticipation. The light source is the star above, not the ground. Color contrasts are soft and gentle rather than harsh. Visible chalk strokes, dreamy and glowing rather than photorealistic — evoking warmth, wonder, and the sense that wisdom is traveling across the night toward its destination.
