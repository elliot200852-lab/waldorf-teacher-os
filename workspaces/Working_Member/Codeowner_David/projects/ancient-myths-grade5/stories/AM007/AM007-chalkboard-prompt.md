# AM007 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文說明

畫面分為左右兩個世界：左側是遠處隱約可見的金色宮殿輪廓，暖光籠罩，象徵悉達多曾身處的夢境；右側林蔭漸深，簡樸而靜謐。畫面中央佇立一棵巨大的菩提樹，樹幹粗壯，枝葉向上張開如雙手托天。樹下，一個人影靜靜盤坐，身形簡單，姿態安定，如同與大地融為一體。天際邊，一顆明亮的晨星正在升起，那是破曉的時刻，也是覺醒的時刻。整體色調從左方的金橘，逐漸轉為中央的青綠，再到右方的深藍晨光。

## English Prompt（供 Gemini 生成）

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, chalk on dark blackboard, soft wet-on-wet watercolor quality blended into chalk texture. Wide horizontal composition.

On the far left, a faint golden palace silhouette glows warmly — domed towers, soft amber and ochre hues suggesting a protected, dreamlike world. The light dissolves as it moves toward the center.

At the center of the image stands a large sacred fig tree (Bodhi tree, Ficus religiosa), with a broad, ancient trunk and wide spreading branches that reach upward like open hands. Beneath the tree, a small seated figure — cross-legged, perfectly still, silhouetted in simple form — meditates on the earth. The figure radiates quiet, rooted presence.

Above the treetops on the right horizon, a single bright morning star glows against a deep blue-violet dawn sky. The light is just beginning to break — the moment before full sunrise, the threshold of awakening.

Color palette transitions left to right: warm gold and amber (palace) → soft sage green and teal (forest and tree) → deep indigo and violet with a single luminous star (dawn sky). The overall mood is still, transformative, and quietly radiant — the silence just before everything changes.

No text. No human faces visible. Emphasize depth, stillness, and the threshold between dreaming and waking.
