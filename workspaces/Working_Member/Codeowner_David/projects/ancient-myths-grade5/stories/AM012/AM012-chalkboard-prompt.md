# 黑板畫 Prompt：AM012 馬爾杜克的創世

## English Prompt

請你生成一張圖片

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style on a pure black background, drawn entirely with colored chalk and soft pastels, with visible chalk texture and luminous, glowing quality throughout. The scene depicts the cosmic battle between the hero god Marduk and the chaos serpent-dragon Tiamat.

At the center of the composition, a tall radiant figure — Marduk — stands in dynamic posture, one arm raised high wielding a mace that trails arcs of golden-white light, the other arm drawing a great bow. His form is rendered in warm tones: deep gold, amber, and flashes of fiery orange-red, with four eyes faintly glowing on his face, suggesting supernatural vision. Swirling winds spiral outward from his body in layered strokes of pale blue, silver, and white chalk — the four cosmic winds he commands — creating a vortex of movement around him.

Before him, Tiamat is vast and serpentine, her coiling body filling the lower and side portions of the composition. She is rendered in deep blue-green, teal, and dark violet chalk, with scales suggested by overlapping curved strokes. Her enormous jaws are forced wide open by the winds rushing into her mouth, depicted as streaks of pale chalk spiraling inward. Her body arches and writhes, tail curling off the edges of the frame. Her eyes glow with a dim, ancient turquoise light.

Above the battle, faint stars and nascent constellations begin to appear in the upper darkness — hints of the cosmic order about to be born from this struggle. A great net of fine golden lines stretches across parts of the composition, tangling around Tiamat's form.

The overall mood is epic, primordial, and awe-inspiring. Colors glow from within the black surface as if lit by inner fire. Chalk strokes are bold and expressive, not photorealistic — capturing raw cosmic energy, the birth of order from chaos, the moment when the world is about to be made. Warm golds and cool blue-greens create dramatic contrast. The style evokes the reverent, dream-like quality of Waldorf education chalkboard illustrations.

## 中文翻譯

華德福黑板畫風格，純黑背景，全程以彩色粉筆與柔性粉彩繪製，帶有明顯的粉筆紋理與由內而外的發光質感。畫面描繪英雄之神馬爾杜克與混沌龍蛇提亞瑪特的宇宙之戰。

畫面中央，一位高大而光芒四射的人物——馬爾杜克——以動態姿勢站立，一臂高舉釘頭錘，錘身拖曳出金白色的弧光，另一臂正拉開巨弓。他的形體以暖色調呈現：深金、琥珀色，間雜火焰般的橙紅色閃光，臉上隱約發光的四隻眼睛暗示著超自然的視覺。旋風從他身體向外螺旋展開，以淡藍、銀白、白色粉筆層層描繪——那是他號令的四方宇宙之風——在他周圍形成動態漩渦。

在他面前，提亞瑪特龐大而蛇形，盤曲的身軀佔據畫面下方與兩側。她以深藍綠、青綠與暗紫色粉筆描繪，鱗片以層疊的弧形筆觸暗示。她巨大的口被灌入的狂風撐開，無法閉合，以淡色粉筆螺旋向內的條紋表現風的灌入。她的身軀扭曲翻捲，尾巴蜿蜒至畫面邊緣之外。她的雙眼散發幽暗而古老的藍綠色光芒。

戰場上方，隱約的星辰與初生的星座開始在高處的黑暗中浮現——暗示即將從這場鬥爭中誕生的宇宙秩序。一張由細金色線條構成的巨網橫貫畫面局部，纏繞在提亞瑪特的身軀上。

整體氛圍：史詩般的、原初的、令人敬畏的。色彩從黑色表面內部發光，彷彿被內在之火點燃。粉筆筆觸大膽而富表現力，不求寫實——捕捉原始的宇宙能量、秩序從混沌中誕生的瞬間、世界即將被創造的那一刻。暖金色與冷藍綠色形成戲劇性對比。風格喚起華德福教育黑板畫那種虔敬的、夢幻般的質感。
