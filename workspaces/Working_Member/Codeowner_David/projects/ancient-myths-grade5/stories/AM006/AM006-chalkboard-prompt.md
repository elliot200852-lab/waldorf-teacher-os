# AM006 黑板畫 Prompt：羅摩與悉多

## 中文說明

深邃的熱帶森林中，羅摩手持長弓，身形挺立如樹，目光遠望；哈奴曼半跪在他面前，身形龐大卻充滿虔敬，雙手合十。茂密的叢林從四面包圍，林隙透入點點光芒。整體色調深沉而神聖，氛圍靜謐、充滿力量。

## English Prompt

A chalkboard drawing style illustration rendered in white chalk lines on a dark green and black background. In the center, Rama, an ancient Indian prince, stands tall and composed in a dense tropical forest, holding a large bow in one hand, his posture noble and still, wearing a simple dhoti and a crown. Before him, Hanuman the monkey god kneels on one knee, his large powerful body conveying both immense strength and deep devotion — his hands are pressed together in reverence, his face turned upward toward Rama. The surrounding jungle is dense and ancient: enormous tree roots, hanging vines, broad tropical leaves layered in darkness. Shafts of soft light break through the forest canopy above, falling on the two figures. The mood is sacred, quiet, and full of inner power — a moment of loyalty and divine purpose. The chalk lines are gestural and expressive, with soft hatching to suggest shadow and depth. No text, no writing, no words, no labels anywhere in the image. Waldorf school chalkboard art style, atmospheric and spiritually resonant.

## 教學說明

這張黑板畫在說書完成後留在黑板上，讓孩子在安靜中開始蠟塊畫作業。羅摩的靜定與哈奴曼的虔敬構成一組張力——力量不來自憤怒，而來自對使命的全然交付。教師不加解說，讓畫面本身說話，讓孩子的手跟著感受去移動。
