# AM002 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：AncientMyths-AM002-chalkboard.png

請你生成一張圖片：一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，主題是「濕婆那塔羅加——宇宙舞蹈」。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing depicting Shiva as Nataraja, the Lord of the Cosmic Dance. The background is a deep dark green chalkboard surface with visible chalk dust texture. At the center of the composition, Shiva stands in the Nataraja pose — a graceful four-armed figure in mid-dance, enclosed within a luminous ring of fire (the prabhamandala). His entire form is rendered in soft chalk lines — warm golden-amber for the body, with flowing garments and hair that stream outward as if caught in an eternal wind. The fire ring is composed of individual flame-shapes in pale orange and rose, glowing against the dark green background. His upper right hand holds a small drum (damaru), rendered in warm gold; his upper left hand holds a curved flame, in deep orange-red. His lower right hand extends outward in the abhaya mudra (gesture of protection), open-palmed toward the viewer. His lower left hand points gracefully toward his raised left foot — suggesting liberation. His right foot stands upon a small reclining figure (the demon Apasmara, symbol of ignorance), rendered in softer, darker tones so as not to dominate. His left leg is raised and bent, the foot pointing gently upward in the flight of the dance. The overall composition is vertical, centered, with the fire ring dominating the middle third of the image and generous dark space above and below. The palette is restrained: deep indigo background, warm gold figure, rose-orange fire ring, soft cream lines. No text, no labels, no harsh outlines. The mood is dynamic stillness — eternal motion frozen in a single breath — a dance that has always been, and will always be.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格黑板畫，描繪濕婆那塔羅加（宇宙舞者）。背景為深墨綠色黑板面，帶有可見的粉筆粉塵質感。畫面正中央，濕婆以那塔羅加姿勢站立——一個優雅的四臂人形正在舞蹈中，被一圈發光的火焰環（光背）環繞。整個人形以柔和的粉筆線條繪成——身體為溫暖的金琥珀色，飄動的衣裝與頭髮向外展開，彷彿被永恆的風吹拂。火焰環由個別的火焰形狀組成，以淡橙色與玫瑰色在深綠背景上發光。右上手持小鼓（達摩魯）以暖金色呈現；左上手持彎曲火焰，以深橙紅色呈現。右下手向外展開作無畏印（保護姿勢），掌心朝向觀看者。左下手優雅地指向抬起的左腳，暗示解脫。右腳踏在一個較小的躺臥人形（阿帕斯瑪拉，無知惡魔的象徵）上，以較柔和的深色調呈現，不搶奪視覺焦點。左腿抬起彎曲，腳尖輕柔地向上指，在舞蹈的飛揚中。整體構圖垂直居中，火焰環佔據畫面中段三分之一，上下留有充裕的暗色空間。色調節制：深靛藍背景、暖金人形、玫瑰橙火焰環、柔和乳白線條。沒有文字、沒有標籤、沒有硬邊輪廓。氛圍是動態的靜謐——永恆的運動凝固在一次呼吸之中——一場始終存在、永遠延續的舞蹈。
