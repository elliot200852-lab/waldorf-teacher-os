# AM001 黑板畫 Prompt

下載檔名：AncientMyths-AM001-chalkboard.png

請你生成一張圖片：一幅華德福風格的黑板畫，主題是「梵天的創世——宇宙蛋」。

## English Prompt

A Waldorf-style chalkboard drawing depicting the Hindu creation myth of the golden cosmic egg (Hiranyagarbha). The background is a deep dark green chalkboard surface with visible chalk dust texture. At the center of the composition, a large luminous golden egg floats on vast, dark primordial waters. The egg glows from within, with hairline cracks forming along its surface, and warm golden-amber light spilling outward through those cracks into the surrounding darkness. The waters below are rendered in deep indigo and blue-black chalk strokes, still and infinite, with no shore or horizon — only the faintest reflection of the egg's golden light shimmering on the surface. Above the egg, the first suggestion of sky begins to emerge — soft arcs of pale gold and rose light fanning upward from the cracking shell, like a dawn that has never happened before. The entire image is rendered in soft chalk pastels — no hard edges, no outlines. Colors blend and glow as if lit from within. The palette is restrained: deep indigo, warm gold, amber, touches of rose and pale cream against the dark green board. No text, no labels, no human figures. The mood is primordial stillness on the threshold of creation — silence about to break open into light. Composition is vertically centered with generous dark space surrounding the egg, emphasizing its solitude in the void.

## 中文翻譯

華德福風格黑板畫，描繪印度教金色宇宙蛋（Hiranyagarbha）的創世神話。背景為深墨綠色黑板面，帶有可見的粉筆粉塵質感。畫面正中央，一顆巨大的、發光的金色蛋漂浮在廣闊而幽暗的太初之水上。蛋由內而外散發光芒，表面形成細細的裂紋，溫暖的金琥珀色光芒從裂縫中向外溢出，照進四周的黑暗。下方的水以深靛藍與藍黑色粉筆筆觸繪成，靜止而無盡，沒有岸也沒有地平線——只有蛋的金光在水面上微微閃動的倒影。蛋的上方，天空的最初樣貌開始浮現——淡金與玫瑰色的柔和弧光從裂開的蛋殼向上展開，如同一場從未發生過的黎明。整幅圖以柔軟的粉筆色彩繪成，沒有硬邊，沒有輪廓線。色彩融合並發光，彷彿從內部被照亮。色調節制：深靛藍、暖金、琥珀，加上玫瑰與淡乳白色的點綴，映襯在墨綠色黑板上。沒有文字、沒有標籤、沒有人物。氛圍是創世臨界點上的太初靜謐——沉默即將裂開為光。構圖以垂直居中，蛋周圍留有充裕的暗色空間，強調它在虛空中的孤獨。
