# AM010 黑板畫 Prompt：瑣羅亞斯德

## 中文描述

畫面為寬幅橫構圖，呈現「荒野中的火焰與先知」的核心意象。畫面中央偏右，一個孤獨的人影站在蒼茫的曠野中，面朝遠方。他的輪廓簡潔，不見面容，只有披著長袍的側身剪影。他不是在走，而是靜靜地站著，像是正在聆聽什麼。

他的正前方，一團火焰從大地裂縫中升起——不是篝火，不是火把，而是一道自地底湧出的光焰，火舌向上伸展，帶著金、琥珀與白色的層次。火焰的光芒向四周擴散，照亮先知的袍角與腳下的碎石。

背景是東伊朗高原的荒涼地貌：起伏的丘陵、乾燥的大地、稀疏的岩石。天空從地平線的暗紫色漸層上升為深藍，暗示黎明前最黑暗的時刻即將過去。火焰是畫面中唯一的強光源，將先知與荒野分成明暗兩半。整體氛圍是孤寂中的莊嚴——一個人獨自面對來自天地之間的聲音。

## English Prompt for Gemini

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, chalk pastels on a dark blackboard surface, soft wet-on-wet watercolor quality blended into chalk texture. Wide horizontal composition.

At the center-right of the composition, a solitary figure stands in a vast wilderness — a prophet in a long flowing robe, seen in profile silhouette. No facial features visible. His posture is still and listening, not walking — he stands as if rooted to the earth, receiving something from beyond. Rendered in deep sienna, umber, and muted ochre tones.

Directly before him, a luminous flame rises from a fissure in the rocky ground. This is not a campfire or torch but a sacred eruption of light from the earth itself. The flame stretches upward with layered tongues of gold, amber, warm white, and pale yellow. It radiates outward in soft concentric halos, illuminating the prophet's robe hem and the scattered stones at his feet. The flame is the sole intense light source in the composition.

The surrounding landscape is the stark terrain of the eastern Iranian plateau: rolling barren hills, dry cracked earth, sparse angular rocks. No vegetation. The emptiness emphasizes the prophet's solitude and the enormity of the moment.

The sky transitions from deep violet and indigo at the top to a narrow band of dark purple along the horizon — the moment just before dawn, when darkness is deepest but about to break. A faint suggestion of distant mountain ridges lines the horizon in blue-grey.

The flame divides the composition into two halves of light and shadow: warm golden radiance fans out from the fire toward the prophet and the ground before him, while the wilderness behind and around recedes into cool darkness — deep blue, violet, and black. The boundary between light and dark is soft, not sharp, suggesting the light is slowly expanding.

Color palette: deep blackboard black and indigo (sky and distant landscape) → violet and blue-grey (mid-ground hills) → warm gold, amber, pale white (flame and its radiance) → sienna and umber (prophet silhouette) → dark brown and grey (rocky foreground).

Overall mood: sacred solitude, the stillness of revelation, a single human being standing before a voice that comes from the fire and the earth and the sky all at once. Not dramatic action — profound listening.

No text. No visible face. No words or letters in the image.
