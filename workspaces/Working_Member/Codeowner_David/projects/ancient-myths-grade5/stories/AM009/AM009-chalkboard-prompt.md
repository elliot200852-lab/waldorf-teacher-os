# AM009 黑板畫 Prompt

## 中文說明

畫面為寬幅橫構圖，呈現「農耕即是對抗黑暗」的核心意象。畫面右側是一位農夫的背影，他手持鋤頭，正走向前方的田野。他的姿態平凡而沉穩，不是英雄的姿態，而是每天清晨出門工作的姿態。

農夫身後的左下方，有一間簡樸的小屋，窗口透出溫暖的橘紅色爐火光芒，那是家中不滅的聖火。農夫前方的田野中，嫩綠的麥芽從深褐色的泥土裡冒出來，一排一排，像一支安靜的軍隊。

天空上方，朝霞從地平線展開，金色與琥珀色的光芒向上擴散，像阿胡拉·馬茲達的祝福從天頂傾瀉而下。光芒照到的地方，土地帶著暖色；光還沒有照到的地方，深紫與墨黑的暗影仍然盤踞，但正在退縮。

整體氛圍不是戰鬥的激烈，而是黎明的寧靜——一種篤定的、日復一日的力量正在推開黑暗。

## English Prompt（供 Gemini 生成）

Waldorf chalkboard drawing style, chalk pastels on a dark blackboard surface, soft wet-on-wet watercolor quality blended into chalk texture. Wide horizontal composition.

On the right side, the back silhouette of a farmer walking forward into a field, holding a hoe over one shoulder. His posture is calm, unheroic, steady — the quiet determination of someone who does this every morning. He is rendered in warm earth tones: ochre, sienna, soft brown.

Behind him on the lower left, a small simple dwelling with a warm orange-red glow emanating from its window — a hearth fire burning inside, the sacred flame that never goes out. The glow radiates gently outward in concentric soft halos.

Before the farmer, rows of tender green sprouts emerge from dark brown earth — young barley or wheat seedlings pushing up through cracked soil, drawn with delicate pale green and yellow-green chalk strokes. The sprouts form gentle rows receding toward the horizon.

The upper sky blooms with dawn light — layered golden, amber, and rose-colored radiance spreading upward from the horizon line, as if a great benevolent presence is pouring light down upon the earth. Where the golden light reaches, the ground takes on warm brown and green tones. Where the light has not yet reached, deep violet and black shadows still linger at the edges and corners of the composition, but they are visibly retreating, dissolving.

A single prominent flame motif floats subtly in the sky within the golden light — not a literal torch, but a luminous flame-shaped glow suggesting the divine fire that lives in all things: in the hearth, in the sun, in the human heart.

Color palette: warm gold, amber, rose (sky and light) → rich earth browns, ochre (farmer and ground) → pale green (sprouts) → deep violet and black (retreating shadows at edges). The hearth glow is orange-red, intimate and warm.

Overall mood: dawn stillness, quiet sacred labor, the unhurried confidence that darkness retreats one step at a time. Not a battle scene — a farming scene that IS a battle.

No text. No visible face (farmer seen from behind). No words or letters in the image.
