But Zeus, the King of the Gods, had no wish to see the human race
become more powerful. One day, however, Prometheus decided he
would help the human race whether Zeus liked it or not. And the Titan
thought that the red flower of the gods was what would help man most.
What was the red flower? It was fire. Today we take fire for granted. We
use it to cook our food, to light our houses at night and to keep us
warm. But in those days people ate their food raw and huddled together
in the cold and dark for warmth
and protection from the wild beasts.
Prometheus also knew that animals are afraid of fire. If, for instance, you
were in the wilds at night and you made a blazing fire, no wild animals
would come near you.
