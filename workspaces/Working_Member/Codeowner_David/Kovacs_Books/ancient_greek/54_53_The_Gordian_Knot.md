Egyptian civilizations. But now Greece had brought a new wisdom into

of Asia and the new wisdom of Greece to be united. However, the only
way he could see of bringing the knowledge of Asia and Europe together
was by making all these countries into one great empire. He did not
want to go to war to destroy towns and cities or to make the Persians
slaves. Instead he hoped to create something new; an empire where the
knowledge of Asia and Europe could become one.
